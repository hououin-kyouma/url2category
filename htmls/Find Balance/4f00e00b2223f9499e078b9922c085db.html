Could it be that the Danes are always being voted the happiest people in the world because of their healthy balance between work and private life?Denmark prides itself on having a healthy work-life balance. The Danish welfare model, with its flexible working conditions and social support networks, including parental leave and childcare facilities, not only puts Denmark at the top of the international equality league table, but also contributes to a generally high standard of living.
