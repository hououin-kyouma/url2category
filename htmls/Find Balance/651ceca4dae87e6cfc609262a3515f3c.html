The yoga class started as part of a wellness program to combat obesity (KTRK)

KTRK
By Erik Barajas
Tuesday, September 23, 2014
HOUSTON (KTRK) --
Fifth graders are Houston ISD's Oakforest Elementary School are learning more than reading and math. These days they're practicing yoga, as part of a wellness program intended to help combat obesity.

HISD received a $400,000 grant from the Sonima Foundation to help implement the program. Yoga will be the cornerstone. Administrators hope it will benefit students who don't participate in traditional sports.
