Published on Apr 3, 2016

U.S. Olympic Luge athlete Erin Hamlin discusses the importance of healthy living in this month’s video lesson. Erin highlights a couple keys to living healthy; Proper nutrition, getting enough sleep, staying active, and always following your heart when faced with tough choices.

Erin finishes her lesson by challenging her students to pick an activity like sit-ups, push-ups, or lunges and set a goal for the month that will help them improve little by little every day.
