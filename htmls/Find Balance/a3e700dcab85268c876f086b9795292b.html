How to Reduce Stress During Cattle Weaning

Management : Weaned Calf

Purina Animal Nutrition

When cattle are stressed, they often quit eating. The sooner you can get your calves to eat, the less likely they are to get sick.

For the first five to seven days you want to encourage calves to eat at least 2.5 to 3 percent of their bodyweight, which can be achieved using available forages with a highly palatable supplemental feed such as Stress Careâ„¢ 5 or by using complete feeds like AccurationÂ® Starter or PreconÂ® Complete.
