Grades K-5

The Together Counts™ Curriculum empowers teachers with the tools to help their students lead healthy and active lifestyles. The lessons focus on the fundamentals of overall wellness, teaching kids to make healthy decisions that foster healthy habits throughout their lives.

Learn Together

Students learn about physical, social, and emotional wellness. They discover how decision making and goal setting can help maintain balance in their lives.

Eat Together

Students are introduced to the key elements of healthy eating patterns. They learn about the different food groups and the importance of getting a balance from them every day.

Play Together

Students learn about the importance of physical activity to their overall wellness. They develop action plans and learn how to overcome common obstacles to being active daily.
