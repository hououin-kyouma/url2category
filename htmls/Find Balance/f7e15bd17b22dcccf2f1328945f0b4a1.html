Planning for Work/Life Balance

Worried about adding a family to your already (over)full life?

By Sharon Meers and Joanna Strober, Co-authors of Getting to 50/50: How Working Parents Can Have It All
Download Discussion Guide (PDF)

Watch this short video to learn effective strategies for achieving greater work/life balance. You’ll learn the value of negotiation, how to ask for more specific goals and objectives at work, and the importance of letting go of perfection. Sharon Meers and Joanna Strober guide the discussion and address real-life examples from Stanford community members. View this second video in the three-part “Work & Family: Getting to 50/50” series from Stanford’s Clayman Institute for Gender Research and join the conversation!
