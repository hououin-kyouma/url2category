Published on Nov 11, 2015

Today I will be taking you through two simple, yet effective, mobility drills to de-stress and reduce tension in your neck and shoulders. These exercises are designed to unglue these tissues when they become tacked down to one another during stressful periods of life.

As you bring these exercises into your daily life, work up to 20 to 30 inhales and exhales for each of them. The goal is to increase your mobility, improve the function of your spine and most of all give you the tools to a pain free life.

If you have any questions feel free to leave them in the comments below!

Cheers to a pain free life:)
