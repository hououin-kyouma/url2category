Published on Jul 5, 2013

http://www.AllYouWantInLife.com
It's easy to lose focus on the important things in your life and to fill the precious time you do have with things that don't mean a damn thing. Prioritizing your time is critical to having a "full" life.

If you start with all the small and trivial things... you'll have no time for the things that really matter. BUT, if you give time to the things that are important first - not only do you have time for them, but you'll find you have time for a whole lot more.

This video analogy depicts this philosophy wonderfully.

Do you need help getting your jar "full"? Visit http://www.AllYouWantInLife.com to learn more.
