Hello friends!

To reduce stress and maintain good physical health, I play my favorite sport: RACQUETBALL. Racquetball has been an important part of my life over the past several years and I wanted to share this part of me with you. I have a huge passion for the game and I'm extremely thankful for its existence.

If you haven't played, I highly recommend you try it out. It's a fantastic workout and an immense amount of fun.

Enjoy this short compilation video highlighting a few shots/drills from my recent practice.
