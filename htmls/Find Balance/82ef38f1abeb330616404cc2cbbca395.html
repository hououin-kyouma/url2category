Published on Jan 17, 2014

Relationships are the 'Cornerstone' of life and no matter what we say or think about them, we all need and crave them. Not superficial or false relationships, but ones of meaning and depth in which we are free to fully be ourselves.

These kinds of relationships can be few and far between!

In the 3rd part of this Building Better relationships series I have attempted to illustrate why the stress that many of us experience can stem from in the context of our daily relationships.

When we place expectations on other people (either too high or too low) we are fundamentally judging them which can always be felt and will generally always be responded to in ways that will very seldom met our personal standards.

Learning to manage our expectations of others is key to the development of healthier relationships alongside the condition of our very own mental and emotional wellbeing.
