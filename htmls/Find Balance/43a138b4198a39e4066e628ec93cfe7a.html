Looking to Live a Healthier Lifestyle?

Do you want to have a healthier lifestyle, lose some weight but find it difficult?

Do you worry you are not getting a balanced diet?

Do you worry you and your family could live more healthily but struggle with the time to make it happen?

Whether you are already living a healthy lifestyle but worried you are not getting enough individual nutrients, such as B12 and Cholorphyll, or looking for a more effective way for you and your family to live a healthier lifestyle, our SuperFood products can support your health.

Here at LiveWheatgrass we are passionate about a healthy lifestyle and nutrition, but we understand that today’s world doesn’t always make it easy to be healthy, so we are dedicated to helping you and your family do yourselves some good from the inside out – simply and conveniently. We produce and instantly freeze the 100% pure, fresh SuperFood Juices and ship them straight to your door. You can then add the frozen SuperFood shots to your preferred juice or freshly juiced smoothies.

Wheatgrass Juice is known as the original Superfood and has for many years been highly regarded around the world for its health promoting and energising properties based on its nutrient content.

It is a remarkable whole food, containing an abundance of 100% natural nutrients all integrated by nature into a balance unmatched by synthetic health products. It is particularly rich in Vitamin B5, B12 and Vitamin E and it contains Vitamin A in 100ml. Wheatgrass juice may therefore:

•  support physical and mental performance and sense of well-being.
•  encourage a healthy immune system.
•  support healthy skin and hair.
•  contribute to red blood cell development.
•  increase vitality to healthy levels, providing a tangible immediate and long term energy boost.
