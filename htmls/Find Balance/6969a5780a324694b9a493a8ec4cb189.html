Published on Feb 21, 2015

Topic: Healthy Life Style.
General purpose: To inform.
Specific purpose: To explain a simple ways to have a healthy life.
Central idea: Living a healthy life style will make a person feel good and look good in their life in easy way.
Introduction
I. Many people wants a healthy life style even there are young or adult and either a man or girl.
II. Healthy life style are needed to all people because it can give more positive effect for us in our life to prevent our body from any disease such as heart disease and high blood pressure.
III. However healthy life style does not mean you have to diet, eat supplement or take any skinny drug or vitamin C for man or girl who like to build their body faster for body builder.
IV. Today, I would like to explain you about a simple ways to have a healthy life.
(Transaction: Let me begin my first point which is eating well.)
Body
I. Eating a healthy diet is part how to live a healthy life.
II. Besides, it is not only can clean diet help with weight management, it also can improve your health and quality of life when you get older.
III. We also can use the new “My Plate” to determine how many calories you need and what food groups you should focus on or if you are looking for smaller changes you can use these tips for simple ways to change how you eat.
a. Eat more fruit.
b. Sneak in more vegetables.
c. Switch your salad dressing.
d. Eat low fat food.
e. Make some substitutes.
(Transition: Now we know how to manage our food now we go through to next ways is exercise)
I. As we always hear exercise is good for us for people who always wants to feel better, have more energy and perhaps to live longer.
II. When we have a free time we should do regular physical activity that can help you to prevent or manage wide range of health problems and also can give more benefits for our body such as:
a. Exercise can combats health conditions and diseases.
b. Exercise can improve mood.
c. Exercise can boots more energy.
d. Exercise can promotes better sleep.
(Transition: After we know about the benefits of exercise now we look at my next point is habits.)
I. For having a healthy life not only just eat well and exercise but we also should not make a bad habits like drink alcohol, smoking and take any drugs.
II. Drinking can make a person potentially in serious physical effects. Furthermore, it can also cause liver disease and can increase risk of heart attack, weight gain and cancer.
III. Smoking also can increase your lungs cancer and heart diseases. If you are already a smoker, could be the ideal time to quit for your health.
IV. Before you are taking a pills you have to read the instruction first and find out as much as information as you can about any pills you are using include the risk.
(Transition: Based on all in the above now we know the ways to have a healthy life.)

Conclusion
I. Creating a healthy life style does not have to mean a drastic changes for our body.
II. In fact, drastic changes almost always lead to failure.
III. Making small changes in how you live each day can lead to big rewards, so figure out what we can to be a healthy life today.
Bibliography
Hitti, Miranda. (2015). Healthy Living: 8 Steps to Take Today. Web MD Feature.
Mayo Clinic Staff. (2015). Exercise: 7 Benefits of Regular Physical Activity. Mayo Foundation for Medical Education and Research.
Lew, Megan. (2014). Students: Smoking, Alcohol and Drugs. NHS Choices.
