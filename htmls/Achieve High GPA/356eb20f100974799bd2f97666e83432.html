High School Seniors

Visiting Pacific

A campus visit is a great way to find out what Pacific University has to offer you! You can meet a faculty member or a coach, talk with an admissions counselor, and take a campus tour. Schedule a visit with us Monday – Friday.  

If you have any questions, please contact our Visit Coordinator, Martha Tunall, at 503-352-2218, 800-677-6712, or admissions@pacificu.edu.

 

Apply to Pacific

Pacific University uses the Common Application exclusively for undergraduate admission. Apply Now.

If you have any questions, please contact Admissions, at 503-352-2218, 800-677-6712, or admissions@pacificu.edu.

 

Admission

Applications are accepted for both the Fall and Spring semesters, and are reviewed on a rolling admissions basis. Admissions decisions are typically made within three weeks of receipt of a completed application package.

Admitted students will receive a formal acceptance package that includes a confirmation agreement, housing information, and merit-based scholarship information.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Merit scholarships are automatically awarded to admitted students based on their incoming GPA and test scores. Pacific offers students the opportunity to apply for a variety of talent awards as well.

To receive consideration for need-based financial aid, you will need to complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) form. You can apply for the FAFSA online. Send your FAFSA information to Pacific University by using our institution code 003212.
