What Classes Do I Need to Take?

The Arizona Department of Education requires that all students complete the minimum required credits for their graduating cohort class to attend any of the Maricopa Community Colleges.  Students selecting to attend a University are also required to take 2 semesters of Language.  ALL STUDENTS are also required to Meet or Exceed each section of the Arizona Instrument to Measure Standards (A.I.M.S) Exam to receive a High School Diploma. Below are the ADE Requirements according to Graduation Cohort Class. 


﻿How Important Are Grades?﻿

Grades are very important. Earn good grades (As and Bs) each year to maintain a high Grade Point Average (GPA). A high GPA will benefit you when you are ready to apply for school and are applying for Financial Aid or scholarships! Some schools will allow you to enter their Honors Programs with and award you a scholarship if you have a high GPA. Study, do homework assignments, read, and practice writing. Most importantly, COMMUNICATE with your teachers! Do not assume you have a good grade, VERIFY!

How Will I Pay for College?

There are many options to help finance your education. Dedicate a few hours or days to researching your favorite college. Find out what type of financial aid or scholarships they offer. Or ask your favorite teacher if they know of any private scholarship funds you can apply for.  Make sure you take advantage of Maya's Financial Aid workshops and be an activate participant in every step of the application process!

You may qualify for the following:
  • Financial aid
  • Grants
  • Scholarships
  • Loans
See your local Community College Financial Aid representative or guidance counselor for more assistance.
