Published on Jan 6, 2017

How do you start a new semester off right? I believe that the best way to start off a new year and a new semester is to set academic goals. However, you have to make goals properly unless you want to become just another statistic. Most students do not set goals at the beginning of each semester and of the ones that do some don’t always stick to them. In this video I’ll go over why you should set goals in school, how to set academic goals and achieve them so that you can get good grades in college.
