Complete NCAA and NAIA Eligibility Requirements http://www.athleticscholarships.net/a...

The minimum GPA depends on the division level you are going to play. Most recruits are concerned about the NCAA Division 1 minimum and that is 2.3 (it is 2.0 for athlete graduating in 2014 and 2015). If you get a 2.3 GPA your test scores are going to need to be very high, it is best to aim for a minimum GPA of 3.0 to play it on the safe side.
