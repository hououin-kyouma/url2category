Published on Jan 8, 2016

I am often asked how did I manage to get 4.0 GPA for 3 consecutive years at university. I thought I'd share some of my study tips in this video. If you have questions and want more details please write me a comment.

I started University as a Bio major and in my second year decided to switch to Geology. My first year grades were not very good (actually they were terrible), so in my second year I decided that I have to do better! From that point on my grades improved dramatically; and if I can do it you can too.
