Published on May 15, 2014

Join Isa Adney, author of Community College Success, to discover everything you need to know about how to get good grades.
We will explore some great resources colleges offer to help you with classes.
Plus, we test students knowledge in our on campus game show.
Finally, Isa sits down with three Phi Theta Kappa honor society students to find out how they keep their grades up.

For quick tips on how to be successful in college, visit our YouTube channel at www.youtube.com/yourtcc22. To find out when more quick tips and new episodes of The SKiNNY are available, like us on Facebook at www.facebook.com/yourtcc22 and follow us on Twitter at www.twitter.com/yourtcc22.
