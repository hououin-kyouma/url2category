Research

add

Over half of our graduates have performed research outside of a traditional classroom setting while at GC, working with our own faculty or receiving funding to work with researchers at other institutions, from Stanford University to the National Institutes of Health

90%

add

We have a long tradition of successfully preparing students for health professions.  Over 90% of our students who complete the application process, including an interview, get accepted to medical school. 

Travel

add

We offer two regular May term courses:  Paleobiology of the Western US and Tropical Biology.  The paleobiology course involves a 5,000 mile cross-country trip to explore fossil beds that span much of the evolution of multicellular life.  Tropical Biology students travel to Belize, where their time is divided between studying forest and coral reef ecology.

About Biology

Distinguished by small class sizes and faculty lab instruction, our biology major offers you excellent preparation for medical school, graduate school and other professional programs. You will gain a solid grasp of core biological principles, as well as cultivating your cultivate analytical skills, and develop your curiosity about the biological world.

“My time with the biology department at Georgetown College helped me understand that reading the material and showing up to class weren't the real act of learning, but rather, encouraged me to be excited about and engaged in the act of scientific discovery.  I began to understand the importance of employing creative, critical-thinking skills with real world applications. I also came to understand the essential role of peer reviewed science, as it holds a light in an otherwise dark world of "alternative facts".  I do not think I would have the stretch of opportunities that are present for me today, had I not started my path in the classrooms and with the awesome professors at GC.  The friendships that were forged in those halls will be cherished forever.”

Megan French,’06, M.D
 

“The education that I received from the biology department was, in my opinion, unparalleled.  The professors were passionate, phenomenal teachers, and had an open door policy that helps students master any topic.” 

 - Dr. Brian Chaffin, ’13, D.O., Internal Medicine Resident at Bowling Green Medical Center

“My years spent in the GC Biology Department are ones that I will always hold dear, especially because of all the brilliant, kind, caring professors who fill the department. I owe much of my own success to their personal investment in their students. Thanks to them, GC is known by medical residencies in the region as a powerhouse that produces numerous future physicians each year. I was proud to boast about my alma mater when interviewing for my residency in Pediatrics!” 

 - Madison Nation, '12

“Without the Georgetown College biology department, I wouldn't have had the courage or the knowledge to get into Optometry school. I couldn't have been more prepared for my entrance exam and my incredible professors are to thank for that!”

 - Hayley Hamman Childers, '16
