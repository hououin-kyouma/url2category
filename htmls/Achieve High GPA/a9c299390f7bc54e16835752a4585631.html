Are you tired of...

Putting in many hours of studying, only to receive mediocre grades?

Always scrambling to complete assignments at the last minute, and never feeling like you have enough time?

This video shares 2 quick strategies that can help you achieve the maximum academic results possible, while putting in the minimum amount of time and effort.

STRATEGY #1

You need to learn to read more quickly, so you'll be able to cover more material in less time. Most importantly, to quicken your pace, you must stop reading each individual word. Some students like to read out loud, or are in the habit of subvocalizing every word in their heads, but this will make you take forever to get through a few pages.

STRATEGY #2

You'll also need to schedule your time effectively, so you don't end up pulling exhausting all-nighters. Write down all the non-studying activities you do in a week (including sleeping, grooming, eating, and class) and about how many hours each takes you. Add up all these hours, and subtract from 168. The difference is the amount of time you have left to study. Once you know this number, you'll find it easier to divide your time between assignments appropriately.
