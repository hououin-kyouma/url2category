Are you tired of the same old library scene or just plain tired? (I know what you’re thinking, “Duh, I’m a college student”) Luckily for DYC students, the city of Buffalo has a lot to offer those of you wishing to get out more or just switch up your studying routine. For those of you who answered, “yes” to being tired, have no fear…coffee is near!

Check out these close-by coffee and eatery stops near the D’Youville area.

Perks Café

448 Elmwood Ave, Buffalo, NY 14222
.3 miles from D’Youville

Perks strives to provide a warm and cozy space to relax and de-stress while enjoying quality food and drink. Perks Café offers locally roasted coffee, tea, and espresso. They also uniquely offer a breakfast and lunch menu all day long. Instagram: perkscafebuffalo

Tipico Coffee

128 Fargo Ave, Buffalo, NY 14201
.2 miles from D’Youville

Famous for their wide variety of coffee types, it is more than likely this café has something to offer every tired, college student.

Spot coffee

227 Delaware Ave, Buffalo, NY 14202
.9 miles from D’Youville

Recognized for selling their coffee just as fresh as they sell their bread, Spot coffee’s specialty is buying in-season coffees harvested from their country of origin, to ensure optimal freshness at all times.

Starbucks

235 Delaware Ave, Buffalo, NY 14202
.9 miles from D’Youville

Is an intro even necessary for this one? If you’ve never been, add this one to the TOP of your list!!! They have wifi…

Spartan café, DAC and the library are all great resources DYC offers to its students, but sometimes they can be overcrowded or let’s be honest…boring. Hopefully this post helps inspires you to get out there and try new places. Happy studying!

– Stephanie Bowman

Business Management, Senior
