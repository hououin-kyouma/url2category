Hello guys! I have a cold and a sore throat so I wasn’t capable on doing a voiceover! Either way, I hope you enjoy this very simple plan-with-me for school! As you can see, I use a very simple color coding system to organize my week, my tasks and my assignments – you can create one of my little “index” guides with a piece of cardstock and the same stickers you are going to use to keep track of your tasks. Then, all you need to do is hole punch the foldable and keep it tucked in your Filofax or Kikki K.
