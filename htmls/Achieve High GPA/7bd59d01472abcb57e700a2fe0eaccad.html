I graduated with a 3.51 in Mechanical Engineering from CSULB

In my last video I discussed why getting a high GPA is important. Now in this one I give practical advice that worked for me in engineering school.

1. Find quality study buddies with the same goals
2. Find a upperclassman as a mentor
3. Buy your books before the quater starts
4. Get the solution manual to your book
5. Get old tests
6. Understand yourself
7. Attitude
