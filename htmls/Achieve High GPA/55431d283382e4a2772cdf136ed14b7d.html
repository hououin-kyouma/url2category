College Access Advising

Send more students to college

More students than ever need help to pursue their college dreams. Use our free brochures and workshop materials to help your students plan, prepare and pay for college.

Great Lakes Education Philanthropy // About Our Philanthropy // College Access Advising

Our field-tested printed materials and PowerPoint presentations give you the tools you need to motivate your students and help them navigate the path to college.

Available to all high schools in Arkansas, Iowa, Minnesota, North Dakota, Ohio and Wisconsin, our pieces were developed to help students take the steps they need to get into college.
