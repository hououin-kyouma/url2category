From a 2.8 to 3.0 GPA

If you currently have a 2.8 GPA or B- average, these are the future grades you will need to maintain for the remainder of your classes to graduate with a 3.0 B average. To use this table, find the semester you most recently completed in the first column, then locate the GPA you must maintain to achieve a 3.0. Note that it becomes harder to change your GPA as you progress in your studies.
