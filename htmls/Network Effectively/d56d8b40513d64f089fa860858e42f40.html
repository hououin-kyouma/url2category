Networking Like a Pro 

Blog
June 12, 2017
Crosstown Solutions

A central Crosstown Solutions, Inc. principle is the ability to place value in relationships. There are several techniques that will help you successfully build and strengthen new connections. Here are just a few of the best ideas.

Remember to follow up. In Crosstown Solutions, Inc. training sessions, every person learns to make contact with a new connection within the first 30 days of meeting. Not only that, when our people follow up, they find it helpful to virtually introduce a contact to other individuals who may benefit from the meeting as well. Simply write an email that connects two parties together. You will find that most people are very thankful that you are helping to build their networks quickly.

Reconsider your priorities. Determine how much time you can dedicate to reaching out to new contacts, and who else in your organization wants to take part. You don’t have to be a manager to network well. Rely on some of your colleagues who may be inclined to strike up new conversations easily. Allow your energetic team to lead the way during networking events.  

Analyze all your objectives. In Crosstown Solutions, Inc.’s meetings, we weigh the pros and cons when it comes to networking events. Many networking outings and groups charge businesses a fee, so our people always write out a list of positives and compare it to the company’s overall plan.
