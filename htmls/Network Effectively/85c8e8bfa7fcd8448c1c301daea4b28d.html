Network Like a Pro: Toledo
Tuesday, June 27, 2017
Toledo, OH

Join us for networking with fellow Buckeyes in the Toledo area

Network Like a Pro is coming to you. At the event, sponsored by the Bill and Susan Lhota Office of Alumni Career Management, you’ll learn from and interact with a panel of experienced Toledo-area alumni as they share their perspectives on the value of networking and how it has advanced their careers.

Panelists

Ursula Barrera-Richards – Human Resources Director, Ohio Department of Mental Health & Addiction Services at NOPH
Mary Galvin – Philanthropic Services Officer, St. Ursula Academy
Katie Rofkar – President of Lucas County Alumni Club, Associate Product Manager, Owens Corning
Dorian Slaybod – Director of Legal Services, Lucas County Common Pleas Court, Domestic Relations Division

This discussion will be preceded and followed by optional networking opportunities.

Schedule

6 – 6:45 p.m. Registration, appetizers and drinks
6:45 – 8 p.m. Panel discussion
8 – 8:30 p.m. Casual networking

Location

Mancy's Italian Grill
5453 Monroe St.
Toledo, OH 43623

Parking is available in the surface lot outside of the restaurant.

Registration

Your $10 registration includes the panel discussion, appetizers and two drink tickets.

Unfortunately, we will not be able to provide refunds after your registration is processed. Registration is limited to alumni. Alumni are permitted to register for themselves and up to one guest (including non-alumni).

Questions
