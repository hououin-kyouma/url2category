How to Network

with Creative Entrepreneurs

Book online through Eventbrite

Ravensbourne and The Factory are really excited to be working with Creative Entrepeneurs to bring you a fantastic and valuable workshop that will demystify the magic of networking. 

Launched by Carolyn Dailey a year ago at No. 10 Downing Street, Creative Entrepreneurs is a movement to bring together - for the first time in one place - the resources, inspiration and networks creative people need to turn their ideas into successful businesses. They deliver this mission across all platforms: in person at their events, and digitally via their website, social media and newsletter.

We are often told how important networking is but not so often told how to do it well; when to use a business card, when is the right moment to introduce yourself and your story, how to follow up on an introduction and how to keep your all-important confidence up.

Getting really hands on you will have the opportunity to try out your new networking skills after the workshop during a relaxed drinks reception.

Find out more about Creative Entrepreneurs here: http://creativeentrepreneurs.co/
