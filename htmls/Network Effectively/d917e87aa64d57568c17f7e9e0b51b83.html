How to Network Like a Pro

April 4, 2017

Registration is now closed. On-site registration will be available.

New York Women in Communications Student Affairs and High School Outreach Committees invite college and high school students to join us for our How To Network Like a Pro event in the Live with Kelly Studios at WABC on April 4 at 6:00 pm.

Attendees will get tips from master networker, Jill Jacinto, on how to network most effectively both in person as well as through email and social media. Then you will take part in short breakout sessions led by NYWICI’s young professionals, who are themselves very familiar with the struggles of networking and know the ins and outs of navigating toward a first job or internship. We’ll cover topics like: ideas for being less awkward while waiting to talk to someone; the best way to follow up after meeting someone at a networking event; and we’ll do some on-the-spot role playing. 

Afterwards, join us for some incredible raffles including 2 gift cards each valued at $50 generously donated by MOO, a free ticket to a NYWICI's Behind the Scenes Event, and the opportunity to gain some expert advice during a one on one coffee date with Jill Jacinto.

Space is limited so sign up soon, for an event where you’ll gain insight and knowledge that could otherwise take years of trial-and-error experience to gather. 

Jill Jacinto is an NYC based millennial career expert who loves helping young professionals with their careers. Jill has secured several guest spots on Fox & Friends and has landed coverage in Forbes, Glamour, USA Today Business Insider, Men’s Health, Refinery29, Fox Business, and Yahoo! Finance. You can learn more about Jill at JillJacinto.com

Date: Tuesday, April 4, 2017
Time: 6:00 p.m. - 8:00 p.m.
6:00 - 6:30 p.m. Networking/Refreshments | 6:30 - 7:15 p.m. Speaker & Breakout Sessions | 7:15 - 8:00 p.m. Continued Networking/Refreshments
Location: Live with Kelly Studio, WABC
7 Lincoln Square, New York, NY 10023 
However, the easiest way to locate the studio is to find the corner of West 67th and Columbus Ave. Click HERE for a map.
