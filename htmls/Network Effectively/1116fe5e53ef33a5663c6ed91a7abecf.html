Exploratory Activity - Participation in industry networks to improve professional competency and benefit the organisation

Purpose

The purpose of this exploratory learning activity is to assist the learner to:

  • participate in professional/industry networks to improve their professional competencies and benefit the organisation
  • achieve industry recognition through the process of networking.

Preparation for using the resource

The coach will need to:

  • read the tutorial activities for an explanation of professional competence and networking
  • suggest that the learner reads the tutorial activities concerning 'Managing self'.

Prior instructions to be provided to the learner

The activity is to be completed in the learner's workplace.

Other related resources or associated activities

  • Case study - Developing new skills
  • Collaboration activity - Evaluating work performance
  • Tutorial activity - Manage self
  • Workplace activity - Self organising.

Ways to customise the resource

Where the learner is unable to access the live chat or bulletin board, face-to-face, e-mail or telephone discussions can be used instead.

Aspects that learners may find difficult

  • Objective self assessment of own competencies
  • Having the time to participate in a network of professional/industry associations.
  • Analysing personal strengths and weaknesses and deciding what you need to do to improve your workplace competencies
  • Improving rapport and communication with individuals and professional/industry associations.
  • Using new technologies to improve personal performances, effectiveness and efficiencies for data transmission.

Ways of promoting collaboration or interaction between learners

  • Assist in identifying other learners doing the same unit
  • Assist in setting up learning cohorts
  • Assist in setting up live chat sessions or discussion sessions.

Assistance to provide the learner

  • Providing sufficient time and resources (physical and human) to complete the activity
  • Provide advice and guidance on how to improve the workplace relationships
  • Assisting in the identification of the people to chat and discuss methods and techniques used to maintain and build positive workplace relationships
  • Assisting in ensuring that chats and discussions continue for the duration of the activity and remain confidential
  • Approving suggestions to improve the effectiveness of the workplace relationships
  • Providing guidance and feedback on the implemented action plan(s).

Suggested answers have not been provided for this activity.

This activity is designed to improve professional competency through participation and involvement in industry and professional networks and associations appropriate to your responsibilities and organisation purposes.

The outcome from this activity will be improved competencies and industry recognition through the process of networking.

Exploring your knowledge

For an explanation of professional competence requirements, refer to the tutorial activities 'Manage self'.

1. How important is your professional competence in the context of yourself, your team, your customers and your suppliers? Explain why.

2. What competencies are you required to have to perform your job in a professional manner? List your job competency requirements and authorisation requirements.

3. How often are your competencies reassessed and why is this required?

4. List the professional/industry networks and associations that you are involved with?

Constructing your understanding

5. What industry standards are applicable to the work you perform? Explain why.

6. How does the involvement of the professional/industry networks and associations in 4 above enhance your professional competencies.

7. Provide an example of how you have improved your professional competence in the areas of:

  • technical job related skills
  • interpersonal skills
  • conceptual skills.

8. List the benefits of your continued networking to your current professional/industry competencies and to your organisation.

You can discuss the benefits obtained using live chat or the bulletin board.

Click on live chat or bulletin board to give your comments.

Transferring your knowledge to the workplace

9. How have you applied your improved competence obtained through the professional/industry association networks in your workplace? Provide two supporting examples.

10. Describe how your involvement with professional/industry associations has provided direct benefit to your organisation's goals and plans. Provide one example.

11. Discuss the benefits of continued networking with professional/industry associations with your manager and develop a proposal to obtain organisation support in time/costs to maintain your networking arrangements. Use the supporting information in 9 and 10 above to justify any proposed arrangement.

12. Provide a copy of the proposal to your coach and assessor for information purposes.

13. Negotiate and agree on a suitable arrangement with your management.

You can discuss the results using live chat or the bulletin board.

Click on live chat or bulletin board to give your comments.

Reflecting on your learning

14. Has networking directly contributed to your improved competencies? Has it been beneficial to your career and to others? Record your experiences and what you have learnt as a result of this activity in your journal.
