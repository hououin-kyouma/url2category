Welcome to another Kim Kardashian: Hollywood game tip by GamerU. Today, I’m going to walk you through how to network your way to the top! It’s really one of the keys to this game. Thankfully, you have Kim Kardashian herself giving you a huge advantage by being friends with her and all the amazing contacts she’s providing for you throughout the game. But you’ll have to do tons of schmoozing and socializing as well. So here’s a few helpful tips.
You want to add as many people as you can to your contact list. You can meet new people at restaurants, bars, nightclubs and just walking along the street. When you meet someone for the first time, you will have two options. You can introduce yourself, which will earn you points or you can guess their name (50/50 shot) and if you guess correctly, you’ll earn even more points! However, if you guess wrong, they will get angry and the only way to make it right is by charming them which costs you some K-Stars.
Be careful when charming people because K-Stars are very valuable and you don’t need to be charming every single person you meet. You definitely want to charm anyone Kim, your manager or publicist introduces you to. As well as anyone in the entertainment industry such as models, fashion designers, set-designers and pop-stars who are one letter above your current ranking. These contacts will earn you extra bonus points if you invite them to gigs to help you.
Continue to build your relationships by charming, networking and even buying gifts for your contacts to level up quicker!
