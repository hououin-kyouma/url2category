How to network at a fair?

14.9.2017 08:30-10:00
Career Services

How to network at a fair?

Location: 
Helsinki
Hanken, Futurum
Preparing session for Hanken Network Day 2017.

Career Services will organize a preparing session for Hanken Network Day where the coach Alexandra Ohls from AO Coaching & Consulting will give her best tips on how to make contacts and network at a fair.

Hanken Network Day and other fairs are a great chance to meet several companies at once and ask career related questions that you have been wondering about. But how exactly can you make the most out of these meetings and use them as an important tool while pondering on your future career? Join us on 14 September and find out more.

You can register here. Keep in mind that your registration is binding, so make sure to input the event in your calendar. Register the latest on 12 September.
