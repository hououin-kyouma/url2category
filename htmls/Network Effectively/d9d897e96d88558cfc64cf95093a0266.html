Alumni Association Professional Network

 

If you're a student looking to learn about career fields, expand your professional network, and or improve your job search strategy, your Alumni Association is here for you. We encourage you to join the Alumni Association Professional Network group on LinkedIn. Our group has a subgroup specifically for student-to-alumni networking. The goal is to connect you with alumni who are willing to provide career advice and insights. 

It's never too early to start thinking about careers and building your professional network. Our alumni were once students just like you, and they remember how it is to be in your shoes.

Our Career Network group on LinkedIn is not an employment or placement program; rather, it is a forum for networking. Reach out to our alumni and tap into their talents, insights and connections.
