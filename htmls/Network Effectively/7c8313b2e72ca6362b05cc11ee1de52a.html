My Story

Kistin Gunnis will inspire and stimulate reflection.

Regularly thrown in the deep end, professionally and personally; Kistin has found a way to accept herself and overcome difficulties to live life to the full.

Having worked internationally for large organisations in operations roles, Kistin’s identity was linked directly to her corporate role.  Post corporate, she is building a global community for professional women and is undertaking a significant personal transition from being the women behind other leaders to being an inspiring leader.
