How to Network Panel

Career advancement is just as much about who you know as what you know.

Need to brush up on your networking skills? Feel nervous attending networking events? Join us on March 23 for a “How to Network” panel in partnership with the CGU Alumni Association, the CGU Office of Student Life and Diversity, the Graduate Student Council, and Keck Graduate Institute. Our panelists will discuss tools for business and social etiquette that will help you get the most out of networking events.

Then, put your new networking knowledge into action the following week! Join students and alumni from CGU and Keck Graduate Institute on March 30 for the very first “2-C” networking social at the CGU President’s House. Practice networking in a friendly environment while enjoying light refreshments. Hors d’oeuvres, beer, and wine will be served.
