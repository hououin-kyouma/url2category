5 Hacks For Overcoming Social Anxiety And Networking Like a Pro
  • Share This: Share on TwitterShare on LinkedinShare on Facebook
  • Copy Link

June 27, 2017

Thinking about attending a networking event or a career fair can stir up some feelings of anxiety.  A certain degree of social anxiety is completely normal–even helpful. Don’t give anxiety all the power and let it hold you back from building a strong network and discovering great opportunities!  Check out these 5 hacks to get yourself out there and to not let your nerves get the best of you: https://www.forbes.com/sites/meganbruneau/2016/05/28/5-hacks-to-overcoming-social-anxiety-networking-like-a-pro/#42fb1864482b
