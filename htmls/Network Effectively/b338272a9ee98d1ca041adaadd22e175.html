Young Professional Network

The Young Professional Network (YPN) is a group of German & American professionals with up to 10 years of work experience, searching for networking opportunities and knowledge exchange within our Business Network.

In addition to our regular chamber events such as Receptions, Luncheon, Conferences and Roundtables, we are also hosting exclusive events for our Young Professionals (YP). These events include networking and workshops to help increase skills that are relevant in today’s business world.

Mitgliedschaftsvorteile

  • Gain valuable networking and leadership skills that contribute to your personal development
  • Expand your network of business contacts
  • Increase your knowledge base and skills through YP-tailored workshops
  • Enjoy exchange between YP and established GACC members
  • Increase your visibility through event attendance and participation
  • Receive exclusive invitations to YP events
  • Obtain reduced admission fee to GACC member events
  • Be part of a unique German-American Community
  • Possibility to purchase the exclusive membership directory (access for members only)

    • For more information see our YPN Flyer!

Mitgliedsbeitrag

We offer a special discount to young professionals up to the age of 30.

  • $100 per year (up to 30 years of age)
  • $150 per year (31 years of age and older)
