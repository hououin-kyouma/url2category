Sports Media & Communication Course
Learn the Skills to Thrive in Every Part of the Sports Industry

There is an underlying skill that is essential in all walks of life but is often taken for granted, the ability to communicate. In the sports industry there is a constant exchange of information, from teams to the media, from brands to their consumers, from scouts to their bosses, from agents to their clients.
The stream never ends.

We all have an audience; someone we are trying to reach through our words or actions - but how effective are we at reaching them? To thrive in the multi-billion dollar sports industry, you must harness the skill of effective communication.

At SMWW, we’ll teach you how to effectively communicate in sports through written and spoken words.

Led by Seattle Seahawks radio sideline reporter Jen Mueller, our 8-week online Sports Media and Communication course teaches you how to leverage the power of communication to grow and enhance your sports career.

Unlike most online classes, we focus our program on audio chats directly with your course mentor, which allows you the opportunity to build personal relationships and rapport with a successful leader in the sports media world. After completing our Sports Media and Communication course, we will help you redesign your resume to highlight your new skills and include that you have been taught first-hand by Jen Mueller. Now that is powerful!

To advance your career in public relations, sports communications, content creation, new media, promotions and marketing you need to learn the skills and inside secrets that sports executives seek.

Why You Should Take our Sports Media and Communications Course

You are living in the golden era of the sports media. With more broadcast channels and digital media outlets than ever before, there is an endless array of opportunities revolving around sports content creation in the global sports world.

The flip side is, as the sports conversation grows so does the demand for talented professionals on the team, league and organizational side to manage the information and access to coaches, athletes and executives. And it doesn’t stop at there, iconic sports brands need talented communicators to help with marketing, positioning, branding and more.

Every role in the sports industry needs strong communicators.

“If you are trying to be an agent or a scout, if you are trying to work in PR or community relations or sales or marketing, it doesn’t really matter, your ability to communicate effectively directly impacts how successful you are going to be in your career,” says course mentor Jen Mueller.

“The whole class builds up to a communication plan, which is a very hands-on way to get a feel for all the things you could be doing for a sports team or organization,” informs course mentor Jen Mueller.

Upon completion of the course you will:

Meet Your Course Mentors

Jen Mueller

Jen Mueller pursued a career in sports broadcasting after repeated comments of "talks too much" from teachers and family members. A 14-year sports communications veteran, Jen currently serves as the sideline radio reporter for the Super Bowl Champion Seattle Seahawks. She is also part of the Seattle Mariners television broadcast team on ROOT Sports.

In addition to her work on the sidelines, Jen launched Talk Sporty to Me in 2009 and teaches business professionals how to improve communication and leverage fandom in business. Jen published her first book Game Time: Learn to Talk Sports in 5 Minutes a Day for Business. She graduated from Southern Methodist University in 2000 with degrees in Journalism and Public Policy. Her goal is to assist you with advancing your career by breaking down barriers to entry. Read more about Jen here.

 

Dr. Lynn Lashbrook

Dr. Lynn Lashbrook, is an online sports education evangelist who has coordinated a team of sports business experts to train students to transform their current career into a sports business career that they are passionate about. Lynn has been working and teaching in Sports Management for over 40 years and has demonstrated a lifelong passion for helping students strategize and reach their career goals in the world of sports. Dr. Lashbrook has served at the collegiate level as an athletic director and athletic academic advisor. At the professional level, he has been an NFL sports agent representing hundreds of athletes. His high energy and motivating personality captivates students and athletes.

Under Dr. Lashbrook's leadership, Sports Management Worldwide successfully trains thousands of students every year for sports business careers, offering a global sports faculty with over 5000 alumni from over 140 countries.
