5.0 out of 5 stars Whether you do or don't know how to network, this book is for you.
BySachin on 3 May 2012 - Published on Amazon.com
Verified Purchase
Firstly I would say, I hate networking. Now, I still hate it, just hate it less, much less. But face it, networking is important to businesses, especially if you're an entrepreneur.

This book is simple, gives solid examples and execution strategies, a process to follow and best of all, a way to measure the success of your networking efforts; as an engineer, that was golden.

Now I'm more focused in my efforts, able to sift through the fluff, categorise my contacts, and see how I'm doing. The confidence I gained from just learning the process was enough to help me actually talk to people.

Now if only I could be less lazy and learn to follow up. Unfortunately, I know of no book that can do that for me, but if you do know, please let me know.
