How to Network and Build Contacts at Conferences
Networking at Revolve Conference was amazing, but it also brought to light the importance of in person networking and the value of business cards and follow ups.

Networking is something I wish I had put more emphasis on in my Graphic Design career when I started, but as I shifted into business and marketing, networking with influential people in multiple industries was something I focused on.

When Networking I think it is important to have 3 ways to get in touch with someone. Following up with your network contacts after a conference is crucial, and you wan to make sure people realize you value the relationship.

A business card leaves an impression and I'm now realizing that putting your face on a business card can be great for networking events so that people can remember exactly who you are and have context.

If you want to grow your business whether you do marketing, graphic design, photography or anything, I highly encourage to attend conferences and Networking events and develop a strong networking strategy.
