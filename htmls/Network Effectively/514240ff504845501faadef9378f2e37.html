NETWORKING and How to Network like a PRO | @BrendonHansford VLOG

In this video, you are going to learn the do's and don't of networking and how to network like a PRO.

Starting with how to research and build databases in your chosen industry, sending an email that won't get ignored and how to follow up. FORTUNE IS IN THE FOLLOW UP.

I will teach how to actually network with people face to face and utilise the research you have done to get REAL results.

Lastly, there is a list of places you can go to network with the RIGHT PEOPLE.
