Network like a pro!

We’re happy to announce that the first joint event between CMI North East and WiM North East was highly successful, with over 40 attendees in Newcastle Upon Tyne.

We covered -

  • The power of networking
  • Dispelling the myths
  • The benefits and value gained from networking
  • How to break down the barriers and eliminate the fears
  • Connecting the dots - How to build an effective network
  • Learn practical usable tools and tips to make your networking more effective
  • Putting what you have learnt into practice

To celebrate excellence in management, we also presented 3 awards to outstanding CMI Members. Victoria Austin CMgr FCMI who won the Mentor of the Year award, David Todd CMgr MCMI for achieving Chartered Manager of the Year and Allan Spencer, winner of the Managing Performance Award. We would also like to recognise Ruth-Louise Scholefield who unfortunately could not join us on the evening, but was the winner of the Outstanding Management Student Award.

Benefits of Membership

Gain professional recognition

Gain recognition for enhancing your knowledge, understanding and practice of management. Display your post nominal as a commitment to continuous professional development and accomplishment as a manager.

Improve your management skills

For managers who want to sharpen their professional edge and enhance their effectiveness, membership offers access to qualifications, courses, mentoring and unrivalled support resources. You can also achieve the ultimate management accolade, Chartered Manager status.

Boost your career earnings

Independent research proves that individuals with professional qualifications and membership stand to earn an additional £152,000 throughout their career. You'll also be part of an extensive management community, opening doors for career progression.

Raise your confidence & competence

Membership equips you to meet daily management challenges in a professional, proficient way. Improving performance and effectiveness at managing people and projects will raise your confidence and ultimately, status in your organisation.
