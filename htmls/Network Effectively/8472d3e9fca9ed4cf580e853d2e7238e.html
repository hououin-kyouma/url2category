Event description

Description

"HAVE YOU EVER WANTED TO BUILD A TEAM OF BUSINESS PROFESSIONALS THAT REFERRED YOU NEW BUSINESS FOR FREE BUT DIDN'T KNOW HOW?"

Calling all Social Entrepreneurs, Business Owners and Change Makers, Retailers, Café / Restaurant Owners, Authors, Coaches, Consultants, Entrepreneurs and Anyone looking to create strategic relationships with other like minded and proactive businesss owner.

BNI is ready to introduce you to the world of networking and how ot build and grow your business through referrals. For the past 16 years BNI has provided Australian businesses with a strategy to generate a constant flow of qualified business referrals and we are pleased to continue extending this opportunity in the Gold Coast region.

Members of BNI in Australia reported that they generated $416 million of business referrals last year. Over the same period, BNI members worldwide passed over $11 billion of new business. On the Gold Coast, members generated over $26 million through 16070 referrals.

We invite you to attend this event where you will learn about neworking like a pro, what BNI is and, more importantly, “What’s in it for you” when you participate with this world-leader in word of mouth and relationship based marketing strategies.

In this interactive Discovery Session, we will have -

  • Open Networking
  • Educational Workshop - Networking Like a Pro including 10 Traits of a Master Networker
  • Information on BNI including testimonials.


Note: Registration is required prior to the event to ensure numbers are firm and catering is appropriate. If for any reason the event is cancelled and the event is a paid event, all registrants will receive notification as well as a full refund.

If you require more information please contact Jane Taylor on jane.taylor@bni.com.au
