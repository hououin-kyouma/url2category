How to network like a pro

Get the fiancée of your dreams and become a millionaire in just 3 hours! - Just kidding. Instead you can learn how to become the Batman/Wonder Woman of networking.

Hvornår?

10. okt. 2017 KL. 16:30 - 19:30
Tilmeldingsfrist
5. okt. 2017 - 23:55
Arrangementsnummer

19 ledige pladser

Hvor?

CREATE
Rendsburgsgade 14
9000 Aalborg

Lok. 5.125 + 5.127
Tilmeld
Priser
Arrangør: Universitetsudvalg, IDA Nord
Medarrangør: CrossCulturalCommunity, IDA Nord

This is a hands-on workshop, where you will learn the techniques that Frederik van Deurs Managing, Partner and Founder - Green Tech Challenge, have used to build a network with thousands of decision makers, cultural influencers and thought-leaders. You will learn how to make the right decisions in your way on the job marked.

You will walk away from the workshop with a clear idea of which events will be relevant for YOU and how to get the most out of attending these events if you want to learn more.

Jump the line to your dream job by learning how to network with the decision makers - don't hesitate, sign up before your neighbour!

 

There will be a sandwich and something to drink at the event.

For any question contact Mathilde Husted on mail: mathildeh@ida.dk or phone: 25731103.

NOTE: In case of problems with the attendance application, Helle Ekstrøm can be contacted at hek@ida.dk. Indicate member number / CPR number and event number. If you are unable to attend the event, please report a cancellation. Cancellations can be reported on www.ida.dk or in case of emergency, it can be given to hek@ida.dk or aau@ida.dk.
