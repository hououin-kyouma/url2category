Motivational, Behavior Improvement Posters
from Youth Change Workshops


These aren't ordinary K-12 school and classroom posters. Created by mental health and special education experts, Youth Change Workshops' posters are cleverly crafted to practically "trick" resistant students into learning new behaviors, attitudes and motivation.

Each attention-grabbing poster delivers a compelling and important message to your students. Each of these special creations facilitates essential insights, while busting through apathy and bad attitudes.

Youth Change's posters aren't just brilliantly engineered to use drama, creativity and surprise to powerfully convey vital messages students normally resist, these designs are especially crafted to complement our books, lessons, interventions and other resources.

These inventive creations even have some "street cred." They've appeared in many movies and TV shows including both Diary of a Wimpy Kid movies.

Maximize your impact by surrounding your students with posters that can change the way they feel, think, act and see the world.These devices are so powerful, that they will immediately put your walls to work changing your students for the better.
