Valencia Degree and Career Programs

No matter your style, Valencia has a program that fits you.

We have several university pre-majors to try on for size, and an A.A. degree that guarantees admission to a Florida state university.

We also offer 33 A.S. career programs that give you the option to pursue a bachelor's degree or strut directly into a new career with a job placement rate of 93% – 95%.

Check out our full line of programs.
