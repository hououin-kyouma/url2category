Business and Management careers

Business and management careers are extremely varied and today's top organisations insist on giving jobs to graduates who can demonstrate fresh ideas and offer their company a competitive edge.

By developing strong business skills and incorporating industry training while you study, you can aim for an exciting management career that offers you flexibility in the modern workplace.

What you'll learn

Business and management graduates are skilled decision-makers, problem-solvers and natural leaders who can work across all facets of an organisation. With good management skills you can choose to specialise in specific areas such as marketing, public relations or event management.

Core business skills include:

  • Leadership and people management
  • Problem-solving
  • Customer service and client liaison
  • Business fundamentals
  • Consumer behaviour

Diploma

The Diploma of Leadership and Management (BSB51915) will be awarded as a university qualification and has been developed by industry professionals and endorsed by a number of industry bodies to equip you with the practical knowledge and skills required to step up and hit the ground running as a team leader or manager in small, medium or large organisation, across all industry sectors. Achieve your move into a leadership role or use your diploma as an entry point into a Business Degree.

Higher Education Diplomas and Degrees

The diploma courses and bachelor degrees offered by APM are university equivalent Higher Education courses fully accredited by the regulatory bodies. The emphasis is on a knowledge and skill-based approach to learning. All course lecturers are industry experts and experienced educators, while the Industry Placement Program and career development components of the degree provide invaluable work experience, helping you to be a cut above the rest to employers.
