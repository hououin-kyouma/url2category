Short Story

I'm Rodvé Lewis and I graduated from San Jose State University in 2011 with a degree in Business Administration-Finance, and a minor in Economics. My passion is writing and telling stories. I've been creatively writing since I was 10.

I finally wrote my first book titled "Dreams or Degrees: How to Graduate from College in Four Years" after many years of contemplation. At first I had no idea where to start, but I just knew that this book would definitely help students and I just started outlining.

My story is unique because I was behind in school, but I was able to make an underdog turnaround to get back on track and out with a cap. Now I feel compelled to share my story with students around the world.

What I Need & What You Get

My goal is $3000 to cover mainly publishing expenses. Publishing is roughly $2000, the other $1000 will be used to get a short run of books printed so I can start selling them. The more money I have toward printing, the cheaper it'll be to print.

It Just Got Real

The fact of the matter is that most schools graduate less than 50% of their students in 4 years. It's actually more like less than 30%. For example, when I graduated from San Jose State University, only 6.8% of the students did it in 4 years, while only 2.3% of the black males graduated in 4 years. It's not normal anymore to graduate in 4 years, so I'm giving you all the keys to success. I didn't write this book to try to make a lot of money. I wrote the book because I felt like what I did was special and if a lot of people agree with me I will be able to continue to write books.

Now don't get me wrong, some books are fun, but most "how-to" books are boring. Well this isn't your typical "how-to" book. I wrote it from my perspective and it reads more like a short story.

Other Ways You Can Help

I understand that everyone isn't in the position to contribute cash, but you can still help. There are millions of students in the world that may need this book: transfer students, high school seniors, regular college students, etc. They might not see my video, but with your help by sharing this, more people may be interested in the book and will have the option to contribute to the campaign.

The Book

Each chapter is self illustrated to give you an idea of how I think. The book will range from 100-150 pages in length when it's finally printed. I kept the typical college student's workload in mind and did my best not to write a text book.
