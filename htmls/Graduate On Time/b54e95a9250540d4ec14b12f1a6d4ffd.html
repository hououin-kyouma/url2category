How will I balance work, family and university life?

Concordia University Chicago recognizes that many find it difficult to attend college classes while working at a full-time job and managing other responsibilities. Our on campus and online degrees are specifically designed for those who need flexible scheduling and learning in an accelerated format. 

Throughout the past 20 years, Concordia University Chicago has worked with adults who know the importance of a college degree, not only in the workplace, but also for creating a better future and better opportunities for themselves and their families.

Our Accelerated Degree Program has been designed and developed by educators who understand how adults learn. You'll join others like you who are juggling family and work. You'll grow and learn together to achieve the success you've always wanted.

Can I afford it?

Concordia University Chicago is one of the most affordable private universities in the Midwest. Our Office of Financial Aid will work with you to seek financial aid and schedule manageable tuition payments.

Format and Pace: Online, Classroom or Blended

Our online, on campus, and blended delivery modes are designed to fit the needs of busy individuals. Both formats foster valuable relationships and support networks that can add value throughout your career. The major coursework can be completed within 16 months and each major course is 5 weeks long. Our advisors can create a specialized program plan to accommodate the needs of each individual student.

*The tuition rate is different for the College of DuPage 3+1 BA in Health Care Management and Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology. Please visit the respective program pages for more information.

**Some courses in the major overlap with the Human Resource Management Certificate. Please visit the program page for more information.
