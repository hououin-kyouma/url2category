Graduate In 4 Years

It typically takes four years of college-level coursework to graduate with a bachelor’s degree. One graduation strategy is to take 12 hours each fall and spring semester and 6 hours during the summer. Another strategy is to take 15 hours each fall and spring semester. Whichever strategy you choose, the academic goal is to have successfully completed 30 hours of coursework each academic year (fall, spring, and summer). See the graph below!
