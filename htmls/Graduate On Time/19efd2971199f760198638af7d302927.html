Degree & Program Offerings

NSU offers more than 150 degrees at the bachelor’s, master’s and doctoral levels. Undergraduate students can choose from more than 50 majors and 50 minors in the arts and sciences, business, education and nursing. As a prospective graduate student you can explore more than 80 master’s and doctoral programs. Many of these are some of the top programs in the nation and include fields like education, business, psychology, computer sciences, marine biology, criminal justice and health care. If you are seeking a professional degree, take a look at NSU’s exceptional and highly competitive programs in law, medicine, clinical psychology, dental medicine, optometry, pharmacy, audiology and physical therapy among others.
