If You're Graduating Early

  1. The student's Integrative Exercise must be completed by March, in order to receive an early degree date.
  2. Register for Bio 399 fall term (3 credits); Bio 400 fall (2 credits) and winter terms (1 credit).
  3. The senior has met ALL major requirements for graduation as specified by the department. (Be sure to meet with your Advisor to verify).
  4. Has attended the required 10 Biology seminars.

 For more information on Graduation Requirements, go to the Registrar's website.
