Converse College

A private master’s university in Spartanburg, SC providing distinctive undergraduate liberal arts education for women and innovative programs for co-ed graduate study

  • Why Converse?
  • Apply for Admission
  • Make a Gift

Voice. Value. Vision.

Converse produces passionate, service-oriented global citizens who lead in their professions and communities – artists and educators, lawyers and doctors, entrepreneurs and business executives committed to making a positive impact on our world.

 

96%

of full-time faculty hold the terminal degree in their field

 

 

– 82% of courses are taught
by full-time faculty

 

– 3 Converse professors have been named SC Professor of the Year since 2007

12:1

student to faculty ratio ensures personal mentoring

Our small classes ensure every student has ample opportunity to interact with professors and speak out in class. Converse students and graduates report stronger relationships with faculty and lifetime mentoring connections.

94%

of our graduates earn their degree in four years or less

A premier private university at a public-school price, Converse offers even more value in that our graduates finish their degree faster than most students at public universities. You can jump start your career while many of your peers are still in school paying tuition!
