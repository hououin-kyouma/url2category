Marketing careers

“Marketing is the activity, set of institutions and processes for creating, communicating, delivering and exchanging offerings that have a value for customers, clients, and society at large.” – AMA American Marketing Association.

In today’s competitive and fast-paced business world having a compelling and unique point of difference is critical to creating value for organisations and its stakeholders. Understanding the mindset and motivations of consumers and designing market offerings that meet their needs is at the core of all marketing activity.

At APM College of Business and Communication we are mad about Marketing. Our marketing lecturers are industry experts who have been there and done it. They bring their expertise into the classroom and provide students with a constructive learning environment. They share their successes and lessons with great passion in lectures and tutorials that are designed to get you on the right track.

What you'll learn

Marketing is a broad field that offers students many different areas of study ranging from advertising to marketing research.

Some of the core skills future marketers will develop include:

  • Problem-solving
  • Creativity
  • Communication skills
  • Leadership & team work
  • Understanding of traditional and digital channels
  • Able to develop brand loyalty
  • Able to execute strategic marketing campaigns

Jobs in marketing

A degree in marketing qualifies graduates to work across a series of different industries such as:

  • FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods)
  • Finance & Banking
  • Consulting
  • Advertising & Promotion
  • Digital Marketing
  • Business-to-Business Marketing

Career outcomes in Marketing include but are not limited to:

  • Brand management
  • Digital marketing management
  • Promotions management
  • Trade marketing management
  • Sales Team Management
  • Customer Relationship Management
 

Higher Education Diploma

A Diploma of Marketing  focuses on learning marketing skill and dedicates more hours to applied, practical and hands-on time – giving you a competitive advantage in the workforce. Use it as an entry-point into your career in marketing or as a stepping stone into the Bachelor of Business (Marketing).

When you study an APM at Torrens University Business Degree, as part of your course you will also complete an industry placement internship, where you will experience working in the industry first hand, gaining the practical skills to complement your theoretical knowledge.
