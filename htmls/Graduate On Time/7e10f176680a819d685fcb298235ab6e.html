Our Freshman Application Deadline Has Been Extended

The UF Freshman Application deadline for the Class of 2022 has been extended to November 15th.

Apply Now for 2018-19 Financial Aid

Complete the 2018-19 FAFSA as soon as possible, starting October 1, 2017. UF's "On-Time" deadline to receive the results of your 2018-19 FAFSA from the federal processor is December 15, 2017. Apply as early as possible to be considered for the most, and best, aid.

New SSAR Tutorial Now Available

The SSAR portal is now open, and prospective students can begin filling in their courses and grades. Students will be unable to link the SSAR to their UF account until after they submit the application. If you need help, check out our tutorial with answers to most questions.
