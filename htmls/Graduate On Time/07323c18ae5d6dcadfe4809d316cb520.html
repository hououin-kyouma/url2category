5 things to consider when applying

Applying to college is a big commitment and we’re here to help. Keep these five things in mind as you get started.

  • REVIEW OUR APPLICATION GUIDES

    Application Guides include everything you need to know – timelines, admission standards, required documents and how to check your status.

  • YOUR ESSAY & RECOMMENDATION

    We go beyond the numbers when reading applications. Your essay and recommendation give you the chance to tell us what you will bring to our campus and contribute to our community.

  • PROGRAMS OF STUDY

    Explore our majors, concentrations and minors to see what Colorado State has to offer and then talk to your admissions counselor about how your passion fits into our programs.

  • WE’RE HERE FOR YOU

    If you need more information before you apply, contact your admissions counselor, visit campus or give us a call. We’re here to help.

  • STAY IN TOUCH

    You can check your application status online, but we’ll also be in touch with you all along the way to becoming a Ram. Be sure to check your email frequently.
