Individual online college courses

Are you looking to learn more about a specific topic or enhance your skill level for professional growth? Our individual college courses can be a great way to increase your knowledge or fulfill educational or professional requirements. Even if you’re not currently interested in a degree program, an individual college course can let you explore topics of interest or offer you the opportunity for personal growth.

You can select from a large catalog of college courses at the associate, bachelor’s and master’s degree level. Many bachelor’s and master’s courses are also available online, with some being offered at select campus locations.

By taking college courses online, you can more conveniently fit continued education into your life. It is also a great introduction to the University of Phoenix experience. As a student you can take advantage of a wealth of online resources, learn from a dedicated and supportive faculty and connect with a network of fellow students.

Our online college courses are designed to help you meet a range of professional and academic goals, such as:

  • Keeping certain professional licensures current.
  • Fulfilling continuing education for teachers requirements.
  • Developing a deeper understanding of topics relevant in your field.
  • Preparing for certain state exams or other professional certifications.

You may also be able to use our college courses to complete degree requirements, allowing you to graduate on time. Please note, if you do wish to transfer credits to an outside school, the transferability of credit is at the sole discretion of the receiving institution.

We make continuing your education convenient, allowing you to take college courses online, from the comfort of your home. Get the education you need, one course at a time.

  • Transferring Credits

Associate, bachelor's and master's courses are academic, for-credit courses. Transferability of credit is at the discretion of the receiving institution. It is the student's responsibility to confirm whether or not credits earned at University of Phoenix will be accepted by another institution of the student's choice. Individual courses are not eligible for federal financial aid.

While widely available, not all courses are available in all locations or in both online and on-campus formats. Please check with a University Enrollment Representative. More information about admissions requirements can be found in the Academic Catalog.
