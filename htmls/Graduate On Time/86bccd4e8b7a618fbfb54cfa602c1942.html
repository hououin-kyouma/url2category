Temple University Admissions

What is Temple Made? Ask any of our students, and you’ll probably get a different answer. It’s hands-on learning and limitless possibility. It’s learning in the rich local culture of Philadelphia to make a global impact. It’s ever-expanding knowledge and opportunity. Most of all, it’s what our students make it.

Apply to Temple

Temple is all about real-world learning. Whether you’re applying for your first year of college, transferring from another university, or an international student looking to join our community, we invite you to learn more about our university and apply for admission.
