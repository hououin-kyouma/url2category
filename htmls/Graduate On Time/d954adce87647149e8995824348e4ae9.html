Hey Guys! Here are some of my tips for graduating early!

1. Have enough money saved up
2. Know which classes you need to graduate
3. Know when each class is offered
4. Space out your hard classes
5. Take Summer classes
6. Don't fail a class because it will set you back

College is an amazing adventure. Enjoy your time there!
