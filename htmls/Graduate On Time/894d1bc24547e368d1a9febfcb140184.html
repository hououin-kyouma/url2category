More Information - Countdown to Graduation

Bachelor’s

The average amount of time to graduate is 2 years compared to 4 years or more at most universities.

  1. Based on a student who transfers in with the equivalent of an Associate’s degree. Transfer credits will vary depending on your past college coursework
  2. Based on a student who takes 3 out of 3 semesters per year
  3. Based on a student who takes 2 courses (e.g., 6 units) per session, every session (e.g., E1 & E2)
  4. Students may be able to graduate even faster by transferring in additional credits

Accreditation

CBU Online is accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) Senior College and University Commission. Many prospective students are uncertain about which college degrees employers or universities will accept. CBU Online's accreditation by WASC means you can be confident that your CBU Online degree is recognized and respected by schools and companies across the country.

CBU Online has received various awards and has been ranked in the top 40 in the nation - best online bachelor’s degree category by U.S. News & World Report for 5 consecutive years.

Length of time to complete a degree.

According to the report, several factors contribute to the lengthy time it can take to get a degree, including insufficient course offerings, work and family obligations, and the need for students to finish remedial courses.

A student who gets a degree in six years from a Los Angeles-area community college will incur an extra $120,000 in expenses and lost wages over his or her career compared to someone who finished in two years. The median student took 4.7 years to reach the goal of an earned bachelor's degree from a Cal State campus during the 2011-12 academic year.

Benefits of Earning Your Degree

Live your purpose. Follow your passion. CBU Online is dedicated to helping you achieve your goals. Earning your degree at CBU Online is a short term investment with long term dividends.



55.9%

Increased Demand

There is an increased demand for job applicants who hold a college degree. According to Job Outlook 2015 Spring Update, more than 55.9 percent of respondents are planning to increase their individual hiring numbers.*

$110,900

Save on EDU Costs

The combination of extra educational expense and lost wages incurred for 6 year degree program vs. 4 year accelerated degree is estimated at $110,900. The average Master’s degree program at CBU Online can be completed in 1 year.*

56%

Increased Income

An individual with a bachelor’s degree earns 56% more annually than a high school graduate. A college master's degree is worth $1.3 million more in lifetime earnings than a high school diploma.*

40%

Promotion

15% of large companies offer signing bonuses and 25% will offer performance bonuses this year. 40% of the employees at large companies could be eligible for a promotion with increased income this year.*
