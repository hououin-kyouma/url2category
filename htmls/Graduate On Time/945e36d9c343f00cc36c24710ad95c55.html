Get started

To get started, choose from one of the following options listed below. Make sure to follow the admissions checklist to be certain we have everything we need to process your application.

  • Freshman Applicants: Admission Success Pathways for first-time college students start here. 
  • Transfer Applicants: If you’re transferring from another college or
       university, start here.
  • International: If you have citizenship outside of the U.S. or you do not yet hold U.S. Permanent Resident status, start here.  
  • Readmission: If you are an admitted degree-seeking student who has not enrolled in any course at the University for three (3) or more consecutive terms.
  • Non-Degree: Students who need to complete pre-requisites for another program and are currently not enrolled in a degree seeking program at FIU or want to take a few courses at FIU but not obtain a Bachelor and/or Masters degree.

Dates & Deadlines

Admissions decisions are made on a space-available basis as applications are completed and all supporting documents are recieved. In order to be invited to apply for merit scholarships you must be admitted prior to November 1 and meet minimum scholarship criteria.
