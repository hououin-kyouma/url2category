Open Days

Our Open Days give you the perfect chance to see what we're all about. You will be able to talk about the subjects you're interested in with staff and students from all of our courses. It's also an opportunity for you to take a look round our accommodation and learn more about life on our beautiful parkland campus.

International students

Join a truly international community of over 17,000 students from more than 150 countries. The University of Reading is the ideal destination if you're looking to study in the UK and achieve your academic and career goals.

Learn a language as part of your degree

Add variety to your degree course and broaden your horizons. As a student at Reading, you’ll have the chance to study one of 10 languages from beginner to advanced level through our Institution-wide Language Programme.

Every student has the option to study a foreign language alongside their degree. You can choose from a wide range of languages to learn, including Arabic, Chinese Mandarin, French, German, modern Greek, Italian, Japanese, Russian and Spanish. 

For more information, visit the Institution-Wide Language Programme (IWLP) website.
