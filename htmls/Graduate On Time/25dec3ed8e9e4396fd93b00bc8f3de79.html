First-Year Students (U.S.)

Are you excited by the idea of learning by doing? At Drake, we focus on real, hands-on projects. We take learning beyond the act of listening and put ideas into action.

Transfer Students (U.S.)

Are you thinking about coming to Drake to continue your college education? We’ll partner with you—in and out of the classroom—to ensure your Drake degree paves the way to the future you want.

  • Apply now to Drake

  • Explore more about transfer student experience at Drake

Are you thinking about coming to the United States to begin your college education? Students from 40 U.S. states and 46 countries have chosen Drake as their educational home.

  • Apply now to Drake

Graduate Students

At Drake, we believe the right graduate degree is a credential backed by a respected university that delivers quality in every aspect of your experience.

Visit our Graduate & Professional Admission page to learn more about the 23 master’s, doctoral, and professional programs offered by Drake University.

More Ways to be a Bulldog

  • Adult learners can add a professional credential, start a second degree, or simply explore a new interest with Continuing Education at Drake.
  • Former Drake students who want to complete a degree can complete the re-enrollment process rather than a full application.
  • Iowa high school students can gain valuable academic experience and college credit before they graduate with Post-Secondary Enrollment Options.
  • Non-degree-seeking students can register for a class without pursuing a degree by contacting the Registrar's Office.
