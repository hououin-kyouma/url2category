Undergraduate Students

From science and engineering to the arts, legal studies and business administration, UB undergraduates choose from more than 110 exciting degree programs. Enjoy an outstanding education at a university ranked among the best in the nation for affordability and value. Plus, with our Finish in Four program, we’ll help you stay on schedule and graduate on time.

Graduate and Professional Students

UB is a leading center for graduate and professional studies, with 205 master’s degrees and 94 doctoral and professional degrees. Our students collaborate one-on-one with faculty mentors in state-of-the-art research facilities. Admission requirements vary by program.

International Undergraduate Students

UB has one of the most diverse student communities in the United States.  At UB, you will meet engaged students from around the world who will help you feel at home, world-renowned professors dedicated to helping you learn and grow, and programs designed to prepare you for success before and after graduation.

2018 Winter Session

Study here — or anywhere in the world — and earn UB credit. Enroll in UB's three-week winter session and catch up, get ahead or pursue a special interest. Online and on-campus courses begin Jan. 4, 2018. Registration opens Oct. 2.
