Look for Jobs and Internships

Ready to build your skills with an internship or put your skills to use with a job? Check out the career resources available to you below.

Jobs


You’ve got the training. Now you’re ready for the job. Check out the resources below to help get into gear.

TCC Job Fair
We make networking a breeze with TCC job fairs. Held every Spring and Fall semester in the Student Union Ballroom, you can visit www.tccfuturelink.com for event details and a list of registered employers.

FutureLink
FutureLink gives you access to TCC’s online job board, resume builder, virtual mock interview system, and more. Register with FutureLink​ to create a professional résumé and search jobs exclusive to TCC students and alumni.

Job Board
Head to the Career Center lobby to find postings on our job board.

Glassdoor
Get Hired.  Love Your Job.
Glassdoor allows you to search millions of job listings, see real employee salaries & read company reviews from employees.  Check it out here.

Hot Jobs!
Want to be a trend setter? View the hottest occupations in the state of Florida and Big Bend region. Click here for more information.

Networking to Land the Job!
Over 80% of jobs are found through professional networking. Get plugged into the following:

  • Access Tallahassee 
  • Big Bend Paralegal Association 
  • Business and Professional Women/Tallahassee  
  • Florida Surveying and Mapping Society 
  • Tallahassee Chamber of Commerce 
  • TalTech Alliance 
  • Network for Young Professionals 
  • Refresh Tallahassee
  • Network of Entrepreneurs and Business Advocates- Tallahassee & Leon
  • Big Bend Society for Human Resource Managers
  • Association of Government Accountants
  • Florida Public Relations Association Capital Chapter
  • American Advertising Federation Tallahassee
  • Design Week Tallahassee
  • Women Who Mean Business (Tallahassee Woman Magazine)
  • Capital City .NET Users Group

LinkedIn can also provide powerful opportunities to network and find jobs not posted publicly.  Create an account and check out the LinkedIn Job Search Checklist . 

TCC Campus Employment

  • For information on work-study, contact the TCC Financial Aid Dept. at (850) 201-8399.
  • Explore available employment opportunities at TCC. Visit the employment page or contact Human Resources at (850) 201-8510 to learn more about openings.

Internships

Looking to score some hands-on, real-world experience? Browse internship openings and connect with potential employers throuhg the TCC Internship Program.

Secure your internship:

  • Complete the online internship application in FutureLink.
  • Submit a current resume.
  • Meet with an internship coordinator to discuss your goals.
  • Interview and land the internship.

TCC’s Internship Spotlights gives a quick glimpse of student success stories. For more information, contact us at (850) 201-9971 or email the program.
