THIS IS THE WHOLE POINT OF THE PASSION PROFILE QUIZ:

This quiz gauges the ideal relationship between your career and your passion in life.

In other words, it tells you how to best tie what you do for a living to something you’re passionate about so that no matter what, your career & life feel fulfilling, energizing, and inspiring to you.

There are 4 possible outcomes: Firestarter, Tribe Member, Side Hustler, and Thriver.

Think of these 4 Passion Profiles like the 4 points on a compass — knowing which major direction suits you will allow you to walk the right path. And once you’re on the right path, it’s so much easier to figure out the finer details of your passion.

A QUICK FYI ABOUT TAKING THE QUIZ:

• It’s only 11 questions and should take you just a few minutes to complete.

• MOST of the questions will ask you about your ideal situation, not necessarily your current experience.

• There will be questions where you’ll feel drawn to multiple answers … just pick the answer that MOST resonates with you.

• Be honest — don’t answer what you think you “should,” just what feels right for you.

• Don’t overthink it too much! Your gut instinct will be right.

• At the end, you will be asked to enter your email address to “unlock” your results. This helps us confirm that you are, in fact, human and not spam. This does NOT automatically add you to our email list.

See you on the other side!
