About this event

In an era of automation, globalization, and disruption - how do we know what skills will be in demand and how do we learn them?

Join as General Assembly and BigTalker.io bring together this expert panel to examine how technology is impacting the workforce and how it will impact your career in the future.

Our panelists will cover which jobs are currently facing automation, which skills will be most important in the future, and how best to prepare yourself for change.

Agenda:

  • 6:30-6:45pm: Check-in, grab a seat
  • 6:45-7:30pm: Panel
  • 7:30-8:00pm: Q&A
  • 8:00-8:30pm: Wrap-up, networking

Takeaways

  • What skills will be in demand in the future
  • How to build the skills you need for future jobs
  • How companies can help their employees continue to develop professionally
