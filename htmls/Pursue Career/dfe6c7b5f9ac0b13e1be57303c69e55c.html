The secret to find your Dream Job

Isn’t it interesting that when we’re in college we have these amazing dreams about what our first job will be?

Then we graduate...and take a job doing grunt work while getting paid a quarter of what we thought we deserved.

Reality smacks us right in the face — we hate our job, we feel the energy drained out of us every day, and we come home and just want to sit on the couch and watch TV.

Days go by, months go by, even YEARS go by and we’re still stuck.

Maybe we get a raise here or there, or move to another company — but the moves are usually lateral and the raises don’t add up to much.

In the back of our mind, we wonder if there’s something better out there.

If this sounds familiar, read on. I want to share with you how I cracked the code for actually finding a job we love — and show you how you can do it too.

The game being played around us

I know how tough it can be to find our Dream Job.

We wonder…

  • When we’ll catch our lucky break
  • Why everyone else but us always gets the best jobs
  • How many more emails we have to send before a hiring manager finally notices us

It’s time-consuming, soul-crushing work.

While attending Stanford, I got sick of the standard job advice (that never seemed to actually work). Things like…

  • Adjusting the font on our resumes
  • “Shotgunning” applications out to dozens of companies (only to never hear back)
  • Applying to jobs we didn’t even really want just because we were told experience is important

So my friends and I decided to learn how to “beat” the process.

We applied to some of the best companies in the world — McKinsey, Google, Intuit, and more — and dug into our results. What do hiring managers really care about on an application? What body language “ticks” automatically disqualify you in an interview? How do you negotiate a $10,000 raise?

We ran a fine-toothed comb through EVERYTHING.

It worked — the job offers started pouring in. McKinsey. Bain. Google. Intuit. Multi-billion dollar hedge funds.

How to find your Dream Job in 8 weeks (or less)

My Find Your Dream Job course shows you step-by-step how to find a job you LOVE — and get paid what you deserve. No more shotgunning your resume out 100x a day, just hoping something will happen. No more Googling for random tactics, crossing your fingers that one of them will finally work.

Instead, you’ll get a solid, proven system that’s worked for thousands of my students — and can work for you too.

Each week for 8 weeks, you’ll get an email right in your inbox that will tell you exactly what you to do.

A roadmap of what to expect every week.

Here’s just a fraction of what you’ll learn each week:

Week 1: The Uncommon Roadmap to Finding Your Dream Job. Starting with the biggest mental barriers you’ll face when starting...and how to overcome them.

Week 2: Discover Your Dream Job. Learn how to “laser in” on potential Dream Jobs — without wasting time researching jobs that you’ll hate.

Week 3: Discover Your Dream Company. We'll cover the “Pipeline Technique” so you can have a constant flow of Dream Job opportunities (you pick the one you want).

Week 7: World-Class Interviewing, including the #1 mistake people make in interviews and what the interviewer secretly wants to discover.
