Search For Internships & Jobs

The most effective way to identify potential opportunities is through networking. Use our Networking Contact Log to keep track of your contacts. Purple Briefcase, LinkedIn, the Internship Host Database, Vault, and online job boards are other ways to identify opportunities.

High Point University Career and Professional Development posts job listings on Purple Briefcase for the convenience of students.  It is the responsibility of students to perform due diligence in researching employers when applying for or accepting private, off-campus employment and to thoroughly research the facts and reputation of each organization to which they are applying. Students should be prudent and use common sense and caution when applying for or accepting any position.

Exclusively for HPU students and alumni, Purple Briefcase is an online internship/job board. This tool allows students and alumni to access full-time, part-time, internship and volunteer opportunities posted directly by the employer or through Career and Professional Development.

NOTE: Please LOGIN using your HPU email address and password (the same username and password that you use to access Blackboard, Webmail, etc). You DO NOT need to register for an account; your information is already in the system. If you have difficulty logging in please contact Career and Professional Development.

LinkedIn is the world’s largest professional networking tool. You can use LinkedIn to connect to professionals in organizations that interest you, HPU alumni, and actual job postings. LinkedIn is also a great tool for researching the job market within specific fields and geographic locations.

Search millions of jobs from thousands of job boards, newspapers, classifieds and company websites on indeed.com

 

Search all jobs at Google. Do cool things that matter. On Google Careers you can search, find, and apply to the opportunities that are the right fit for you at Google.

 

 

WayUp is an online platform for finding jobs and internships. Students can sign up for free and gain access to job postings from companies who are hiring interns and full-time employees.

 

 Locate and research companies that you want to work for. ReferenceUSA has details on 14 million US businesses including 200 million human resource contact names, business descriptions, company size, and website links to job postings.
