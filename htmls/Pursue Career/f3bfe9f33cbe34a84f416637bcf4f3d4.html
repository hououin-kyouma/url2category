    Pioneer Internship

    Pioneer your journey

    Join our select group of Deloitte Pioneers, and you'll get an inside look at what it's really like to work for a professional services organization.

    The Pioneer Internship, designed for freshman level summer interns, gives interns first-hand exposure to the professional services industry, allowing you to learn new business skills and deepen your knowledge of your field of interest. During this six-week long experience, you’ll have the opportunity to foster business relationships with Deloitte professionals and acquire the critical and soft skills needed to succeed in the modern workplace and global marketplace.

    Learning, networking, and the National Internship Conference

    Beyond exposure to real life projects, the internship also delivers a variety of national and local learning and networking events. In addition, throughout the internship there are ongoing learning events such a lunch and learns, industry/service line spotlights, and regularly scheduled training offerings designed to help our interns develop technical and client service skills. The interns will understand first-hand what a career with Deloitte is like and have the opportunity to build a cross-functional professional network.

    Pioneer interns are also invited to a three-day National Intern Conference at Deloitte University in Westlake, TX during the summer months.

    Additional social activities are planned throughout the internship period to provide opportunity to network with Deloitte professionals.

    Eligibility

    • Freshman standing in a full-time academic program, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in accounting, technology, or related field
    • Strong academic performance; 3.3 cumulative GPA strongly preferred
    • Demonstrated problem-solving skills
    • Effective interpersonal and communication skills; adaptive to change; creativity & self-confidence; leadership qualities; team-oriented and collaborative

    How to apply

    Deloitte selects accomplished students from top colleges and universities across the United States, Check out the interactive campus map to find us on campus.  Please contact your campus recruiter to apply to this program.
