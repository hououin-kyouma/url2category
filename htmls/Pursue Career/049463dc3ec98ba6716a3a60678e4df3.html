A passion for perfection

What does it take to build a Rolls-Royce motor car? Pride, dedication and an unswerving commitment to perfection. When you join us at our home in Goodwood, England, you are joining some of the most talented craftspeople and engineers in the world. We are looking for people who will help shape the next chapter of our legendary story.

Our culture

From the engineering labs to the workshop floor, we are a close team united by one desire: to work together to make something truly beautiful. We live by the words of Sir Henry Royce: "Strive for perfection in everything you do."  To help you achieve your best, we provide many opportunities to develop and grow your potential. That’s what inspires people to join, and makes them want to stay.

Benefits

Along with a competitive salary and bonus scheme, we offer an outstanding benefits package. A generous workplace pension, 26 days’ annual leave, childcare vouchers, occupational health services, an exclusive employee discounts scheme and a Rolls-Royce wedding car are just some of the benefits available to all permanent employees.


As part of the BMW Group, you have the option to run vehicles at a significantly reduced cost. You have access to a range of brand new premium cars and motorcycles via competitive vehicle scheme options. You can also explore the options to develop your career within a global group of highly-respected companies.

Who we are looking for

We offer a wide range of careers at Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, including engineers, specialist artisans, HR professionals, marketeers and sales managers. We look for hard-working team players who are responsible, honest and able to be creative when faced with a challenge. Hand building the world’s most iconic marque requires a flawless attention to detail, which should follow through in everything you do.

Start your career here

We are paving the way for the next generation of engineers and craftspeople. Whether you are a recent graduate or looking to learn skills on the job, start your career at one of the world’s leading car companies with our entry-level programmes and schemes.

Apprenticeships

If you are about to leave school and are passionate about cars, training with us as an apprentice is a rewarding and hands-on route into an automotive career. We are searching for talented and enthusiastic people who are ready to take on real responsibility and learn from the best in the industry.  

Apprenticeships last between three and four years  and are based at our home in Goodwood. Courses on offer include Business (Foundation Degree), Engineering Technician (HNC Level 4), and Modern Manufacturing (NVQ Level 3), which will provide you with essential skills in areas such as leather, wood and lacquering, paint, and electro-mechanical maintenance engineering.

Apprenticeships usually start in September, and we accept applications from December until the end of January.

Internships

Our paid internships immerse you in the working culture and processes at Goodwood from the ground up, as well as providing you with training from some of the best in the industry.

As an intern, you will enjoy hands-on involvement in a number of projects from day one. The areas you can gain experience in include Manufacturing, Engineering, Quality, Logistics, IT, Planning, Finance, Marketing, Sales and Communications. 

Internships are open to third-year university students and last between 6 and 12 months. We offer a highly competitive salary and a range of benefits, including 26 days’ holiday.

Graduate schemes

We are looking for graduates to bring talent, drive and fresh thinking to Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. In return, you will feel all the pride that comes with designing the world’s leading luxury motor cars.


Over the course of the two-year programme, you will get the chance to shape your career by working across different areas of the business with the chance to work around the world. You will be able to make a real impact from day one, all with the full support and guidance of senior mentors.


You can enjoy a highly competitive salary package with a range of benefits, including access to car schemes, pension, performance-related bonus and 26 days’ holiday.
