Career Guides by Major: What can you do with your major or degree?

Main Content

Explore how to connect majors, minors or concentrations to specific career paths by diving into our What Can I Do With a Major In guides. Learn how to apply studies to specific career areas, discover employers you could potentially be working alongside, gain job search strategies for breaking into particular fields, and more. Remember, your major does not always determine your career; you can use your education in a variety of fields and industries.

Understand that these guides provide a sampling of what you can do with your degree; these guides are not meant to provide a comprehensive list. Gain a more in-depth understanding of how majors connect to careers by meeting with your career advisor.
