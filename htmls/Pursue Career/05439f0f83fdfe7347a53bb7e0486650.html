"How to Be a Successful Musician" is a channel aimed towards discussing all the music related topics relevant to every aspiring musician and music enthusiast.

In this first episode I urge you to tap into your subconsciousness to figure out what music really means to you.

WARNING: This video contains a 10 minute meditation!

If you found this video helpful/motivational, I would also recommend you to:

1) Write "I AM ENOUGH" on your mirror (preferably with lipstick/crayons or other easily removable colours and on the top part of the mirror).
- Give it a few weeks and your subconsciousness will start to relate this affirmation to the image you have of yourself.
- You can also come up with affirmations of your own (IKEA has done exactly that: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W30-H...)
- Comment below what you wrote or share the photo of your mirror artwork with the rest of us, as I would really like to know if this technique works for you (I am currently testing it).


2) Watch the awesome dream speech from "The Pursuit Of Happiness":
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xt6mk...
Will Smith says it all!

Last but not least: Enjoy gathering inspiration and inspiring the people around you :-)
