4 Free Aptitude Tests to Find Your Perfect Career

Deciding what you want to pursue in your professional career can be pretty challenging. While some people use their interests and abilities to figure out what they want to pursue while still at a young age, others have a very hard time figuring out what they want to do in their lives. At the same time, even people who know what they want to pursue at a young age typically start studying and pursuing their dreams only to find out that may not be what they wanted to do after all. Career aptitude tests are designed to assess your skills and interests to determine what type of careers and professions match you. And luckily, nowadays, there are career aptitude tests online that are free of charge to help you determine what to pursue for your professional goals. In fact, an article posted on TradeMartes talked about how especially helpful career aptitude tests are and some of the ways that they work. Here, we have compiled 4 aptitude tests that are free, reliable, and available online.

  • CareerPath.com Career Test: CareerPath offers tests that apply to a number of individuals to help them determine what careers will suit them best. There are different tests for individuals at different places in their lives, and all of these offer individuals the opportunity to find a job that suits them. In addition, there are tests for those who are considering career changes, and tests for those who are interested in finding out more about their current job and attributes.
  • CareerColleges.com Free Career Assessment Test: This free assessment consists of 15 questions to help identify the best careers based on your habits, thoughts, and actions. The questions measure your feelings on decision making, work environments, the form in which you learn and work better, and whether or not you are detail-oriented. The test also covers what your methods are for working on new assignments, your ability to communicate verbally, your ability to compute numbers in your head, and your artistic form.
  • Quest Career Services Career Test: This career tests asks questions on your motivations, interests, work method preferences, and talents to determine possible career paths. The tests also measure your strengths to decide what jobs match them and gives a review of the results of your test. Additionally, it helps you to learn more about your motivations and preferences as they relate to your professional and career needs.
  • Similiar Minds Career Test: This test is compiled of 30 questions to evaluate the type of emotions and actions you generally feel in order to measure what types of jobs will be most convenient for your personality. The test measures your cleanliness, organizational skills, the order in which you place your needs, and your ability to work under pressure. Other questions on the test include those to measure the way you deal with your emotions, how you feel in certain environments, and how social you are.
