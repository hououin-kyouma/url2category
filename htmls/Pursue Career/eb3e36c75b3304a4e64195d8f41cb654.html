Career Quiz: Personality Test

The Open Colleges Career Quiz is designed to give you an understanding of your personality and the career areas that you would be best suited to. It takes less than 5 minutes!

We consider this quiz to the first step of an honest and thorough approach to finding a suitable career. The quiz gives you suggestions for what career areas you might like. Your second step should involve having a look through information about that career at the Open Colleges Careers Advice site. This includes salary expectations, employment outlooks, typical hours, etc.
