What Do I Want To Do With My Life?

Do you feel lost, confused or uncertain about your career?If you are looking for clarity and direction, you have come to the right place. This six-week intensive career course was designed especially for people who want to get on a career path that supports their passion and lifestyle preferences. To be sure you are the right candidate for this course, consider the following questions:

  • Are you unhappy in your current job?
  • Do you feel unclear about what next step to take in your career?
  • Are you struggling to find a job or any sense of direction?
  • Are you unsure of what you really want?
  • Are you comparing yourself to friends who seem more successful?
  • Do you hate your job but stay anyway because you feel you should?
  • Are you questioning a career path that you thought you would love?
  • Do you want a career that gives you a better lifestyle?

If you answered “yes” to more than one of these questions, then this course is for you. You are at a crossroads in your career path; don’t worry it’s not a dead end or a wrong way sign. Completing the reading and exercises in this course will support you in navigating your next steps.

Any kind of lasting change requires change on a mental, emotional, and behavioral level so this course will address all three. The exercises and stories in this course will help you discover and overcome roadblocks in regards to your career and clarify your unique skills. Completing this course will not give you instant answers, nor will it fill your mind with empty promises and platitudes such as “there’s always a silver lining.” This course does require work and an investment of your time and focus. It won’t be the sitting in a cubicle from 9 to 5 type of work. It will involve self-reflection, honesty, planning, commitment, and follow-through.

If you take the time to read these stories, do these exercises, and really evaluate what you have learned, by the end of the six weeks you will be well on your way to knowing and going after what you want to do with your life.Here’s what I found on my own journey: I kept looking for the job that would complete me or make me happy. And I kept thinking that I would find it through the traditional routes of sending out resumes, networking and searching on line. But what I now know to be true is that discovering our true calling cannot be found in those traditional ways! Rather we have to do internal work and reflection so that our external work becomes more of a reflection of who we truly are and what we truly want! Once we make this shift, the results are incredible. The most common obstacle is we do not know how to make this shift.

The most common mistakes in implementing this program is to skip over exercises, do the exercises in your head rather than in a journal, or get behind on the course work. If you invest 100% into each exercise, set the intention of clarity and direction, and complete each exercise in written form, you will have profound “aha’s” and clear next steps. I’m so confident in the results you will experience that I offer a money back guarantee. That’s right, if you are not fully satisfied with the course, I will refund your money.

I acknowledge you for your willingness to take an empowering step toward your dreams and goals!
