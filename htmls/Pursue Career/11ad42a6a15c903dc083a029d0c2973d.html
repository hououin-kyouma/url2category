If you are looking forward to join your dream company, this video will be helpful in attaining it. This is a Tamil video. In this video I explain the nitty gritties of finding and getting your dream job.

Connect with me on Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/lavanyajayak...

The 10 Expert Tips To Get Your Dream Job:

1. Analyze your passion and come to an understanding about your dream job.
2. Learn your current Skill Gap (Softskills and hardskills) and device a plan to bridge the gap.
3. Network with people who are already working in your dream job/dream company.
4. The 2 ways to get your dream job
5. The importance of bypassing the gatekeepers at your dream company and HOW TO DO IT
6. How to rock the interview by giving a big picture
7. Over promise, over commit and over deliver
8. The importance of working while studying college
9. What not to say during your job interview
10. The biggest mistake in salary negotiation at your dream company.
