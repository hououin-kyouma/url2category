The Passion Finder: How to Find Your Dream Job

My List Share Tweet Share

When:
Fri 30 Sep 2016, 4:00pm–7:00pm

Where: InterContinental Wellington, 2 Grey St, Wellington

Restrictions: All Ages

Ticket Information:

  • Dream Job Ticket: $49.00

  • Additional fees may apply

Website:

  • Find Your Dream Job; Unbox Your Gift

Listed by: ritajoyan

Title: Turn Your Career Around: How to Find Your Dream Job.

3 costly mistakes professionals make that leave them stuck, unfulfilled and un-valued in their job!

For professional women who feel frustrated, stuck or bored in their job/career, who want to finally find their dream job and make their difference but struggle with working out what it is or how to get there!

Special guest: Rita Joyan, Canberra’s 2015 Young Business Woman of The Year. Founder of The Dream Job Blueprint and the Signature Speaking Success System.

Are you tired of dreading Mondays? Join Rita Joyan and discover how to find your dream job using your ‘own life story’, gain the clarity of finding your next best move without the guesswork and boost your confidence and focus without doubting yourself and give voice to your brilliance.

In this action-packed session you’ll learn:
- What a dream job is and the 3 unknown facts within the workforce that will inspire you to find your dream job without feeling guilty (this will surprise you).

- The mindset behind your dream job, including a simple, strategy you can use right away to instantly set you on your path towards your dream job.

- The 3 keys to working out your own life story to gain insight into your dream job, (no, it’s not one size fits all!)

- And most important, the map, how you can get crystal clear on your own dream job, gain the confidence, clarity, and know-how to make your dream into a reality with your very own strategy to pinpoint your Dream Job.

Someone once said that whenever you see the dates of someone’s life the most important part is the dash in the middle. What did they do to fill the dash? To live life knowing you have more potential to tap into but not know what that God-given gift/passion is frustrating. You are here for a reason. Work out that reason, give birth to your passion, ignite your purpose and make that dash count.

If you're tired of feeling bored, unchallenged or stuck in your job, and you know moving from job to job isn't the answer, do whatever you can to get yourself to this event.

If you're ready to learn what your passion is and turn it into a dream job, book now - not much time left until this event or until the clock strikes 2017!
