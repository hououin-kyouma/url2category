    How to Find Your Purpose

    January 5, 2014

    Next video

    share this video:

    •  

    share this video:

    •  

    Hi Spirit Junkies!

    In my lectures and workshops I often hear people complain that they don’t know what their purpose is. They feel confused, disconnected to the world and misdirected. Does that sound familiar? It can be very difficult to walk around feeling purposeless.

    In this video blog I share tips on how to stop searching for your purpose and instead allow your purpose to find you.

    In response to their discomfort I see many people pushing and controlling their actions to show themselves and the world that they are worthy, powerful and helpful. The ego makes us think that our purpose is outside of ourselves hidden behind a credential, a job or an altruistic mission. This outside search for purpose can lead to a lot of inner turmoil.
