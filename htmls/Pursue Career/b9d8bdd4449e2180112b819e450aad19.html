Understanding Your Financial Needs
Our Lifestyle Assessment will determine what salary you should aim to earn to support your lifestyle. This salary figure, along with the results of your Career Assessment, allows us to pinpoint career recommendations for your individual lifestyle and career interests.
Find The Careers That Interest You
If you spend a few minutes with our Career Assessment, we’ll make specific recommendations for careers that match your interests and personality profile. Once you have taken our Lifestyle Assessment, we'll even assess the likelihood that a given career can financially support your lifestyle.
Gain Unique Insights Into Every Kind Of Job
We have built a video library of 2,600+ informational interviews to help you gain unique insights and honest advice from real people who hold those jobs.
Get In-Depth Information About Each Career
We put more information than you can imagine about every job at your fingertips, including localized salary data, education requirement, major recommendation, projected job growth, work environment, number of job opportunities in each metro area and much more.
Find Careers For Your Major
Ever wondered what you could do with your College Major? Our major database is not just showing you all relevant information about each major, it tells you the potential careers you can pursue after you earn that degree. You may see careers that you have never thought about before.
Plot Your Education Path
You can explore the best schools for your career path or course of study with our powerful College Search tool. We help you to evaluate each school by providing you with a comprehensive report that includes detailed admissions data, average test scores, availability of financial aid, and more.
Build Your Resume
Our highly intuitive and user-friendly Resume Builder allows you to create your first resume in under 10 minutes. We have a guided process with tips and ideas to help you from start to finish. We also offer a range of resume templates that will make your resume stand out from the crowd. Finally, you can use the resume to apply for jobs on our job board right away!
Attend a Job Fair
We have the largest database of job fairs in America. You can browse all upcoming job fairs and save the ones you are interested in attending. When the date nears, we will send you a little friendly reminder so you will never miss a job fair again. We also offer this feature on Apple Watch.
Find Your Next Job
Our Job Board lets you browse and apply for new job opportunities in the careers you want to pursue. Thousands of new jobs are added every day. You can also subscribe to receive “New Job Posting” notifications with a set of customizable criteria.
