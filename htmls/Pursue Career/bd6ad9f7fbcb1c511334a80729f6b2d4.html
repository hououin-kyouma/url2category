Career Path Coaching

Feeling lost or confused about your career path is a real downer
We can help you with that.

We created our one-on-one style career path coaching to
• Help people with big hearts and big ideas to re-gain their sense of excitement, confidence and freedom about their career choices
• Meet your unique set of needs
• Provide top notch support (our programs have been expertly designed by psychology qualified and experienced Career Development Professionals)

Here’s how it works…

Our introductory career path coaching package will help you to:
• Identify work tasks you might find satisfying
• Discover work environments that would be a good fit
• Reveal specific occupations and career fields you might enjoy
• Access ways to alter your work environment to make it more satisfying
• Consider leisure activities to balance your work life
• Access strategies for career development
• Generate tactics for staying motivated during the career exploration process.

Here’s what you can expect…

Within one business day of booking and submitting payment
We’ll send you an email with:
• access to two scientifically robust online career assessments valued at $125
• a pre-coaching form which you can get started on straight away
• confirmation of the time and date of your introductory session
• directions to our office (only applicable for face-to-face sessions)
• a request for you to send through your resume (if you have one…it doesn’t have to be up to date)
• a request for you to send through your LinkedIn URL (if you have one…it doesn’t have to be up to date)

During the introductory session we will:
• Increase your confidence and intrinsic motivation over a one and a half hour session valued at $250
• Identify your career exploration goals
• Tap into the strengths and resources which have enabled you to achieve so far
• Discover the opportunities you have in front of you and what you’d like to do next
• Focus on solutions, giving your attention mainly to ‘how you can move forward’
• Develop an understanding of your current ‘stage of change’
• Receive two reports from the online assessments you completed prior to the first session

At the end of the introductory session:
• You’ll be armed with vital information to kick-start your career path exploration process
• We’ll have a discussion about whether you would benefit from a two or three month coaching package, or whether you’re good to go

It’s all very flexible. Nothing is set in stone.

Plus, you’ll receive ongoing support over the two weeks following your introductory session:
• We’ll email you with a list of additional resources that may be useful
• We’ll be available to you via email

You could spend hours, weeks or MONTHS! (We personally know people who have spent years!)
trying to do this by yourself by endlessly pondering and ruminating, searching in your mind for answers, scrolling through the internet or waiting for an epiphany to hit you out of the blue.

Or you can let us help. We LOVE this stuff.

Through the introductory package alone, you’ll be calmer, clearer and ready to move forward with renewed confidence and new knowledge about yourself and a way forward.
