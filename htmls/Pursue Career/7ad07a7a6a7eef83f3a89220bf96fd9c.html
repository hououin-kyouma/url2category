Whether you’re looking for an internship, wondering about a career, or just gathering information, the Center for Careers and Internships is the place to start. From your first-year, to your last semester, and beyond, we are here to help. Let’s explore what’s next!

Introducing Handshake (formerly MOJO)

Complete your profile and begin discovering internships, jobs, organizations, and events, all personalized to your specific career interests. NOTE: Handshake is available for Middlebury College undergraduate students only.

Explore your career options

Find out what’s possible and how to make it happen. Start from the beginning or review the Career Path pages most relevant to your interests:

  • Arts, Media, and Communications
  • Education
  • Finance, Consulting, and Business
  • Government, Law, and Policy
  • Health Professions
  • Science and Technology
  • Social Impact

Make an appointment

Our advisors each specialize in different fields so you can choose who to meet with depending on your interests—or make an exploratory appointment with any of them if you’re not sure what you’re interested in yet.

Internships

Learn how to conduct your internship search for summer or winter term—plus find out about opportunities for funding.

Tools, templates, and resources

Get help with résumés and cover letters, find guides for how to ace an interview and network, and more.
