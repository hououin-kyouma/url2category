Your Skills. Your Future.

We believe that Career and Technical Education allows students the opportunity to hone their skills in order to prepare them for success in postsecondary education and future career opportunities.

Ranging from STEM to Advanced Manufacturing to Machining and more, classes are available to students in Cleveland and Bradley County that provide hands-on, real-world training and experiences to prepare the next generation workforce for success.
