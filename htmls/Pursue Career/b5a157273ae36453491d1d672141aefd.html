Macy’s Internships

Find Your Career Fit

It’s difficult to know if a retail career is a good fit for you until you try it on for size. With a Macy’s internship, you’ll get that chance.

At Macy’s, we offer a range of internship programs for a variety of majors and interests. Each is tailored to specific organizational positions and career paths, providing you with in-depth knowledge, hands-on experience and interaction with senior leadership. More importantly, regardless of the business area you choose, from day one you’ll be welcomed as a contributing team member—a full participant in company operations and initiatives. We welcome your ideas.

Lastly, all Macy’s internships are paid positions offered during the summer months. So you’ll not only be learning, you’ll be earning. We believe that’s a good fit for everyone.

Store Management

In this exciting, fast-paced program you’ll get a firsthand introduction to the strategies and partnerships that make Macy’s an industry-leading retailer.
Learn More

Human Resources

As a Human Resources intern, you can be part of a people-focused effort. At the same time, learn valuable functional skills and fully participate in Macy’s overall business initiatives.
Learn More

Omni Buying / Digital Merchandising

This internship allows you to explore what it takes to assort, market, and price a merchandise category in order to drive omni-channel demand within Macy’s New York-based Merchant division.
Learn More

Omni Planning

Omni Planning interns are exposed to financial planning, localization, and distribution strategies and learn first-hand what it takes to manage a multi-channel, multi-million dollar business within Macy’s New York-based Merchant Division.
Learn More

Technology

Macy’s Technology teams develop and advance everything from groundbreaking in-store systems to the mega-volume online marketplace of our e-commerce sites.
Learn More

Product Development

Based exclusively in New York City, Product Development interns have the unique opportunity to help advance a specific new product line, gaining invaluable insight into the production cycle.
Learn More

Design

A Macy’s Design internship puts you at the forefront of corporate private brand design. With our growing collection of successful lines, that’s just where you’ll want to be.
Learn More

Finance/Accounting

Work side-by-side with our top-notch team of corporate finance teams by executing, contributing and collaborating—laying the foundation for a successful career in Corporate Finance.
Learn More

Credit and Customer Service

Our Credit and Customer Service interns explore and experience the full dynamic scope of major retail credit in a supportive, challenging and fun setting.

Internships Interview Process

Getting started with your internship at Macy’s all begins here, where you can learn about our exciting and dynamic opportunities. The next steps involve a hybrid of virtual and on-campus selections and interviews, giving you a chance to learn about your potential role at Macy’s—and giving our teams a chance to learn more about you. The conclusion? We pick the best interns for the job.
