Build Your Experience

Your experiences both inside and outside the classroom give you the skills and qualities employers want. These experiences can help you build your resume and portfolio. Here are different ways to gain experience.

  • Join student organizations and participate in extracurricular activities on campus!
  • Find out if there are research assistant opportunities on campus.
  • Look into studying abroad.
  • View this handout on how to integrate your curriculum into your career.
  • Internships are highly valuable experiences. See our internship resources page.
  • Build your portfolio with freelance work.

Develop Your Plan

Take your skills, qualities and interests, pair them with the job you want, and map out a plan that works for you.

  • Read and watch the videos in our Identify Your Skills page to learn about the skills SJSU students develop and how to market them. 
  • Make sure you did your research about employers and careers. 
  • Map out your job/internship search plan using our job search plan handout.
  • Your online presence can also help you in your job search. Read our section on using social media and your online presence.
