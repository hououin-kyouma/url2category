IT Professionals Day

27 April 2017

CELEBRATE THE PROFESSION.

Thursday April 27 2017 celebrates the role and contribution IT professionals play in helping business and society.

As part of IT Professionals Day, applaud the heroes who work in technology and strive to make life easier.

As a professional, use the day to recognise your value and support your professional development.
