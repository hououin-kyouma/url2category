Ultimately I want you to feel supported, aligned, happy and fulfilled, doing work that is deeply rewarding and allows you to make your unique difference.

WHY THIS PROGRAM IS UNIQUE

What makes the Woman on Purpose Career Alignment Program unique is the balance of professional development, personal development and spiritual development (including energy, intention and Law of Attraction).

At the core of this program is the alignment of your authentic self, your higher self, your mindset and your outer expression in the world, which includes your work and career.

It’s a beautiful collision of internal alignment processes with external planning and inspired action for tangible results.

YOUR COACH – RACHAEL PICKWORTH

Imagine you had someone who really got you…

…someone who got your work challenges, your hopes and your dreams and understood that deep down you have all the clarity, confidence, passion, power and possibility locked within you, and all you need is a little help with turning the key.

My aim is to nurture you through a powerful journey to reach a confident and empowered place of clarity of purpose…

I have always had a deep sense that I have a purpose; a unique contribution I need to make in my life through my work.

My challenge was finding and maintaining the consistent clarity I needed to stay on purpose.

I started and built a business in my 20s that brought me much acclaim. Before I was 30 I was inducted into the Australian Business Women’s Hall of Fame, was Business Bulletin’s Young Entrepreneur of the Year in 200o and we were finalists in the Telstra Small Business Awards.

I looked successful, had achieved my goals and found myself unhappy and unfulfilled. 

For some of us looking successful doesn’t mean feeling successful.

I went on to sell the business and became committed to aligning my work with my passion and purpose.

After I discovered Coaching I journeyed through Life Coach Training, Business Coach Training and Team Coach Training via Results Coaching Systems (now the Neuro Leadership Institute) and also invested in workplace training and facilitation skills.

As I worked with people I found that my strength was in colliding all of my business experience and coach training with my personal and spiritual journey, which has spanned 25 years and has included dozens of diverse programs, from Rebirthing Practitioner Training to 10-day silent retreats in Thailand to Tony Robbins’ Unleash the Giant Within.

I STRADDLE TWO WORLDS

One world is business, career development, goal setting, planning, going for it, achieving results and, quite frankly, making shit happen.

The other world is spiritual alignment, co-creation, law of attraction, surrender and allowing things to unfold.

My entire world hums along beautifully when I bring these two worlds together.

This philosophy underpins the Woman On Purpose Career Alignment Program.
