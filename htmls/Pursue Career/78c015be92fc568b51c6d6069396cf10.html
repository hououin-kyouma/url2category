Your Role as a Security Analyst

Launch your cybersecurity career with our global powerhouse of security experts and out-innovate the attackers. Leveraging global resources and next-gen tech, you'll create integrated security solutions that are tailored to each client to protect their entire value chain, end-to-end. As a Security Analyst, you’ll nurture your skills working across industries and the full spectrum of our security services. Learn from the brightest minds in security, along with our role-specific training and certification support.

Technology Development Program

Uniting technology with the latest digital methodologies and design thinking, we power clients to drive disruption and keep ahead of the digital curve. As a member of the Technology Development Program (TDP) you will take your skills to the next level through technical immersion, virtual self-paced training and hands-on job training.
