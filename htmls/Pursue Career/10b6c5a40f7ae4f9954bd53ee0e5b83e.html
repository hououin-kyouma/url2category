TERRA Helped Me Develop My Skills and Launch My Career – Now I Am in a Role I Love

By Jezabel Martinez

Posted on Sep 20, 2016

Savanah Shore graduated from high school in 2014. She spent some time at Western Washington University before deciding that she wanted to join the workforce.

Knowing that staffing agencies are well-connected to great employment opportunities, she decided that her first step would be to reach out to one. A family referral led her to TERRA.

She met with Everett Branch Manager, Patty Stoddard. Patty was immediately impressed with Savanah’s eagerness to work and her willingness to take on any opportunity. Because she was also comfortable commuting, the Kirkland office was able to partner with the Everett office to help Savanah find the right fit.

Thanks to her flexibility, the Everett and Kirkland teams were able to place Savanah on a variety of contract assignments, which helped develop Savanah’s skills and experience. Not only did Savanah’s temp assignments go well, they were often extended because the clients wanted to keep her on board a while longer. She even worked for both TERRA’s Everett and Kirkland offices as a temporary Office Coordinator and Recruiting Coordinator!

Yevon Yoo, Kirkland Recruiter, was able to help find Savanah a temp-to-hire role as a sales assistant with a smaller start-up company. And the client loved her so much that they hired her on as a full-time regular employee!

“My experience with TERRA was fantastic!” Savanah shared. “It gave me employment history and opportunities I would not have had otherwise. Working internally with TERRA, I got to see how the business is on both sides and I learned that TERRA looks for the right fit for their candidates and their clients. TERRA really shows that they care about their candidates, employees and clients.”

She added, “I would like to thank Patty Stoddard. She helped me get my foot in the door with all of the opportunities TERRA had to offer. Cristal Olberg – for introducing me to TERRA. Morgan Mason and Yevon Yoo – for being great support systems and mentors. And thank you to everyone else I worked with!”

We are so glad that we were able to help Savanah kick off her career and that she is now in a role she loves and that allows her to learn and grow.

Looking to start your professional career? Or looking for the next step on your career path? We can help. Get started today by looking at some of our current openings.

We have offices in the Seattle-Puget Sound, Portland-Metro, and Phoenix-Metro areas and our expert recruiters are waiting to hear from you. Let us help you find the right fit!
