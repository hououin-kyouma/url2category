    Connect to HarrisLink

    HarrisLink is a comprehensive, searchable database where thousands of interesting and meaningful jobs, internships, and fellowships are posted for Harris students and alumni. New opportunities are posted daily, and students/alumni can establish ongoing searches that sort listings by geographical location, policy area, position title, or type of organization. The database includes comprehensive listings where students/alumni can research employers, while employers can search the student and alumni resumes posted within HarrisLink. Upon request, Career Development Office staff will compile resume books for employers—so having an approved, up-to-date resume in HarrisLink is very important.
