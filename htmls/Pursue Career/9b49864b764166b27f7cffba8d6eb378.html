Jobs and Internships

Good student jobs and internships are not always easy to find. The Alumni & Career recruiting portal  offers a selection of attractive jobs outside of the university. Companies utilize the portal to advertise student jobs, internships and opportunities for students to complete their bachelor's and master's theses. In addition, various TUM institutes and department chairs place ads for student jobs, internships and academic theses positions on the "bulletin board".

TUM Career Service

Interested in earning extra money? Here you can find all of the important TUM job portals. Image: Andreas Heddergott

Students can find attractive jobs outside the university through the TUM recruiting portal. The jobs can be sorted by key words, profession, location, company or country.

  • Part-time jobs
  • Student jobs
  • Internships
  • Theses and dissertations
  • Full-time jobs

The recruiting portal is just one of the many employment placement services offered by the Alumni & Career

Bulletin Board

Are you looking for a student job or internship at TUM? Many institutes and department chairs offer the opportunity to become involved in research projects and activities. You can find a wide selection of available jobs on our bulletin board.

If you can't find anything suitable, check out the Garching campus bulletin board:

Munich Student Union Job Portal

The Munich Student Union operates its own job placement service in conjunction with "Die Job Börse," a private placement agency. The service is located in the cafeteria of the Ludwig-Maximilians University.
The Job-Börse handles placements for students, graduate student internships, student jobs and part-time or full-time positions. This free service is available to all job-seekers. Companies can pay a small fee to fill vacant positions. More

Labor laws for international students

International students from countries outside the European Union or European Economic Area may work in Germany on a restricted basis only. In contrast, most European students have unrestrained access to the job market, essentially putting them on equal footing with German students.
The student union provides a comprehensive overview of labor laws for international students.  More
