Free Career Assessment

Trying to figure out your best career path? One that matches your likes and desires. Fill out our Free Online Career Assessment tool and find out.

Step one is to take our free online career assessment to match the best career training program for you. It will provide you with detailed results, and a Mildred Elley Admissions Representative will contact you to help you understand your results and help you with your next steps.

Please note, this is a very detailed career assessment, so make sure to choose a user name and password that you will remember since you may not finish it all in one sitting!
