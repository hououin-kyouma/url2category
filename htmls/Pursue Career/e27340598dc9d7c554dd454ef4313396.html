How to find your dream job

Story by Nadine El Dada
Posted on Jun. 14, 2015
Share

Looking for a fulfilling career? Don’t know where to start?

Take a look at the list below to help you make a list of potential career jobs.

It’s simple to find a career that you are passionate about…

Follow these tips to find your dream job:

1. Outline your values

Take some time do a self-assessment on: your values, beliefs, goals and interests.

Which industry interests you? What kind of work would you like to do?

Would you still work even if you don’t get paid?

The mistake most job seekers make is not being able to identify what job they want.

 2. Slowly build experience

Don’t be worried whether or not you are experienced enough.

Ever heard of the famous idiom: ‘Rome was not built in a day’?

It means that time is needed to create great things and this can apply to one’s career too!

3. Don’t rely on the first step alone

Don’t rely on the resume or the interview alone.

It’s a great first step but always remember performance says it all.

Make sure your portfolio illustrates who you are and your accomplishments.

4.  Be flexible

Did you know being flexible is one of the most sought out characteristics in candidates?

Try to be flexible and open to new opportunities that come your way.

Adapt to change, educate yourself and be a swift learner.

5. Remove outside influences/obstacles

 

Don’t be drawn into a job solely because of the money or title; you won’t be satisfied in the long-run.

Don’t pursue a job simply because someone told you to and you feel the need to please them.

Pick a job that you are passionate about and you are great at doing.

6. Stand out

There are a number of practices to help you stand out – from building a strong influential network to mastering your skills and delivering strong results.

Search for the best techniques to stand out in your industry.

Whether you are a student looking for a job or someone who is looking for a fresh beginning; find the time to discover the career path that is right for you!

It’s time to eliminate any obstacles and start your journey in discovering your dream job!
