In the fashion industry, having mentor is one of the best ways to open up any door. It allows you to take charge of your future and learn from someone who has already made a name for himself or herself. Still, finding a mentor is difficult because everyone else is already looking for one as well.

Remember that this is the person you can go to when things get rough or you don’t understand something. Whether it is a question you have about something you say in the course or something about the trends you are seeing today. The only limit is what you can think about.
