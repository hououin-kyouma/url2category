How Do You Decide on a Career Path?

27 January 2017 by Guest Author

There are more than 12,000 different job titles, from animator to zoo keeper. With so much to choose from, which career path should you pursue?

Career Savvy have the answer – take a look in the infographic below!

Takeaways:

  • 75% of students change their degree subject at least once and 35% of students don’t know what they want to do after university.
  • Start out with properly assessing your skills, for instance you can do some online research and take some career quizzes.
  • Volunteering, work experience, internships and online courses can all help you to see whether you’ll enjoy a subject at the next level, as well as being great for your career development.
  • Have the confidence to take control of your career, and you’ll be one step closer to finding a job that’s perfect for you.
