Bloomingdale’s Internships

Find Your Career Fit

Want to test drive a career in retail? There’s no better place to get on track than an internship with Bloomingdale’s.

We offer a range of internship programs, each tailored to specific positions and career paths within our organization. All our internships are designed to provide a combination of hands-on education and experience along with classroom training.

You’ll experience a focused area of the business, have exposure to senior leadership and participate in company initiatives, including community involvement. Internships are paid positions offered during the summer months in our New York City locations.

Store Management

Be part of the exciting behind-the-scenes action that takes place within our upscale department stores to make the customer shopping experience like no other.
Learn More

Omni Buying & Planning

Gain hands-on corporate buying and planning experiences during our 8-week internship located in our headquarters in Midtown Manhattan.
Learn More

Analyst

Gain hands-on analyst/financial experiences during our 8-week internship located in our headquarters in Midtown Manhattan.
