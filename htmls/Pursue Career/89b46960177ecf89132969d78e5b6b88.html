Stand out with Career Readiness Bootcamp

Nine out of ten employers say they need employees with soft skills. Invest in yourself with our Career Readiness Bootcamp, and learn communication skills, planning and organization, productivity tools, decision-making, and teamwork — the skills you need to stand out in any job.

Why Do I Need Soft Skills?

Whether you’re currently a student, on the hunt for a job, or working in your field, our online Career Readiness Bootcamp was created for you. Companies across the U.S. say it is becoming increasingly difficult to find applicants who can communicate clearly, take initiative, problem-solve, and get along with co-workers.* Show employers you’ve got what it takes by taking the next step toward becoming desirable in today’s workplace and unlocking career advancement opportunities.
Curriculum
Learn 9 Valuable Soft Skills at Your Own Pace

Communication Skills

Active Listening, Non-Verbal Communication, Receiving Feedback, Communication Channel Etiquette

Problem Solving/ Decision Making

Identifying Problems, Decision Making Tools and Strategies

Planning and Organization

Creating and Managing Plans, Prioritizing, Time Management

Working with Tools/Technology

Using Technology to Solve Problems, Common Technology Tools and Applications, Accepting New Technology

Professionalism/ Adaptability

Barriers to Change, Adjusting to Changing Priorities and Work Conditions

Teamwork

Being a Team Player, Relationship Building/Cooperation, Conflict Resolution

Customer Focus

Anticipating and Delivering on Customer Needs; Evaluating Service Effectiveness

Respect

Appreciation for Diversity, Tolerance, and Multicultural Awareness

Integrity

Honesty, Responsibility, and Reliability; “Doing the right thing” and “Keeping your promises"
