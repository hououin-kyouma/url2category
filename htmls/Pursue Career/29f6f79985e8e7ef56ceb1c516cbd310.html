Accenture Emerging Technologist Development Programme

Overview

DOWNLOAD VIDEO TRANSCRIPT (PDF)

Are you an aspiring software engineer ready to create the technologies of the future? Apply for the Accenture Emerging Technologist Development Programme, a one-year learning initiative that gives you the chance to solve complex technology problems for leading global businesses and governments. You will also gain the skills and certifications you need to embark on a global career with Accenture.

DOWNLOAD A COPY OF OUR POLYTECHNIC E-BROCHURE AND LEARN MORE ABOUT THE CAREER OPPORTUNITIES AVAILABLE FOR YOU [PDF]

Click here to learn how the Accenture Emerging Technologist Development Programme (ETDP) supports Singapore’s SkillsFuture movement

The Singapore SkillsFuture Earn and Learn Programme for Software Development aims to train fresh information and communications technology (ICT) graduates from polytechnic schools for work in emerging areas such as Big Data, business analytics and cloud computing. It was developed by the Singapore Workforce Development Agency (WDA) and Nanyang Polytechnic (NYP) to support Singapore’s future as an ICT hub and help fuel the country’s socioeconomic growth.

In line with the SkillsFuture movement and as a company committed to training and developing people, Accenture has developed the ETDP. The initiative will provide participants with specialised on-the-job training in areas including design thinking, Agile software development and advanced information systems. Anyone who wants to start or switch to a technology career with Accenture is welcome to join the ETDP.

"The ETDP reflects our ongoing commitment to help develop the skills of our Singapore workforce, especially in the areas of emerging technology. We are confident that this programme, with the support of our partners, will be successful in providing a 'talent on tap model' for entry-level technology hires,” says Lay Lim Teo, senior managing director ‒ ASEAN, and Country Managing Director ‒ Singapore.

My1stDegree Study Grant

  • Study Grants
  • Advantages
  • Framework
  • Eligibility

Accenture encourages and enables students to pursue and build a career in technology. We offer study grants to eligible Polytechnic graduates interested in gaining work experience while pursuing a first degree. Two study modes are available for successful applicants who want the best of both worlds:

  • Part-time study while working for Accenture

    Part-time study applicants will continue to work full time under the ETDP, a unique learning initiative which aims to develop aspiring software engineers into Accenture technology experts.

  • Full-time study after at least one year of service

    After completing approximately one year of service employment, participants can opt to pursue full-time study and will be granted a leave of absence for up to three years to pursue their degree.

Eligibility criteria and qualifications:

  • Only applicable to ETDP participants
  • Highest qualification is a diploma from a recognised polytechnic
  • Less than one year of formal working experience
  • Interested in pursuing a first degree

Your Career Path

The ETDP aims to skill you up in new technologies. Through online and classroom trainings, you will obtain certification in areas such as digital content, analytics, big data, and digital marketing. You will also have the chance to network with experienced IT professionals who will share their insights and knowledge. All these will help you advance professionally in the Client Delivery & Operations career track, where you will grow into delivery-focused roles, and may progress laterally or upward as illustrated below, together with accompanying employee thoughts.

Apply Now

As a software engineering professional, you will be deployed to real client projects in various areas such as Client Delivery, Mobility, Digital, Advanced Technology and Architecture, Cyber Security and Emerging Platforms.

Through your experience in these large-scale projects across different industries, you’ll have the opportunity to learn a variety of delivery skills as you work with world-class industry, functional and technology capabilities.

If selected, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Access more than 9,000 online training courses and on-the-job trainings

  • Deliver innovative solutions to Global Fortune 500 firms

  • Learn and be certified in new technologies related to Digital Content, Customer Insights, Analytics, Big Data, API Management, SaaS, and Digital Marketing

  • Meet and connect with other IT specialists in our global network

  • Be supported and guided by experienced Accenture IT professionals

Qualifications

To qualify for this program, you must have:

  • A relevant diploma from a recognized polytechnic, with less than one year of formal working experience.

  • A passion for software programming

  • The ability to use one or more development languages.

  • Training in programming and software development.

Professional Requirements

  • Ability to work collaboratively

  • The ability to work creatively and analytically

  • Problem-solving skills

  • Flexibility
