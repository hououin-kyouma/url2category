Description

Welcome to "Turning your Passions into Paychecks"

I designed this course to help you articulate your career goals and best compete for internships or jobs that blend financial rewards with social impact and environmental stewardship. This course is designed to democratize access to top career advice. This means I want to make top career advice accessible to professionals who can't afford to hire a good career coach, and to students whose career center staff might not be well-versed in 21st Century impact internships and jobs available beyond on-campus recruiting programs.

This course is a blend of my career coaching experience at Brown University and at the Indiana University Kelley School of Business, as well as of the content development with my co-founder, Dr. Mark Albion (Faculty Founder of Net Impact) through our B Corps, More Than Money Careers:

  • Week 1 and 2 of this course will help you GET CLEAR by articulating your own unique motivated skills, work/life priorities, as well as prioritizing which passions you want to turn into paychecks.
  • Week 3 and 4 will focus on helping you GET CONNECTED to the most relevant resources and contacts based on the unique goals and passions you have chosen to prioritize through the GET CLEAR portion of this course.
  • Week 5 and 6 will focus on helping you GET HIRED by gaining access to 21st Century job searching advice as well as insights from cognitive neuroscience to help you tailor your resume to fit each job description, and how to convert applications into interviews and interviews into offers.

Throughout this course, you will be required to complete deliverables and post them on the discussion board so you can receive feedback and ideas on how you can make the process of turning your passions into paychecks faster and easier.

Please use the discussion area to post any questions or suggestions you might have!

Who is the target audience?
  • Working professionals interested in aligning their passions with their paychecks
  • Graduate students interested in turning their education into employment that blends financial rewards with social impact and environmental sustainability
  • Students majoring in entrepreneurship or social entrepreneurship interested in securing meaningful work in existing organizations

Passions 2 Paychecks - Best Practices And Introductions

This course is designed to help you GET CLEAR, GET CONNECTED, and GET HIRED for opportunities (e.g. internships, jobs) that match your passions. No matter whether you are currently a student or have extensive work experience, the frameworks that I will share with you through this course will help you narrow down your priorities and will make you save time and effort as you translate your intentions into a opportunity that blends financial reward with social impact and environmental sustainability.

For this course, you will:

  • Complete lesson deliverables at your own pace each week;
  • Post your deliverables online, and comment on other participants' deliverables;
  • Get feedback and suggestions from the instructor and your peers to reach your impact goals faster.

The time commitment for each week will approximately be 2-3 hours per week, but remember the more time and effort you put into this course, the more value you will get!

This community is built on trust and we therefore ask that you do not share any information about other participants or their contributions or posts related to this course outside of this course.

It might also be that your career goals are centered around making as much money as possible to pay off your student loans. The MTMCareers elearning platform distills Dr. Boutla's 10 years applying neuroscience principles to career coaching with Dr. Albion's 30+ years contributing the social business sector to equip students with the skills they need to secure internships and jobs in sustainability, cleantech, corporate social responsibility, impact investing and social change. MTMCareers is currently deployed across 40 campuses and 10 fellowship programs, which serve over 600,000 emerging leaders.
