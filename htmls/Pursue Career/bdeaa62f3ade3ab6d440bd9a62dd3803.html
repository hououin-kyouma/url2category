My Career at UC San Diego

Last Updated: April 7, 2017 3:31:32 PM PDT
Give feedback

My Career at UC San Diego is a portfolio of resources related to transfer and promotion opportunities, compensation, career development and training.


Use this portfolio to help assess your skills and interests, explore the many career opportunities at UC San Diego, and prepare for and reach your career goals. The career information and resources in My Career at UC San Diego will help you determine where you are going and how to get there.
