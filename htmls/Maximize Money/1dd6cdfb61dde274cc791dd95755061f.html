Published on Feb 23, 2017

I used to be a university student with more loan money than I knew what to do with. Shopping and spending money was my pastime. I wish I knew then what I know now... and I wish I could have told my younger self to save up! That money would have been better spent on things I really valued... like travelling! In this video I share with you my top 30 money saving hacks. Hope they help!
