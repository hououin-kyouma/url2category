10 Apps to Make Money While in College

03 Oct 10 Apps to Make Money While in College

Posted at 20:43h in Personal Finance by Melissa 1 Comment
0 Likes
Share
Facebook Twitter

Want to make some quick and easy cash? All you need is a smartphone. There are a number of apps that will even pay you just to download them. Download all 10 of these apps, and you’ll be surprised how fast the extra money adds up! From taking surveys to kicking back and watching TV, here are the apps to earn money and put a little extra cash in your pocket.
RELATED: 10 MORE Apps to Make Money in College

1. Gigwalk


Gigwalk users sign up for a free account and make money performing odd jobs around town.  Currently, most of these gigs involve taking a series of panoramic pictures of local businesses for Microsoft, which pay $5 each. Anyone can post or complete gigs.

2. Pact



How about an app that pays you to get into great shape? Pact makes going to the gym profitable beyond your physique.  It’s a community of people committed to working out.  Each participant sets a weekly workout schedule, pledging to pay between $5 and $50 for every workout they miss. At the end of the week, the payments are divided between those who stayed committed to their goals.  If you can just stick to your fitness pact, you’ll come out a little bit stronger physically and financially.  Gym Pact verifies workouts using check-ins and geo-tags, so there’s no cheating.  Pact also offers Veggie Pact and Food Logging Pact, for even more ways to stay in shape and make money.
RELATED: 4 Must-Have Apps for Students to Keep Up on Their Credit Scores

3. Ibotta


 Looking for an app that pays you to shop? Ibotta offers a different rebate every day.   Unlock it before shopping by answering a trivia question or posting on social media. Then head to the store and pick up the daily rebate items.  After checking out, hit Ibotta’s “redeem” button and take a picture of your receipt to confirm your purchases.  Once you have $10 or more in your account, you can cash out with PayPal or Venmo.

4. Receipt Hog


Hold onto your receipts, because with Receipt Hog, every time you take a picture of a receipt, you earn coins.  Coins translate to money, which can be paid out through PayPal or redeemed for gift cards. As you submit more receipts, you’ll unlock ways to make more money by taking surveys.

5. App Trailers


How many times have you skipped over those Trailers that play when you launch an app you just downloaded? Well, App Trailers pays you every time you watch a 30-second-long trailer for an app, or engage with certain other apps. The more you watch, the more points you accumulate. Points get converted into gift cards, or can be cashed out with PayPal.

6. Viggle


Most people don’t realize how much content they consume every day. This app wants to know what entertainment enthusiasts are watching and hearing.  When you’re watching TV or listening to music, open the Viggle app so it can listen and identify the song or show.  For every song or show you check out, you’ll earn points that can be redeemed for gift cards, trips, or electronics.

7. Surveys On The Go


Surveys On The Go offers surveys that pay from $0.25 to $5 per survey.  The survey data is bought by companies, political campaigns, and market research firms.  Surveys take just a few minutes, and you can cash out with PayPal.  

8. TaskRabbit



TaskRabbit provides a great way to utilize your down time.  Use this app as a supplement to your main source of income. TaskRabbit users get paid for the things they’re good at doing. Get approved to be a tasker and set your hourly rate–most start at $30 per hour.  Tasks include things like running errands, building ikea furniture, wrapping gifts, driving, walking dogs, and organizing. Those who need your services will request you through TaskRabbit.

9. Mobee


Familiar with the TV show Undercover Boss?  This app turns you into a real life simulation of the popular show.  Mobee deploys mystery shoppers to its partner stores to browse and report their shopping experience back to Mobee. The stores use these reports to improve their practices. Within 24 hours of your mystery shopping mission, you’ll be awarded points based on the quality of your report.  You can trade your points for cash, gift cards, and event tickets.

10. Snap by Groupon


Snap By Groupon offers rebates on groceries, and makes earning money simple.  Just go to the store, buy your items, and take a picture of your receipt.  It doesn’t matter where you shop.  Once you have $20 in your Snap account, you can cash out, and you earn one dollar every time you refer a friend.  So feel free to host that dinner for your classmates–whether they ever pay you back for the groceries or not, you’ll at least be earning a chunk of your money back through Snap.
