Published on Nov 5, 2013

Delaware's Mount Pleasant Elementary School students were asked "What does saving mean to you?"

Their answers clearly display what their newly established Bank At School program is teaching them: why saving is important, how students can accumulate personal savings, how and why they should use banks to save their money. It's clear that "Save early, save often!" lessons can be instilled at a very young age!
