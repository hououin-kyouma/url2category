Published on Apr 10, 2017

Today Humble shares his experience being in debt and how he overcame it. Money is something that a lot of people don't want to talk about, and those in debt can have a lot of shame associated with it. He shares how he decided to take action, change his lifestyle, and make some positive change.

Humble says that there are 3 things we do when we encounter a problem:
1. We ignore it (denial)
2. We pray for a miracle
3. Take a step back, stop, reevaluate the situation and humble ourselves.

He obviously encourages us all to do number 3 and stop, hit pause, and then decide what the best next step is. His experience with debt has given him an extreme fear of being in debt, and he feels that credit cards are terrible, and that if you don't have the money to pay for something, you shouldn't do it. He also shares how important it is to surround yourself with people who share your priorities and don't shame you for your financial situation.

Keeping his head down, living on just the bare necessities, and focusing on his goal helped him get out of debt in 3 years! So if you are out there and struggling with debt, know that you can get out and there is hope!! xoxo Please share! You never know who could use this information.
