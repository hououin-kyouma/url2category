5 Easy Ways To Make Money Online for College Students

April 29, 2017April 29, 2017 Nessa 0 Comments easy ways make money online college students, how to make money online college student, make money online college student, Make money online college students

When you are in college, you are told that studying is your job. But how are you going to pay for your textbooks or food without having to take out another loan in the process? That is where many students turn to find how to make money online as a college student. The problem is that many college students end up falling for scams instead of making actual money. Hopefully by the end of this post you will learn how to make money online as a college student and get some extra money for yourself to pay for your books or to use to enjoy a day out.



Ways to Make Money Online as a College Student

1. Sell Your Notes

If you are an excellent note taker in your classes, then this is the perfect way for you to make money online. There are many places online that will pay you for your notes, regardless of the class. Most of these websites tend to pay you for each semester and will only pay if the notes are thorough in that particular subject. You can send in your notes or study guides and when other students who are taking a similar class buy those materials, you are given a cut of that sale.

The best websites to use to sell your notes include:

Stuvia–this is an online site that allows you to upload past class notes, study guides, case studies, thesis papers, essays, flashcards and past exams. You earn money on these items every time they are sold and you earn a percentage of that sale. Students who have used this website have been able to earn as much as $700 per semester.

Notesale–this website works similar to that of Stuvia but is primarily based in the UK. However, unlike Stuvia this website is used primarily for notes or study guides that you have personally created and own the copyright to. Still, you can earn up to $400 to $600 per semester if you price the notes correctly.

2. Become a Tutor Online and Sell Your Education

Another thing that you can do to make a little extra money online is to become a tutor online to those who are struggling with the same classes you took. When you become a tutor you can help other students as they progress through classes or subjects you are knowledgeable in and get paid at the same time.

The best websites to use include:

Wyzant–with this website you are able to select the students you wish to tutor and set a schedule that works best for you. You can set the rate at which you want to charge to tutor these students and can even get paid through direct deposit for easy and quick payment.

3. Buy and Resell Textbooks

We all know how expensive college textbooks can be. What many people do not realize is that they can sell their textbooks once they are done using them. Most people just store their textbooks after they have passed the class or worse, throw them out in the trash. Instead you can resell those books for a decent price, as long as they are in good condition.

The best websites to use to sell your textbooks include:

Text Book Rush–this website allows you to list your textbooks for sale and ship them with ease. Just list them on the website and wait for someone to purchase it. On top of that you can get paid in cash, PayPal or store credit, making it one of the most convenient places to use when selling the textbooks.

Valorebooks–this website offers to buy your college textbooks for cash. As long as the books are in decent condition without any missing pages, broken spines or ripped pages, you can turn in your books for a nice profit.

Texbook Maniac–this website not only purchases textbooks, but purchases a wide variety of other books as well. This is a website that offers you the best selling price on your college textbooks so you can get more bang for your buck.

4. Ace Your Classes

Another unique way that you can make money online as a college student is to ace your classes. Many schools offer cash incentives to students if they maintain a certain GPA throughout their college career. The best website to use in this instance is a site called Ultrinsic. This site gives students the chance to set a target grade they wish to earn in their classes and then choose a cash incentive based on that goal.

For example, let’s say you are taking College Algebra and want to earn an A for that class. You would contribute $100 to Ultrinsic. If you get an A you would then earn $200, but if you do not then you wouldn’t get anything.

This website allows you to give yourself motivation to pass your classes by allowing you to reap rewards in the process which can in turn make school more fun for you. Ultrinsic is currently in 36 schools around the country so be sure to check out their site to see if it is available in your school.

5. Drive People Around with Uber

If you like driving around and don’t mind dealing with some customer service along the way, then driving with Uber is the best thing that you can consider. With Uber, you get to set your own hours and work as much or as little as you want. You earn money with Uber based on the number of rides you give and what you charge per ride.

In order to begin driving with Uber you need to be at least 21 years old and must have a valid driver’s license. You also need to have a clean criminal and driving record. You will need to check specifics on the Uber website but as long as you have a vehicle with the ability to seat at least 4, is a four door vehicle, has insurance and is registered in your state, you can begin driving with Uber as soon as this week!

 

As a college student, I know how hard it can be to earn the money you need while still being able to have time for your studies. That is why I hope that with the list you are able to make money online as a college student, yet still have the time and energy you need to dedicate to getting your degree.
