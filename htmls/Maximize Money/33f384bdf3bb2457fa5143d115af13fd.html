Make Money This 4th of July

June 22nd, 2017 Posted by Vivek Mehra mobile app, Parking, Parking Apps, ParqEx No Comment yet

Whether you are a college student looking for extra food money or a parent hoping to make some “fun” money on the side, the sharing economy can help you bring in a bit of extra cash without much effort. This becomes especially true during holidays and other special times of the year when people are looking to do out-of-the-ordinary things and are willing to pay money to do so.

Independence Day is a wonderful example of one of those special times of year. This fun-filled day is coming up soon, and it presents part-time entrepreneurs like yourself with a wonderful opportunity to fill your pockets while providing people in your community with an excellent holiday experience.

Want to get in on the fun? Here are three ways you can use the sharing economy to make some extra dough this 4th of July:

#1: Sell Baked Goods

Everyone wants to have something delicious to take to their family gathering or neighborhood potluck, and store-bought cookies aren’t usually the ideal option. Therefore, holidays such as July 4th are an ideal time to be in the business of selling baked goods. After all, far too many people find themselves short on time during the week leading up to a holiday, making it difficult to toss a cake in the oven.

If you are a good baker, why not whip up some of your special cupcakes, cookies, or muffins and list them for sale on Etsy? You might be surprised just how many orders you get, especially if you give the treat a little patriotic twist and advertise your products on social media.

#2: Share Sporting Equipment

With all the 4th of July parties coming up, there are bound to be a few people looking for sporting equipment to add some fun to their gatherings. In this instance, a garage full of baseball bats, basketballs, and football gear can be an absolute goldmine.

Why not set up shop on a site like SidelineSwap and make bank on your unused sporting equipment? This simple website gives sellers a chance to make some extra money while allowing buyers to save a buck or two. What’s not to love about that?

#3: Rent Parking Spaces

If you live in a crowded downtown area, or even if your home is in a rural area but near a good fireworks show, there is a good chance the parking in front of your house is pretty valuable during Independence Day. Take advantage of those prime parking spots by renting them out to people in your community.

ParqEx offers a wonderful solution in this situation. This service gives parking space owners an opportunity to easily rent out their spots while simultaneously offering renters a trustworthy place to shop for parking. Clearly, this is a win-win situation for all involved.

As you can see, a little bit of creativity is all you need to come up with some pretty awesome money-making schemes. Since 4th of July is just around the corner, now is the time to start brainstorming and get your business up and running before the big day arrives. So what are you waiting for? Pull out that sporting stuff, whip up those scones, list those parking spots, and get ready to see the money come rolling in!
