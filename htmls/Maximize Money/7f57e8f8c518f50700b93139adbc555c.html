Published on Oct 26, 2015

Desperate times call for desperate measures; at least, that's what our college and university students think. Managing money is part and parcel of a student's life, and we wondered how they cope with an increasing cost of living in the city.

R.AGE went to a few local and private universities to get the students to share their most creative money-saving tips. From freeloading food to physically restraining their friends from a sale, these students have some innovative and effective ways on saving money that you can pick up too!

What are your most creative ways of saving money? Share them with us below in the comment section!
