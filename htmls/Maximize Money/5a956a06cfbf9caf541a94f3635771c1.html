10 money saving tips for students

Tuesday 30-08-2016 - 10:00

1. Write up a budget plan.

 

 

2. Get an NUS card. And any other student discount card you can get your hands on.

 

 

3. Don’t buy brand new books-look for second hand for a fraction of the price.

 

 

4. Get a part time job but don’t work so much that it affects your studying.

 

 

5. Learn to cook so you don’t spend all your money on takeout.

 

 

6. Get a bike, you’ll save on short journeys and help yourself stay in shape.

 

 

7. Use cash not card especially on nights out (just leave your card at home), that way you can track your money easier.

 

 

8. When you’re shopping search for bargains and don’t feel bad using Vouchers. 

 

 

9. Sell things you don’t use anymore, Gumtree, EBay, amazon, and Shpock are all good places to start.

 

 

10.  You’re allowed to say “no!” to nights out.
