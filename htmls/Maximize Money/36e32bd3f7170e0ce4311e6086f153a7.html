Most of your monthly expenses will go into paying the house rent. Why not save those bucks by following some quick tips bought to you by Amberstudent :)

Website: https://amberstudent.com
Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/amberstudent/
Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/amberstudent/

Going abroad for education? Amberstudent.com helps you in finding a safe and affordable place near your university. We have an array of properties ranging from student rooms, apartments, independent homes etc. which enables students get exactly what they want. Students want safe, affordable accommodation and we help student get exactly that.

Accommodation search is a tedious process which involves taking many suggestions from friends then contacting tons of property managers and landlords, long paperwork etc. We make these processes smooth as a butter for you.
After registering on Amberstudent, you can get list of all types of accommodations with all details on a page. You can shortlist the apartments, share it with your friends to take suggestions. At Amberstudent, a consultant will be assigned to you for the whole process. Ask your consultant whatever and whenever you want.

After you like the particular apartment, we would just need few docs like visa copy and I20 and we will process the application for you.
