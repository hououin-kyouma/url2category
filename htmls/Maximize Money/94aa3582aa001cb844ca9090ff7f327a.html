VIDEO: Malaysian students share creative money-saving tips

  • by Samantha Chow
  • Filed under Editorial, R.AGE TV.
  • Tagged Videos, Vox Pop.

Desperate times call for desperate measures; at least, that’s what our college and university students think. Managing money is part and parcel of a student’s life, and we wondered how they cope with an increasing cost of living in the city.

R.AGE went to a few local and private universities in the Klang Valley to ask the students to share their most creative money-saving tips. From freeloading food to physically restraining their friends from a sale, these students have some innovative and effective ways on saving money that you can pick up too!

What are some of your creative ways to save money? Share them with us in the comment section below!
