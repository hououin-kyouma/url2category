Money: Tips to get out, stay out of debt in 2017

Getting out of holiday debt

Meredith TerHaar, WZZM 10:02 AM. EST January 01, 2017

Pile of money, stock image. (Photo: Thinkstock)

CONNECT TWEET LINKEDIN GOOGLE+ PINTEREST

GRAND RAPIDS, MICH. - Was your Visa or MasterCard in overdrive this holiday season?  

Mark Guimond from Michigan First Credit Union joined the WZZM Weekend Morning news with tips to help you get out, and stay out, of debt in 2017.  

"The best tip I have for decreasing debt is to create a budget -- if you have a plan in place you are likely to spend less," Guimond says.

"Set some financial goals for the year, bucket out the expenses you know will be coming, account for some surprise expenses and continue to keep a pulse on your budget."

Guimond also mentioned avoiding making the same financial mistake more than one.

"If you were in holiday debt this year, budget out some money for that this coming year and stick to that budget. As always, if you are unsure about how to create a budget or what payment structure will work for you, talk to a financial advisor."
