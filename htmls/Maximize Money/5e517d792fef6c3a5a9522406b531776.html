What is the best way for a college student to make money online?

By
LoveUMoney
-
04/04/2017
0
168
views
Share on Facebook
Tweet on Twitter

If you’re in college and looking for the best way to make money online then you are at the right place. Here in this article, we will share the real story of ‘Techshristi‘ website, which is earning 150$ per month without any Investment. College is the best time to start such website because students spend a lot of their time on the Internet and they have enough content resource to start this type of website.

All you need to do is just start an educational website related to your college and profession. Next, upload all your educational materials like Powerpoint Presentation, Question Papers etc on that website. Then ask your friends and classmate to visit your website. Gradually, your website will get famous on the college campus and you will have a good number of visitor on your site.The best part of such website is that it never get die. Your juniors will keep visiting your website even after you finished with your college.

Now, let us discussed the story of Techshristi Website.This website was created in 2011 using free article sharing script. Then, in 2014 it moves on to WordPress Platform.

Initially, it was created to upload and share Project Reports and Class Room Notes but later it allowed students to interact with each other. In 2012, it started an online forum for the students. This forum was created using open source SMF forum.

Today, this website is making 150$ per month without any investment. Even the website is not updated since a long time. But still, it is making money out of it. There are several benefits for starting educational website during college times like

  • Being in college you are aware of latest Updates and News
  • It is easy to get Classroom Notes and Project Reports for your website
  • You can easily promote your website on your college campus and thereby get quality traffic for your site.
  • You have enough free time to work on your website
  • Starting a website in early age will provide a great benefit in the latter stage.

So, it is good to start such website during the college times itself. If you are college students and want to make money online then start a website immediately. At least start collecting educational materials from your friends and classmate. If you don’t know how to start an online website then read the below answer, it may help you
