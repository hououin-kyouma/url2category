Don’t go through school broke! Use these tips to help you manage your money and make it through school without starving or selling your motorcycle!

Let's go over my top three suggestions for getting through school without starving or being completely broke.
1) Make a budget.

2) Save money when and where you can.
- Rent and transportation
- food, clothing and other things you need
- student discounts
- flyers / collecting coupons
- shopping when things are on sale
- shop online
- how to get cash back
- Tuition
- scholarships
- Books
- iPad/tablet


3) Make money.
- Part time work
- Job ideas
- Direct sales
- Affiliate
