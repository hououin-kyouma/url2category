Managing your money at university can be a daunting experience. Hear from our current students about how they manage their money, with tips about applying for student finance and bursaries, getting part time work, planning a budget and making the most of your student discounts. QMUL’s Advice and Counselling Service also offer lots of help and information about managing your money: www.welfare.qmul.ac.uk.
