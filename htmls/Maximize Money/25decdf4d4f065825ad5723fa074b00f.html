Making Money Work

Identify your personal, career and educational goals while earning high school and CNM college credit free of charge.

Contact Us

For more information, email makingmoneywork@cnm.edu or call (505) 224-3352.

More info for students and parents.

More info for school administrators.

Making Money Work is FIN1010

FIN1010 is a distance learning Dual Credit course taught online at your high school by a CNM instructor.

Participating High Schools

Refer to the list of Participating High Schools to see if your school offers FIN1010.

If you don't see your school on the list of participating high schools, contact your principal or high school counselor.
