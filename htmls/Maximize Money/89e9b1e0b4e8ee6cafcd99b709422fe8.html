Hello Everyone and Welcome to Top Trending Videos.
Today's topic is: Money Saving Tips which Every College Student Need To Know.

Number 10: Apply for grants and scholarships
If your grades are good enough to apply for scholarship and grants, try it. With this you will release financial burden of your parents.

Number 9: Find roommates
If you want to save and you are living in apartment, best way is to find roommates so you will share the living expenses.

Number 8: Learn to cook
Cooking your own meals will help you save BIG money avoiding going to restaurants.
At the same time this skill is very useful and you can have a great time if you cook with your friends or roommates.

Number 7: Apply for a part-time job
If you struggle with your finance, find some part-time job. With this you will solve the finance problem, and also you will gain some work experience.

Number 6: Buy used books
If you don't mind that the books are used, you can save a lot of money.
You can go online to find the books or maybe you have Used Book Fair on your campus or university.

Number 5: Take advantage of student discounts
Lot of places offer student discounts, and the only proof they need is a student ID. So don't forget to always have your student ID with you.

Number 4: Get a bike
With having a bike you will save on transportation. At the same time you will exercises.

Number 3: Reduce ATM Fees
Try to plan your expenses for whole week, or even month, so you will not go to the ATM every they. With this you can avoid ATM fees.

Number 2 Take advantage of free or low-cost activities
Most of the colleges and universities organize some free activities and events for their students.

Number 1: Don't forget that you are a student
Don't waste money on unnecessary things because you still don't have a career.
