      Get out of debt

      $10k goal

      Campaign created 4 months ago
      Donate Now  Share on Facebook
       Share
        Tweet
       14 shares
      • Story
      • Updates   6
      Jessie Goss  WAURIKA, OK
      Hi,
      My name is Jessie and a few years ago my wife Niki  suddenly died.  I was left with sorrow, sadness and heartache and  a lot of bills. My town was very good to us and always have been. Our funeral expenses were taking care of within two months which was amazing.
      Of course I prayed and I prayed alot   for months and years. A classmate came into my life and she has been quite successful in her life and she bailed me out of debt. So now I am in debt to her and I want to get her paid back in full.  My daughter is on her own now and she wants to go to School but has to work full time. So it would be nice to have extra income to help her get started and see her finish what ever she wants to do.
      This is Niki my wife who passed in 2008. She was 45 yrs old. She had seizures.
       When you lose someone you love it effects your life in many ways. First of all their not there to greet and comfort you or just to talk to and do things with. Then there is the heart ache and sorrow and mourning.
      My daughter Jessica was barely a teenager when her mother passed. So I tried to take care of her and mourn to as she was dealing with the loss of her mother. Then on top of that my Dad dies 25 days after my wife died. Then trying to deal with the loss of my father and be there for my mother. Which I know none of these things are anything that we all at sometime will face. Experiences from life actually enrich us in so many ways. I kept a positive attitude and allowed those who are helpers to help and truly appreciated it all. My main strength was and is my relationship with God. He actually carries His people at all times and many other people who aren't even aware of it. I just want to get my friend paid.
