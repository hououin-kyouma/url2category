“How to get out of debt?” Whether you have college loan debt, want to pay off your car or house loan, or just want to leap forward in your personal finances. This SIMPLE 7 step debt-breaking system is for you!

It’s time to free yourself from bad debt and learn how to properly take on good debt.

The 7 Step Debt-Breaking System:

  1. Build up a crisis fund.
  2. Pay the minimum payments only.
  3. Utilize the DEBT SNOWBALL SYSTEM.
  4. Create additional income streams NOW.
  5. Amp your crisis account to a 4 MONTH RESERVE.
  6. DO NOT cut the credit cards – build your DISCIPLINE.
  7. Get into GOOD DEBT.
