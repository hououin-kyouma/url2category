Hi everyone! I'm Laurie and I'm a master's student at the University of Stirling, Scotland. In this video I show you what I eat in a day. Of course this is different from day to day, but especially for breakfast and lunch it's quite similar every day. I have also included some money saving tips anyone can do to save a few pennies every month! :)

I hope you enjoy it! If you have any more questions or have other tips you want to share, please leave a comment below.

The ingredients used for every meal:
Breakfast
- Natural Greek Yoghurt (Low Fat) (Lidl)
- Simply Sumptuous Maple & Pecan Crisp Granola (Lidl)
- Nescafe Cappuccino (Aldi)

Lunch
- 2 eggs (the third one you see in the video was for my flatmate Bo)
- toast (Lidl)
- pinch of salt and pepper (Adli)
- a little bit of mayonnaise (Adli)
- Squash (Tesco)

Dinner
- 1 aubergine (Lidl)
- 2 leeks (Lidl)
- 1 tomato (Lidl)
- 1 courgette (Lidl)
- 1 pepper (Aldi)
- cherry tomatoes (Aldi)
- Freshona Tomato Pascata (Lidl)
- Combino Lasagne (Lidl)
(Honestly, we just threw everything that was about to go bad in it so we didn't have to throw any food out!)

Thank you for watching!
