ABOUT THIS VIDEO:
What is the meaning of life? And what is my life purpose? How do I find it?.. Within this video I try my best to explain the meaning of life on a scientific and sociological level, as well as a practical level that you can use to find the purpose of your life and why you were put here on this earth.

The meaning of life, from a sociological view, is to contribute to society so that humanity can continue to exist. However, that meaning of life is not very fulfilling unless you're someone like Darwin or Freud.

From a different point of view (The Dalai Lama), the purpose of our life is to seek happiness. At first, this may seem like another general answer that cannot be applied to our lives specifically. But if we look deeper, we can find the purpose of our lives if we simply study the things that make us the happiest.

The Dalai Lama explains that to live a life of purpose, we must "Identify the factors that lead to happiness, and identify the factors that lead to suffering. Then, we must gradually eliminate those factors that lead to suffering, and cultivate those factors that lead to happiness."

Once you identify the things that make you happy, you should then move on to create a life where you are doing all of those things. This will give your life purpose, fulfillment, and a sense of meaning.
