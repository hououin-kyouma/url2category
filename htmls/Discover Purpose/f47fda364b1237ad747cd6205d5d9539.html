      Finding your divine purpose

      By Kalli White 1488298830000
      0 shares
       
      tweet now!
      0 shares
       
      tweet now!

      Leah Barnett from She Awakening believes every person comes to this planet with a divine gift to give to the world.

      Women building each other up rather than competing with each other is key to being able to live a life of joy and purpose, Leah told Fresh Living.

      Leah's passion is working with women one-on-one to empower them to discover that purpose and believes we are not able to truly live an authentic life if we aren't aligned with our soul purpose.

      Visit SheIsAwakening.com for more information on workshops that focus on the importance of women meeting together to build the bonds of sisterhood.
