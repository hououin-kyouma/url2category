Are You Ready To Discover Your Life Purpose?

Are You Ready To Discover Your Life Purpose?

I saw a quote I loved yesterday and I want to share it with you #now:

“To exist is to change, to change is to mature, to mature is to go on creating oneself endlessly.”

– Henri Bergson

And I think I love this quote because it encapsulates so much of what I’m all about and this quote includes two out of the four spiritual laws of creation. CLICK HERE if you would like to learn the fundamentals about the four spiritual laws of creation and how understanding them can change your perspective and your life.

Lately I’ve been sharing a lot about following your joy and fulfilling your life purpose and what’s interesting is how many subscribers wrote to me this week and said, “Alison, I really don’t know! How do I know what I’m supposed to be doing?”

And it’s not uncommon because I find most of my new clients ask me the same question but the thing is, I find most of them actually know, at least they know unconsciously…..

And for those of you who subscribed during the week or missed last week’s newsletter and you can CLICK HERE to read it, I mentioned that one of the reasons why we do not know what brings us joy and why we do not consciously know our purpose is because we spend so much time doing things we do not enjoy…. for the money, the security, the certainty or because our job is easy when we know what we are doing and so we have forgotten our WHY.

When we spend all day doing something we do not enjoy and even a whole lifetime in a career we do not like, that creates a negative state of mind and that state of mind attracts things into our life of a matching kind or matching energy.

Our state of mind creates our life and so we are either in or out of alignment with our true nature or self and our emotional state is our guide.

Some people like to call this process manifesting and whether or not you prescribe to the woo-woo term manifesting, essentially you are always manifesting… you are either manifesting perfect happiness or perfect misery for yourself.

What are you manifesting #now?

Would you like to know how to manifest happiness? I can show you how to manifest what you want with my Manifest Your Dreams coaching session or programme. CLICK HERE to find out more.

And this reflection, of perfect happiness or perfect misery or otherwise known as our life circumstances, is how we evolve because it gives us the opportunity to understand where we are at, in terms of our personal evolution and the opportunity to recalibrate so we can create more of what we prefer.

Who said we are supposed to feel sad, live in misery, believe life is hard and full of tragedy, give our power away to others and feel helpless, experience lack and do things without purpose and fulfilment? Is that how we should live? No!

Life does not have to be that way and I can help you to get back on track with my new programme so if you are curious read on to learn more……

Now there are big changes happening in the world right now and perhaps this is the reason why so many people are seeking answers and why so many people are looking for more meaning in their lives and these changes, that are taking place on a macro or global level, mirror those changes taking place on a micro or individual level.

And what I feel these changes are asking from us are, it’s asking us to let go of the old way of doing things that no longer serves us and the planet and, with courage, evolve and step into a new way of doing life and why that is difficult for us and others is, because it creates inner conflict.

It requires us to renew our beliefs that have served us well up to now but are no longer creating the experience we want and listen and align to what our soul’s desire which is sometimes in conflict with what our egos want.

And even though our egos think we want that secure, certain job with good money, a big home, a flash car or other material things for some reason it doesn’t feel like it should or how we thought it would and it feels like something is missing and there is an inner yearning for more of something but what?

Are you feeling this too? I felt like this and then I found the answers that helped me to resolve the inner conflict, clear the blocks and step into my purpose and I reveal all in my  eBook, What Was In The Way Of Love, which is available for instant purchase and digital download by CLICKING HERE.

What is it, that you are yearning for? Our highest intention is to experience joy daily and feel the fulfilment of living a life with purpose or working in a vocation that is your life mission but what exactly is it?
