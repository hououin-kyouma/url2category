What road are you on?  Journey of life quotes invite you to travel down varied paths to inspiration and awareness. Life holds many possibilities when you have an open mind and traveler’s spirit. 

Read more...

Journey of life quotes speak of ‘journey’ as a metaphor for life.  Gloria Gaither reminds us: “We may run, walk, stumble, drive, or fly, but let us never lose sight of the reason for the journey or miss a chance to see a rainbow on the way.”  What is the reason for your journey?  For many, it is connected to a feeling of purpose. Are you feeling joy in your journey of life? If not, looking through these life as a journey quotes may help you explore your current priorities.  

Research shows happiness is tied to close family or community connections, doing work we believe is meaningful, and pursuing pastimes or work we find enjoyable.  An easy way to evaluate what’s working (and what isn’t) in your life is to observe when your energy is blocked and when it flows.  Simply do more of what fills you with energy, and less of what drains you.  Making baby-step changes can take you down a whole new path.  While you are at it: make up your mind to complain less and inspire more, want less and share more.  When you reach out and help others, you will notice more and more joy finding you.

The more you visit the journey of life quotes and related subject pages, the quicker you will begin a chain reaction of positively inspired moments with the potential to stimulate change.  Enjoy each moment of your journey.  You were born to live a life which brings you joy…it’s up to you to create it!
