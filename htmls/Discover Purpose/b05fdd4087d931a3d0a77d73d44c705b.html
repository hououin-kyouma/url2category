Archangels

How the Archangels can help YOU everyday!

Basically, all Angels can and will help you with any and every thing you can think of!  Asking could not be simpler: "Angels please help me" is all that is required of you in order to invoke their assistance.

A question that is often asked is this: "If angels can see that I'm in trouble, needing help etc, why don't they just help me and be done with it?"

Answer? Your Free Will gift. If the angels could get involved without our invitation, then it wouldn't be FREE, would it?

There are exceptions - if we are in danger of going Home/ leaving our physical body BEFORE our time, then, they can jump right in. Think about it; have you ever felt SURE you would be in an accident for example, and somehow escaped without harm? That's the angels right there.

Working with the angels is not an 'either/or' option. The angelic realm will work hand in hand with your chosen health-care professionals if you call upon them.

Have a look at each Archangels name: Archangel Uriel's name means "Gods light". Uriel will light you way. Call for enlightenment, Uriel will help you.

Archangel Michael  will boost your courage, cut away all fear based thoughts that lead to fear based actions. Michael can and will, live with you, surrounding you with his love and protection - if you ask him too.

Archangel Raphael is a powerful healer of physical bodies. Call him! You can also call him (and the others) on behalf of others. Archangel Michael works closely with Raphael, so don't be surprised when you feel him around anytime you are working with Raphael.

Archangel Metatron is very concerned about children who have been labeled as having ADD, or ADHD, and helps parents, teachers, family, Doctors etc to know what these children need for their highest good. If you know, or know of, someone who had been labeled ADD or ADHD, PLEASE call on him for assistance and guidance for everyone involved to know and do the correct and best thing for this child/person.
