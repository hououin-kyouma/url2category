What is My Purpose in Life?

2/17/2017

Comments

 
As a musician, I can't even begin to count the number of times someone has told me "you are so lucky you get to do what you love... I wish I knew what I wanted to do in life or could be a musician." Every time I get this statement my response is always "it doesn't have to be a musician. I know people who's calling was being a teacher, a yoga instructor, a spiritual healer, an artist, an actor, hell even a programming engineer for NASA! The truth is that we are always lost before we are found and I know you can find your calling if you simply start looking for it in the right place and listen to what you really think and feel".
​ Well, what is your purpose in life or how can you start a journey into figuring out what your calling is or what you want to do in life? Here I would like to kick off what I hope will be a series of similar posts, comprised of videos I find inspirational and clear in helping you pave your own path toward the life you want to live & doing what you love!

​As someone who's gone down the rabbit whole and left his career for another one, I want to leave you with a solid piece of advice.... 
Always remember that the greatest things in life - such as pursuing your dream or passion - is never easy.... But do you know why? It's because life is testing you. Just like school did, life is trying to get you emotionally and mentally in shape to help you shed off who you were and help you grow into who you will become!

​Stay grateful, hungry, inspired and wired my friends! - J. Danno
