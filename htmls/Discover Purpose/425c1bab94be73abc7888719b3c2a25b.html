How can you find your life's purpose?

I remember asking myself this at 12 years old. If I had a time machine I would tell myself these key tips:

1. Go directly for what you want, with focus.
2. Build on strengths
3. Stick to it (sometimes is take more time than you expect)
4. Pray for what you want

Ask and you will receive. Seek and you will find.
