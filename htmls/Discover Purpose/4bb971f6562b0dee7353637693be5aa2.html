The Best Motivational Quotes

by Kevin Ngo 62 Comments

Here is a list of the very best motivational quotes which I’ve carefully chosen out of thousands. Some will resonate with you, while some won’t. When you find one that does, remember to write it down. More importantly, use its effect that it has on you to take action. Without action, these inspirational quotes or any other tools on this site for that matter, will be absolutely useless.

This list of quotes is constantly expanding so you can bookmark this page and check on it regularly for more quotes. The quotes on this page are geared towards helping you to take action. If you’d like to see quotes in other categories, scroll down towards the bottom for a list of topics.

The great thing about these inspiring quotes is that they can help you increase your motivation in order for you to take action and accomplish your goals. The hardest part about obtaining any goal is to get yourself to do the things you need to do when you need to do them. Anyone can take action when they feel like it. If you can motivate yourself to do what needs to be done even when you would rather do something else like procrastinate, you will be ahead of the majority of people.

These quotes can help you remember the reasons why you are pursuing your goals in the first place. It’s easy to forget the point of our dreams when we are met with difficulty and setbacks. When you feel like giving up the most, these inspirational quotes about life can help you stand back up and keep moving forward.

Sharing these motivational quotes with a team is a great way to increase their motivation as well. Whether it’s a business setting or a sports setting, these phrases of wisdom can help energize your team and help them step up their game.
