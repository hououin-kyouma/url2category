Hello Hina Munir and welcome to the forum.

Would you maybe grant us with the favor of elaborating more about what that so called blue pearl has done to YOU in term of expanding your awareness and your spiritual development?

I beg you to forgive my skepticism. But it just seems to me that there is a definite trend within the current New age movement in trying to externalize spirituality. As far as my intuition tells me, it seems like one cannot measure his/her level of spiritual development simply by looking at whatever marks that appear in his/her body. Even if that theory could be true (which is highly unlikely), it should necessarily be the effect (but not the cause) of one's change of level of being.

I sincerely hope and wish that you will find a way to get out of that confusion you are in (as you said it yourself), and that you will actually begin to do some real work to improve yourself and help others by shining your inner light to them. Don’t be stuck and absorbed in looking at and observing whatever new marks that may appear in your body in the future and assume that those gives you a confirmation of a “spiritually advanced” person. That attitude amount to what may be termed daydreaming if not spiritual narcissism.

May love and light shine upon you.
