College On Purpose: Quick Summary

Whether you’re an entering freshman or about to graduate, you are going through some huge transitions and decisions. What’s your purpose? What do you really want to do with your life? Who do you want to become? Can you really live the life you’ve dreamed of? Isn’t that just for the lucky few?

When you discover your Passionate Purpose you develop clarity about what’s most important to you and why. You now have a powerful reason to wake up excited – every day. The question is: Will you pursue it, or find an excuse not to? The answer to that will change your life.

Target Audiences

Students at every level from incoming freshmen to outgoing seniors who are looking for their Passionate Purpose that will guide them to exceptional success in their schooling, careers, and life. Presentations are custom tailored to your specific audience.

Intended Outcomes

Participants will:

  • Become inspired and convinced that finding their Passionate Purpose will lead them to exceptional success and greater well being in their schooling, work, and life
  • Learn strategies and techniques to discover what they truly want and why they want it in every aspect and season of their lives
  • Discover the actions necessary to reach all their goals
  • Learn how to stay motivated when obstacles arise
  • Leave with practical, action steps they can implement immediately
  • Optional takeaways: A personalized, paperback copy of GO! A digital version of GO! The companion eBook, 5 Steps to Finding Your Passionate Purpose, packed with action pages to move your people forward
Do Less and Achieve More: Quick Summary
Who else wants more time? This isn’t about time management, it’s about spending time on what’s essential. Student’s busy lifestyles and the demands on their time have them pulled in a million directions. Class, studying, texts, social media, tweets and handling “emergencies” distract them from the vital things they want to do.
This event leads to a new mindset and provides tested strategies to silence the distractions and focus on what’s most important so they can make a difference in the world.
Target Audiences

Students at every level who are struggling with the demands of classes, tests, work, community service, peer pressure, and more. Presentations are custom tailored to your specific audience.

Intended Outcomes

Participants will:

  • Discover the paradox of how doing less can actually help them achieve more
  • Realize lack of time is not the problem, lack of focus is
  • Understand how multitasking, being reactive, poor email management, and “drifting” destroy their productivity
  • Learn strategies and techniques to arrange their day, focus, and effort on the right things at the right times for exceptional success
  • Leave with practical action steps they can implement immediately
  • Optional takeaways: A personalized, paperback copy of GO! A digital version of GO! The companion eBook, 5 Steps to Finding Your Passionate Purpose, packed with action pages to move your people forward
