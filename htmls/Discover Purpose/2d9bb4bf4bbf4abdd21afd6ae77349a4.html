About

Welcome to Personal Excellence — a personal development site for people who are passionate about achieving excellence in life! I’m Celestine Chua, the founder of this site. If you want to achieve your highest potential and live your best life, this is THE place for you.

The mission of Personal Excellence is: “To touch others’ lives, help them achieve their highest potential and live their best lives.” No matter who you are or what you do, you have unlimited potential to achieve everything you have ever wanted. This site is here to help you do that.  For the past 10 years, I have supported millions of people in their growth through PE and coached tens of thousands of clients to unleash their highest potential in their Career, Relationships, Love, Health and Life.

I’m passionate about YOUR growth and supporting you to be the best you can be. For more about my story, click here.
