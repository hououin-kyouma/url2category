What is My Purpose?

Stumble
Pin
WhatsApp
Email
Shares 87

Wanting to discover a meaning and purpose in life is one of the oldest desires of mankind.

But how can you find a meaning and purpose in life when there are thousands of possible paths to take?

In other words, where do you go from here?

If you’ve come to this page, you are likely at a crossroads in life. You might feel lost and completely without a clue as to what your true calling is.

Perhaps you’ve searched for years, but nothing quite “fit” or seemed right. Or perhaps, you’ve only begun the search recently and feel completely stranded, overwhelmed and depressed.

The truth is that deep down, you want to make your life mean something. You want to dedicate your time doing what you love. But HOW?

WHERE do you start?

Your Purpose …

While we can’t reveal to you what your meaning is, we can help you find your purpose.

Why is this?

Meaning is subjective. It comes from the mind. It is dependent on your personal tastes, desires, goals, and dreams.

Purpose on the other hand is from Spirit. It is programmed into us. It is within our very cells. It is written into each and every destiny.

Just look at a tree. Its purpose is to grow from a seedling into a fully grown tree. Or look at a bird; its purpose is to hatch from its shell, learn to fly, leave its nest and start its own family.

Within us all, there is an ancient pattern, and that pattern is of maturity.

Everything in life matures, from the people around us to the animals, vegetables, and minerals in our world every day.

Regardless of what religion, race, gender or identity you have, the underlying purpose of your life is to soulfully and spiritually mature. Without maturing, we remain stunted, immature and unable to fully embody who we are destined to become. Without maturing, we remain stranded in anger, judgment, and fearful behavior.

So how do we fully mature, both physically and spiritually,  so that we can fulfil our purpose and life work?

Soulwork

Soulwork is the process of working with your soul. It is the core teaching of both this website and our work collectively.

In order to work with our soul, we must listen to it, understand it, and learn how to embody it. On this website, we help you to do that through a number of sacred practices, which you can read more about here.

The aim of Soulwork is to help you fulfil your purpose in life: to soulfully mature.

An individual who has fully matured is what we refer to as an “Elder Soul.”

Elder Souls

We like to picture the Elder Soul as a person who resembles a large Oak tree: they have grown to their fullest potential, weathered every season, grown deep roots and stand strong in the midst of life.

An Elder Soul is a Master, awakened being, and self-actualized person.

The term “Elder Soul” is simply a label we use to describe a person who has cultivated so much wisdom, presence, and freedom from suffering, that their quest for wholeness has ended. Unconditional love, peace, and joy remains.

This can be described as the ultimate state of being.

Finally …

Spiritual maturity comes with effort, dedication and practice. We are both here to help you grow into it through Soulwork.
