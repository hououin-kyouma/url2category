Hands Up remix of Find the Purpose in your Life from MLP:FIM Season 6 Episode 19: The Fault in our Cutie Marks

Free download: http://www.mediafire.com/download/mqc...

This was a super fun one to remix! Fell in love with the song when it aired (CMC songs are my favourite) and was so excited to start work on it.
I've literally just moved into uni accommodation so juggling the remix with moving in was a chore, but I still managed to release this remix the day after the song aired, so that's a double win in my books!
Really tried to break conventions and be experimental again with this and I am really happy with the results. Hope you enjoy!
