Purpose Alignment Indicator Survey™

Welcome to the Purpose Alignment Indicator Survey™!

This survey is designed to assess how connected you are to a happy, fulfilled and purpose-driven life. Your statement ratings will be used to calculate your Purpose Alignment Score™ and for general and anonymous purpose-based research.

The following statements should be read and honestly and thoughtfully rated from 1 thru 5 (1 being “Strongly Disagree” and 5 being “Strongly Agree”). There are no response time limits on this survey.

Once you have rated all 27 statements and submitted your responses at the conclusion of the survey, you will receive your Purpose Alignment Score™ and information on what it all means.

Thank you for taking the first and most important step in understanding where you are on your journey to a happy, fulfilled and purpose-driven life! I hope you will find this Purpose Alignment Indicator Survey™ helpful as you think about your life in a more purpose-driven way.

This Purpose Alignment Indicator Survey™ is also part of my new workbook How to Find a Job, Career and Life You Love available on my website at LouisEfron.com.

Now over to you!

Have fun, be happy, and live on purpose!

Best wishes,
Louis
