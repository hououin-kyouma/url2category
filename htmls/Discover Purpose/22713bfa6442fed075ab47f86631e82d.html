Published on May 29, 2017

In this video I talk about how to find your purpose and passion in life and career. If you are someone who think you are too young or too old to find your purpose and passion, your calling, this video may help you find it.

Some people are lucky enough to find their interests very early on and they start working on them to become the masters of their craft. But not all of us are lucky, still we can decide now and start at this moment to find our passion by following an interest that is close to our heart.

Pick an interest you have that has the potential to be useful for others and go after it. Train hard and be highly determined to become the best in your endeavor. When you enjoy what you do, you will start doing more of it because its more fun and it gives you internal motivation. What you enjoy doing, you become passionate at it and what you are passionate about, you become great at it. When you are good at something + you enjoy doing it + you are passionate at it, Good things start to happen.
