At various times in our lives, many of us ask the eternal question: What is my purpose?

This question often comes up when we are young and idealistic and yearning to make a difference. It can come up when we are frustrated and bored in a career that is unchallenging or unrewarding. Even if we are successful, we may feel some discontent in our soul, wondering if there isn’t something even more fulfilling for us to do in life. It raises a question, each of us may ask, “What do I want to be when I grow up?”

How to Find Our Life Purpose

By looking at your horoscope and seeing which “house” your Sun sign falls into, you may learn more about your life purpose.

Let me explain. The zodiac sign that a person’s sun is in (i.e. their Sun Sign) is established based on the time they were born. The house one’s sun is located in—tells the person, in which area of life they can best express their purpose and shine, internally, and externally out in the world. Keep in mind that there are twelve houses in the birth chart. Each one represents a different area of a person’s life. To find out which House your Sun falls, use Free Transit Calculator (at the end of this article).

Once you know which house your sun falls into, just look at the description (below) showing all 12 Houses. The information about each House may help you gain greater insight into your life’s purpose. In the PART 1 we discuss Houses 1 through 4. PART 2 will discuss the other Houses.

The 12 Houses of the Zodiac

1st House of Personality & Appearance. If the Sun falls here we may find that our life purpose concerns ourselves, our personality and identity. We may be interested in opportunities to make a new beginning where we assert our individuality.

Possible Life Purposes: We may pursue a life path where we can be our own boss as an entrepreneur or leader. Our courage makes us the ideal adventurer who can strike out boldly in the world as a law officer, surgeon, fire fighter or professional athlete. We have drive to be whatever we want to be.

2nd House of Money & Values. If the Sun falls here we may find that our life purpose concerns making money and accumulating possessions or doing something that is consistent with our values. We may yearn to develop our hidden talents.

Possible Life Purposes: We may pursue a life path where we accumulate wealth through investing or acquiring possessions such as stock broker or realtor. We may gravitate toward a career where we can use our talents and live our life according to our values such as a musician, fashion designer, gardener or sculptor.

3rd House of Communication & Intellect. If the Sun falls here we may find that our life purpose concerns communications, education, learning or teaching as well as transportation. We may seek find new and improved ways to communicate or look for new ways to stimulate our minds.

Possible Life Purposes:  We may pursue a life path where we can express our need to communicate by being a writer, teacher or lawyer. We may enjoy opportunities to travel in order to satisfy our curiosity. Some of us may use our logic and math skills pursuing technology. Others of us may need a variety of activities to keep us stimulated.

4th House of Family & Home. If the Sun falls here we may find that our life purpose relates to the family, home or children and the nurturance of others. We may enjoy real estate and expressing ourselves through food and other comforts. Or, we may feel a need for a strong emotional connection to whatever path we take.

Possible Life Purposes: We may pursue a life path where we can express our need to nurture others through such professions as being a teacher, psychologist, actor, chef, farmer, realtor or a caretaker of some sort. We have a need to use our feeling and imaginative natures to connect with others on a deeper level.
