Contributing to a changing world: How to find my purpose?

1 March 2016 | 18:30 to 21:30 | Geneva

Member Initiative by our members Pierre Volant and Deborah Küttemann

Objective:

Learn to clarify your vision and align your actions to what moves you.

Description:

Do you feel frustrated in your current life or job, knowing you could use your talents differently? Do you see the world changing, would like to contribute to it but don’t know how? Do you want to give more meaning to your life and wonder what your purpose is?

Then this workshop is for you!

Get a taste of some of the tools that will help you clarify your vision and align your actions to what moves you.

During this group session, you will get a clearer idea of what is meaningful to you and where you stand with your purpose ; you will learn to connect to yourself; to develop the resources to help you find your way ; to overcome your limitations and to start your first steps to action.

Take away benefits and tips:

  • Learn to connect to yourself, listen to your inner voice, and develop self-awareness.
  • Receive external insights from peers on your present situation. 
  • Connect to your « future self »: Know where you stand with your vision and what are the next steps to put in place.
  • Be conscious of your body, physically feel what is blocking you and experience how to go beyond your limitations.
  • Discover how to develop your listening skills and your intuition. 
  • Get a taste of mindfulness practice through simple, easy to do exercises and learn to create a space for reflection in your daily life.
  • Get to know a group of like-minded people and make meaningful connections.

Price:
25CHF for Impact Hub Geneva members
50CHF for not-yet-members (pay at the door)

Number of participants: 
Maximum 16 participants

Duration: 
3 hours, from 6:30pm to 9:30pm
Welcome and registration at 6:15pm

Language: 
French and/or English according to he Participants

Trainers: 
Pierre Volant, Deborah Küttemann

Pre-requisit:
Open-mindedness, strong will to explore your potential, to connect to your future possibility and make meaningful connections

 

About the trainers:

Pierre Volant

Pierre’s objective is to enable the transformation of people and organizations towards more meaningful, soulful and innovative actions. He has worked in the private and public sectors for 12 years, where he led large transformational projects in international and multicultural contexts. In addition to his Master’s degree in Industrial Engineering, he’s trained in physical theatre and in collective intelligence techniques. He also  practices and teaches mindfulness meditation.

Deborah Küttemann

A dual Franco-German national, Deborah is a certified coach (ACC ICF) dedicating herself to conscious personal and professional development. She works with managers and executives in increasing their self-awareness towards more purposeful engagement. As an actress trained at the Geneva Conservatory and at the Cours Florent, she also teaches the art of presence and public speaking at Universities in Geneva. In a previous life, Deborah was leading the marketing and communications department of a major international financial institution.
