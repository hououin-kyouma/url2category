Finding your Purpose

A class for learning the biblical approach to getting a job, Finding Your Purpose (formerly called AIM) helps those who are unemployed, misemployed or underemployed seek God’s guidance in regard to their work. While this class will not replace the responsibility of searching for a job, it will teach you to discover the purpose in your work.

Trusting God

“I came away from the class with added tools and scripture to encourage me to trust God. It helped me to realize that the God we serve owns everything. All we have to do is ask, trust and rest in Him, and He will work it out.”

Brian Johnson

When + Where

Saturday, Oct. 7th, 2017 | 9am-2pm | Main Building, The Zone, Norcross

Class sessions are held in February and September.
