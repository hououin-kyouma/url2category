Why is Santa Barbara called “The American Riviera?”

The climate along this south-facing stretch of coast is similar to what you’d find in southern France and Italy – sunny and warm without ever becoming too hot or humid.

The centuries-old architecture reflects the region’s Spanish heritage while the Santa Ynez mountains rise dramatically over the city and make a scenic backdrop to the town.

You’ll love the local culture, nearby wineries, and countless opportunities for outdoor adventure!
