Published on Jun 28, 2017

Few things wake you up like burnout or the loss of a loved one. Sylvia Mulinge had both these experiences and she started questioning her life, her choices and her arrival at what she had been taught 'success'.
She shares her journey and finally her arrival at her purpose or true north.

The number 2 in the number 1 company in East Africa, Sylvia Mulinge talks about finding her purpose
