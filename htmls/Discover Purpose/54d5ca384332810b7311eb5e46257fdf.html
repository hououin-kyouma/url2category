Life, Work, Dreams
I truly believe that everything that we do and everyone that we meet is put in our path for a purpose. Marla Gibbs
Positive, Trust, Door

The journey is valuable, but believing in your talents, your abilities, and your self-worth can empower you to walk down an even brighter path. Transforming fear into freedom - how great is that? Soledad O'Brien
Freedom, Journey, Fear
As a single footstep will not make a path on the earth, so a single thought will not make a pathway in the mind. To make a deep physical path, we walk again and again. To make a deep mental path, we must think over and over the kind of thoughts we wish to dominate our lives. Henry David Thoreau
Deep, Mind, Thoughts
If you're walking down the right path and you're willing to keep walking, eventually you'll make progress. Barack Obama
Progress, Walking, You
Over every mountain there is a path, although it may not be seen from the valley. Theodore Roethke

Success, Persistence

Strength, History, Goals
Trust your own instincts, go inside, follow your heart. Right from the start. go ahead and stand up for what you believe in. As I've learned, that's the path to happiness. Lesley Ann Warren
Happiness, Trust, Heart
Passion is what gives meaning to our lives. It's what allows us to achieve success beyond our wildest imagination. Try to find a career path that you have a passion for. Henry Samueli
Success, Passion
Your competition is not other people but the time you kill, the ill will you create, the knowledge you neglect to learn, the connections you fail to build, the health you sacrifice along the path, your inability to generate ideas, the people around you who don't support and love your efforts, and whatever god you curse for your bad luck. James Altucher
Love, Time, Knowledge

Without such a right, the values of liberty, justice and equality will have no meaning. Ignorance is by far the biggest danger and threat to humankind. Moza bint Nasser

These are great qualities that they must work towards. This is my message to the young people. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam
