Saturday, March 18 – Sunday, March 19, 2017

Setting A New Course:
Discover Your Life Purpose

Marcelo Cardoso

Ticket Information

A purpose-driven life not only puts you in the driver’s seat, it provides clarity and direction for where you are heading.

Fast becoming one of our signature experiences, the Purpose Workshop with Marcelo Cardoso gives you what you need to start orienting your life from a deeper, more meaningful sense of who you are.

In this 2-day immersion, you will engage in inner inquiry and purpose-focused exercises and reflections so that you can:

  • Find and frame the central theme of who you are
  • Reveal your latent talents and interests
  • Uncover the emotional structures that hold you in ruts and keep you from a more natural flow of life
  • Find resistances that limit the expression of your purpose—and learn how to overcome them
  • Discover how to live with more clarity and direction.

You will leave with fresh perspectives and insights that will open you to new thinking, expanded possibilities, and an empowered connection to yourself, your relationships, and your impact on the world.

Adults at all stages of life are welcome!

 

Watch Eileen Fisher: Marcelo Is a Gem

 
Ticket Information

Marcelo Cardoso is a gifted coach and facilitator. Marcelo works closely with Eileen Fisher and EILEEN FISHER Inc. to create a purpose-driven approach to the work they do. Passionate about how individual transformation drives the transformation of systems (organizational and societal), he has worked for 20 years in a leadership capacity within large companies focused on creating transformation for individuals and companies.
