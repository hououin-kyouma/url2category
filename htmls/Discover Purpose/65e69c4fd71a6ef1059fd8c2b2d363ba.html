Published on Aug 30, 2016

Today we’re talking about why I left my job as a manager in an advertising company to search for my life purpose and how I eventually found it in doing YouTube videos. It hasn’t been an easy transition and we get into the details in the video, but although the challenges I wouldn’t choose any different.
If you are searching for your life purpose, working in a job you don’t like or feeling unhappy, try starting from where you are and doing what you can right now, every small thing counts. Focus on the things you enjoy doing since you can remember yourself, from singing to dancing to researching and reading, these things are usually a good indication to what might be your personal purpose in life.
Another very powerful tool you can implement in your life no matter where you are is helping others. The simple act of connection gives a higher sense of purpose, as we are all in this together and we can truly help each other by sharing our own unique talents and passions.
Doing what you love means we will do more of it and if you don’t give up on yourself you will eventually succeed (and the thing about following your passion is that it doesn’t really feel like work even when it is ;)). Be it as a full time job or as a hobby, there are many paths to happiness but all of them start from a decision to build a life you love.
