Published on Aug 31, 2016

1) Success is knowing your purpose in life, growing to your maximum potential, and sowing seeds that help benefit others. So how do we find our purpose? We have to dig deep within ourselves and ask ourselves what it is that we are passionate about, and what we are gifted in. 2) What are you passionate about? Passion is the great Energizer! When you have passion you find energy and excellence! Passion and Purpose are usually linked, but not always. You can be passionate about something you're not good at. If what you want to do is an area that you are skillful at then you will be successful, it is critical that your wants align with what you are good at. Passion sets us above the crowd. When you pursue what you want to do and what you love, you will never work another day in your life. 3) What are my gifts? It's important to know what you are good at, and use that along with your passion to discover your purpose. Those two elements in harmony create a lot of energy and killer drive to succeed. Give your purpose to something bigger than you. When you are bigger than your purpose, you have a career. When your purpose is bigger than you, you have a calling. 4) When you are in an area of weakness you get tired, but when you are in an area of your strengths you will be energized! Stop focusing on your weaknesses like everyone always tells you to, because if you focus on your weaknesses you will stay weak. Give your energy to your strengths because that is what makes you unique and different! When you do this you'll find that you have a lot more energy and the line between work and play will dissolve! 5) True significance is when you start adding value to other people, that's when you will finally lose yourself and start becoming part of the bigger picture and you will be more successful. Being average has never caught anybody's attention and made anybody go the extra mile. But by focusing on your areas of strength and giving all your energy to your passion you will set yourself above the crowd and then you can take that energy and help others achieve their goals. When you start seeing other people grow that you have poured into that is the ultimate sign of success.

When you know what your passionate about and what areas you are gifted in you are on the right track to discovering your purpose!
