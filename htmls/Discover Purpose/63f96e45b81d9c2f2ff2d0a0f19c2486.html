Published on Aug 18, 2016

In this video I talk about what is the Ikigai and how to find it. It's a Japanese (Okinawa) concept meaning "a reason for being". Everyone, according to the Japanese, has an ikigai. Finding it requires a deep and often lengthy search of self. Such a search is regarded as being very important, since it is believed that discovery of one's ikigai brings satisfaction and meaning to life.
This is a concept they don't teach in school so I hope this video can be helpful for you :)
