Find your purpose

Are you ready for this?

You only find happiness and fulfillment when you are living your purpose. I can give you the clarity to understand what your purpose is, and give you the tools you need to make it your reality. 

I will guide you through each step by explaining every part of it, and make it very clear exactly what you should do.

Finding your lifes purpose is the answer to everything you ever wanted in life because, you will be totally fulfilled living your purpose.

Living your purpose is a happy life with a constant experience of joy and peace of mind. You will discover that doing you lifes purpose is effortless and that everything you need will be given you when you need it.

Signing up for this online workshop is your first step to clarity, and your willingness is a clear statement to the universe: I am ready!

How it works

There are 10 lessons. Each leasson is a video where I explain each step carefully with lots of examples. For each lesson there is a task that you must do. I will tell you exactly how you should do it, and all that is needed from you is the willingness to be open and do the tasks.

The whole workshop, including the work you have to do, can be done in one day. Or you could use three evenings. It is important that you do the whole workshop within a few days.

Non-religious

My teachings are spiritual - not religious. My understanding of God and Spirit is spiritual and the content of the programme is based on my own experience of moving into a guided life and finding my life's purpose.

My intentions?

My intention is to share and to connect. To share because everyone should be living their purpose. To connect because my purpose is to communicate what I learn from Spirit. Most of what I communicate is free, some is payed for. When you sign up for this programme you accept being on my maillinglist, and I will keep you informed of my teachings. 

 

Disclaimer: I do not believe in “quick fixes”, only in hard work. Being in my programmes you must have the willingness to do the work to transform your life, and you must trust the proces. Your results will be individual to you. Some may experience huge transformations, other may just experience clarity in being a specific problem. I can not, and will not, give you any guarantees about the result. If you do the work, you will experience some kind of tranformation, but how huge your “step” will be depends on your willingness and your own plan for transformation. If you feel that what I teach resonates with you, then you can be sure that you are guided to learn something from me and I invite you be enter into my teachings with an open mind and the willingness to be shown what your teaching is. You are always the one making the decisions, I can not, and will not make your decision. I can only point to the truth and you can/will use this in whatever way that you choose. You always make your own decisions, and before you make any step into tranformation I invite you to be clear that you always are the one deciding what you want and what you do not want. If you experience resistance towards what I teach, then I invite you to look at that resistance, and if it feels true, then I advice you to seek guidance elsewhere.
