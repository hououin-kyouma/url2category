Friends can help each other. A true friend is someone who lets you have total freedom to be yourself - and especially to feel. Or, not feel. Whatever you happen to be feeling at the moment is fine with them. That's what real love amounts to - letting a person be what he really is. Jim Morrison
Love, Freedom, Friends
Happy is the man who finds a true friend, and far happier is he who finds that true friend in his wife. Franz Schubert

Silence, Friend, True
A true friend is someone who is there for you when he'd rather be anywhere else. Len Wein
Friendship, Friend, You
A true friend freely, advises justly, assists readily, adventures boldly, takes all patiently, defends courageously, and continues a friend unchangeably. William Penn

A true friend never gets in your way unless you happen to be going down. Arnold H. Glasow

If you have one true friend you have more than your share. Thomas Fuller

I say find one true friend to help you get through the tough times. Kelly Osbourne

Friendship, Blessings, Care
A true friend is someone who is always there during the ups and downs, I actually have a song called 'True Friend'. Miley Cyrus
Ups And Downs, Song, Friend
A true friend encourages us, comforts us, supports us like a big easy chair, offering us a safe refuge from the world. H. Jackson Brown, Jr.
World, Easy, Friend
The two most misused words in the entire English vocabulary are love and friendship. A true friend would die for you, so when you start trying to count them on one hand, you don't need any fingers. Larry Flynt
Love, Friendship, Words
I have a family, loving aunts, and a good home. No, on the surface I seem to have everything except my one true friend. All I think about when I'm with friends is having a good time. I can't bring myself to talk about anything but ordinary everyday things. We don't seem to be able to get any closer, and that's the problem. Anne Frank
Family, Time, Home
A true friend is someone who thinks that you are a good egg even though he knows that you are slightly cracked. Bernard Meltzer
Good, Friend, You
I don't know if I've ever been in a clique. The older I've gotten, the more I've realized what a true friend really is. So my friendship circle has changed a bit. Aimee Teegarden
Friendship, Know, Friend
If fear is the great enemy of intimacy, love is its true friend. Henri Nouwen
Love, Fear, Great
What sweetness is left in life, if you take away friendship? Robbing life of friendship is like robbing the world of the sun. A true friend is more to be esteemed than kinsfolk. Marcus Tullius Cicero
Life, Friendship, Sun
If Christ Jesus dwells in a man as his friend and noble leader, that man can endure all things, for Christ helps and strengthens us and never abandons us. He is a true friend. Saint Teresa of Avila
Leader, Man, Friend
It's OK to argue with your friends. Guys can do it better than girls, usually, but if you ever get into a fight with a true friend or a spouse or a boyfriend, get it out, fight, be angry for five minutes, and then move past it. Don't let it fester; don't hold a grudge. If you do, that's when it will get worse and worse. Ike Barinholtz
Friends, Past, Fight
It is not often that someone comes along who is a true friend and a good writer. E. B. White
Good, Friend, True
You want a friend who's going to tell you the truth. That's what it's about. If you don't have a friend who's going to tell you the truth every time something comes up, you feel like he's not a true friend. Donald Driver

Every human being must find his own way to cope with severe loss, and the only job of a true friend is to facilitate whatever method he chooses. Caleb Carr
