  • Johan Svensson

    Hi Folks, 

    Sorry for the frustration and confusion!  We apologize that this is not easier and appreciate your feedback.

    This article does a good job of outlining some of the ways to find your friends on Strava. 

    Invite Athletes

    1) Click on the "Invite Athletes to Strava" button when you see it on your Dashboard, your list of followed athletes, or anywhere else.

    2) You can add emails of the friends you want to invite, or select Gmail to browse your Gmail contacts for easy selection.

    3) You can also add friends via Facebook: just click on the Facebook icon at the left side of the page. From here you can see all your Facebook friends that are already on Strava, or select those that aren't in order to invite them to join in on the fun.

    Athlete Search

    You can find friends through the Athlete Search. Just click on “Explore”, at the top of your screen. Then choose “Athlete Search” from the secondary row of tabs. Or simply visit  http://app.strava.com/athletes/search.

    Additionally, Strava will recomend people you can follow. Look on your Dashboard for the “Who to Follow” list on the right side of the page. Or, from your Profile page, click on “Who to Follow” at the top, and see who Strava thinks might be interesting for you to follow.

    Looking for your friends on Strava? Use the Athlete Search to find them.

    Today, we released a new search bar for Athletes on Strava that can be found at the top of any Strava web page.

    To use, click on the search icon placed after the Strava logo in the upper left corner of any page, and the Athlete search bar will appear (screen shot attached).

    Why this is helpful: Using this simple search bar, you can search for Strava athletes you're following and for friend's your not following. This is also a quick way to see if your friends are already on Strava or whether you need to invite them to join. 

    I hope this new tool makes it easier to search for friends on Strava.

    (To note, a feature similar to this already exists on the Strava apps for mobile, but can only be accessed on your Profile tab)
