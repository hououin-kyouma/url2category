A Community Bank Giving Back

10:21 AM, Jul 12, 2017
Share Article

First Bank Financial Centre (FBFC) is a true community bank that believes in giving back to the communities we serve. In fact, in 2016 FBFC employees spent over 15,000 hours volunteering with local non-profit organizations. In Ozaukee County,they have partnered with Mel’s Charities. In 2017 they are sponsoring four of their events, including Melapalooza, a softball tournament and music festival in Grafton. Joining us to discuss this meaningful partnership is Jeff McCarthy from First Bank Financial Centre and Tom Stanton from Mel's Charities.

To learn more about Mel's Charities and Melapalooza, visit MelsCharities.org.

And to learn more about all the cool events FBFC is sponsoring throughout the summer, visit their events calendar at FBFCWI.com.
