3 Steps: HOW TO MAKE FRIENDS in High School, College, Work, or Middle School, How to Be More Social

How to make friends may be a daunting task especially if you're an introvert or just a bit shy. There are many resources out there, such as this video on how to make friends as well as a great book and audiobook by Dale Carnegie called, Win Friends and Influence People (some search How to Make Friends and Influence People). Making friends and becoming popular doesn't change too much whether you're in high school, college (university), middle school, elementary school, or at work. Hell, you could even be online playing games like minecraft, at a convention, or at a first day of school at a new school or city like New York City (NYC). Today, we'll be going over how to make friends easily (easy) in 3 steps and with 2 bonus tips at the end.

Step #1 on How to Make Friends: The first step is to get into contact. This works for anyone, boys, girls, new country, kids, online on GTA, etc. If you want to get into contact with someone, say "What's up? Where are you from?" Or, if you're not from a different school ask for a pencil or something and then that way you spark up the conversation. After that, you can ask the guy or girl for their phone number if you need notes for instance for secondary or high school, and branch out from there. This doesn't matter if you're a child, a teenager, or a grown man or woman.

Step #2 on How to Make Friends: Join more activities and clubs. The way to build up a social circle even if you have social anxiety or were kind of awkward before is to branch out into what you enjoy doing and this way you and your friends will already by close fast because you already have something in common. Play games with them such as on Xbox, or play sports with them outside of class. If you're an adult and in a new city, check out http://meetup.com and try going to things that you enjoy doing. People there will like you as you like the same things.

Step #3 on How to Make Friends: Practice social skills online You can laugh at me, however if you want to win friends and influence people and keep them, then practicing is the best thing to do. You can play games on Twitch (Stream/Steam), then Skype them, make videos on YouTube on how to make friends (haha), test out platforms on FaceBook, PS4, SnapChat, etc. Get rid of your social anxiety and just do this as a habit each day to talk to more and more people, and see if you're doing well or not. Even if you're homeschooled you can do this, without the pressure of talking to so many people in real life especially if you are a bit awkward. If you're a freshman for instance, not too many people will talk to you unless you play sports or are a part of their social circle, so I suggest playing sports but also using your tools online. Even AlphaM made a video on how to make new friends, 9 new friend finding tips. You'll be much happier knowing these psychological tips and will make friends quickly.

So those are the 3 steps to making new friends no matter where you are in life, 4th grade, 5th grade, 6th grade, 7th grade, 8th grade, 9th grade, etc. Those were all the searches on YouTube for grades so I wanted to make sure making friends was universal around the globe and for all different ages. I also have 2 extra bonus tips on making friends.

Tip #1 on How to Make Friends: Go to all social events when invited. For me, I used to be the kid that was semi-popular but declined invitations to birthday parties in elementary school, bar mitzvahs in middle school, and house parties in high school. It was stupid, and I realize that now. When you have the time, and you're invited to hang out with friends or a different social group, just do it. The worst thing that can happen is you don't like them and you don't have to talk to them after that. Unless you have a bunch of work to do outside of your social life or have better friends to hang out with, just try it out and go with them.

Tip #2 on How to Make Friends: Look better. You can call me shallow but I want to associate myself with other people that are seen as attractive. That means there will be more girls that are attractive in my social circle as well are more guys that are successful. Increase your intelligence by learning more in and out of school, be more social, but also dress better, workout and eat more or less whether you are gaining muscle or losing fat, and the world will be a much better and more social place to live in. Look better and you will make more friends, get more girls flirting with you, and have more respect from other guys in school, work, or at college.

Making friends is almost a science, however it really isn't too hard, just take 1 step at a time, and over time, you will become a more happy and social person.
