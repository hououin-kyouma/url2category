FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

 

Is this course just for online businesses?
Not at all. Home School will help you find clarity and take the next step no matter what your idea is. Whether it's a shop, service or product, this will give you that clear path to getting your startup off the ground.

I don't have a clear idea to work with. Is that a problem?
No. We're confident weeks 1 & 2 will help to unlock your passion and creativity and narrow down any ideas you may have. Talking with fellow tribe members and hearing other people's stories will no doubt spark ideas. Two of our past students who'd never met before ended up co-founding an idea together! Read more about that here.

If I start Home School and find that I can’t commit to the program, can I defer to the next tribe?
If you're prepared to wait longer to make a start on your ideas then sure. Email hello@thehappystartupschool.com to defer your enrolment. You will get lifetime access to the course and materials and can work at your own pace if preferred.

Will I get access to the course content once the 4 week program finishes?
From the date the Home School tribe you have enrolled on launches, you’ll get lifetime access to all course content to revisit classes any time you like.

How do I connect with other tribe members also doing the course?
We have a private Slack community which is open 24-7 and encourage students to introduce themselves, share their work and ask for help.

Is this program something I can manage alongside a full time job?
We get asked this a lot. 90% of the 600+ students who have come through Home School manage successful careers alongside their side hustle. We’ve worked hard to ensure that the classes can be completed with the busiest of schedules. A level of commitment is required though, with the approach we take to building a business you’ll launch something faster than if you go it alone.

Will I have access to advice from Laurence & Carlos?
Carlos & Laurence will participate in the Slack channels a few times per week to answer any questions students may have. 

What if someone steals my idea?
The best part of the Home School is that everyone shares their progress, asks for help and gets feedback and love from fellow students. It’s like a small startup family. Our community is all about collaboration, not competition. It's time to embrace this new mindset – people are here to support, not harm, you.

What happens after Home School is finished?
Don't worry you won't be left on your own! You'll be invited to become a member of the Happy Startup School where you'll get access to practical resources and a community of collaborators to keep the momentum going.

What’s your refund policy?
We offer a 100% money back guarantee within 30 days of the course starting, no questions asked.

Have a question not answered here? No problem email us.

LESSON BREAKDOWN

WEEK 1

1.NEEDS
What are you looking for from this business? What needs are you serving?

2.PASSION
What lights your flame? What are you fighting against? What are you looking to change?

3.SUPERPOWER
What are you great at? What assets do you have at your disposal? How can you use these to your advantage?

WEEK 2

4.VALUES
What's important to me? What will you commit to never doing?

5.PURPOSE
Why do you do what you do other than for money? What is it that motivates you to get out of bed?

6.SUCCESS
What does success really mean to you? What will your world look like in 5 years time? What will you have achieved?

WEEK 3

7.STORY
What story do you want others to tell about your business? 

8.PEOPLE
Who do you want to serve and how do you get their attention? Who should you connect with?


9.PROBLEMS
What problems are you solving for them? How can you help them?

WEEK 4

10.MONEY
A clear indication that your idea fulfils a need in the world is people will pay.

11.DESIGN
Who do you want to serve? Learn the importance of design for getting your idea accross.

12.LAUNCH
Use what you've learned and worked through to create something that will inspire others to join you.
