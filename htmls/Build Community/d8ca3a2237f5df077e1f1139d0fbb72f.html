Community

JetBlue has a proud tradition of supporting dedicated community organizations in our BlueCities through partnerships, donations and crewmember volunteering.

Watch video about JetBlue crewmembers and community service

JetBlue crewmembers are dedicated to service.

JetBlue crewmembers are passionate about service – in 2015, JetBlue volunteers logged more than 150,000 hours!

Inspiring humanity through crewmember volunteerism

Community Connection is our volunteer program designed to align corporate giving with the individual passions of JetBlue crewmembers.

Playing a part in the community

Since 2006, JetBlue has partnered with KaBOOM! to create safe play spaces for children across the communities JetBlue serves.

 

From Blue to You – Empowering JetBlue volunteers

JetBlue crewmembers show their nonstop dedication to JetBlue's mission to inspire humanity by giving back to their local communities in meaningful ways.

JetBlue and The DREAM Project

As JetBlue continues to grow, we maintain our belief in giving back to the communities we serve. In pursuit of this mission, JetBlue has teamed up with The DREAM Project, a nonprofit organization operating in the Dominican Republic that runs education programs for children from impoverished communities.
