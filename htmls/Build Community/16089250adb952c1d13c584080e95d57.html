Giving Back to the Community

Newport News Shipbuilding is proud to be a part of the Hampton Roads community, where our shipbuilders give generously of their time and money to make the community a better place for everyone. NNS received the 2015 Darden Award from the Civic Leadership Institute in recognition of creating sustainable efforts toward the betterment of life for the people of the Hampton Roads region and demonstrating exceptional leadership in the pursuit of regional cooperation.
