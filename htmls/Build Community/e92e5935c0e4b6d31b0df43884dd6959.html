Become a Volunteer

More than 100 volunteers perform a wide range of services in nearly all areas of the facility benefiting patients, residents, employees and visitors. Our volunteers play a valuable role in providing excellent patient and resident care to our family and friends in the community. Our volunteers range in age from 14 to 90 years old.

Whether you are interested in volunteering to gain experience or to give back to your community, we would love to have you as part of our team. Learn more.

Greater Fairbanks Community Hospital Foundation

For the past 40 years, the Greater Fairbanks Community Hospital Foundation has invested in facilities, equipment and services thanks to the generous support from the Fairbanks community. Philanthrophy is vital to our ability to provide ongoing, high-quality health care. Some of the programs and services supported by the GFCHF include:

  • J. Michael Carroll Cancer Center
  • Circle of Hope Campaign
  • Denali Center
  • Porter Heart & Vascular Center
  • With All Your Heart Campaign
  • Hospice Services
  • Pregnancy Loss Bereavement Program
  • FMH Surgery Project

    Visit the GFCHF to learn more about how you can make a difference in our community.
