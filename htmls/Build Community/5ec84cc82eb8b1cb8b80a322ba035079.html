Find a solicitor

It’s easy to find a solicitor or Will writer to help you when making a Will. Their support can give you peace of mind for the future.

Why not start the process now. Type in your postcode below to find a local professional advisor in your area. They can also give you guidance on leaving gifts to charities.

Remember A Charity has a network of well-known national firms, partners and sole practitioners who can assist you in the process of writing or updating your Will. We work with both solicitors and professional Will-writers.

Find a solicitor or Will-writer who is a member of The Law Society, or meet our qualified Will-writing practitioner definition. Many are also accredited by The Law Society’s Wills and Inheritance Quality Scheme.

We work hard to ensure that organisation’s details are up-to-date. However, should you experience difficulties contacting an organisation, please let us know.
