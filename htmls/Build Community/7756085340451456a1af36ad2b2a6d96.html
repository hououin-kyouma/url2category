Find Your Tribe & Love Them Hard

By Lucy 31st July 2016 Blog
  • No Comments
  • 0
  • 0
    0

Finding Inspiration At Every Turn

Mumpreneur, mum boss, mummy blogger, mum gang, mums in business: We’re all in it together.

We’re living in an interesting time where more and more people are embracing their creative spirit, their entrepreneurial sassiness, their business know-how and passion for a more fulfilled life. In my own quest for a life where I follow my passion and play to my strengths, I have found so many inspiring women who have helped to guide me and usher me in the right direction.  But the real journey has been not only finding out what I want to be and where I want my life to take me but who I am taking along for the ride?

In this blog journey, I’m going to be taking a snapshot of mums who are down right awesome and i’ll be getting to know their motivations and inspirations through the medium of snail mail, that’s right, old fashioned pen to paper or should I say, the even older fashion of Pen Paling. I sent some hand written letters to some pals and they sent it to their pals and they sent it…. ok you get it! So who knows where the letters will go and who will get in touch but I hope through the journey of my letters and the ladies who respond, someone somewhere finds some wisdom in the text and it helps them to find their tribe.

Our From Mums With Love series will be kicking off with the awesome Vicki Hodges from Bo & Bel and from there the joy begins; who knows where this journey will take us!

Like the original girl gang said before us, “Free your mind of doubt and danger, be for real, don’t be a stranger”.

It has been a journey to find my tribe and since finding them, I have learnt to love them and respect them hard.

Love Lucy x
