Hey guys! Today we filmed a video all about friendship! How to make friends, how to keep long distance friendships and relationships, and so much more! We answered a ton of your questions regarding how to make new friends in high school, how to make friends in college, and how to be a good friend. Going back to school can be challenging when you're trying to make friends. We know how this goes! We talk about school, friends, going back to school, and how to handle a friend who is going down the wrong path in life as well.
