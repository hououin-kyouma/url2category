Giving Back

Giving Back ... Our Passion

Giving — it's not just part of our culture. It's our calling. With a Heart to Serve, WorldVentures Foundation™ creates positive change for children worldwide by teaming with volunteers, donors and service allies to support sustainable programs that enrich young lives. WorldVentures contributes resources and volunteer hours to help the foundation fulfill its global mission.

We are making a significant global impact in communities around the world:

By the end of 2016, WorldVentures Foundation opened 30 Dream Courts in the United States with Nancy Lieberman Charities, funded 100 bottle schools in Guatemala with Hug It Forward, and, with Waves for Water, implemented clean-water solutions for quake-ravaged Nepal. The foundation has been active in the following countries:  Africa, Australia, China, Cuba, Greece, Guatemala, Hong Kong, Hungary, Indonesia, Kenya, Malaysia, Mexico, Peru, South Africa, Thailand, United States and Zimbabwe. Donations to the foundation go directly to organizations that uphold causes, including: global footprint, education, community, culture, sustainability and disaster relief.

Since its inception in 2010, WorldVentures Foundation has touched the lives of children in 100 global communities, from Asia to the Americas and points in between, and their poised to give back at an even higher level in the future.
