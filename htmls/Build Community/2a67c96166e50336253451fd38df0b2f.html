Making friends in college!
I have been SO EXCITED to do this collab with Nicole! She has an amazing channel check it out HERE:

https://www.youtube.com/user/TheNicol...

And check out her collab video HERE:
http://youtu.be/LGVInKvSipI

This is such an exciting time for high schoolers who are going to college, or even people who are transferring schools or going to grad school! Making friends can be tough in a new city or a new town. Making friends in college is a lot easier than you think. You just need to be outgoing and know that you are an awesome person with an awesome personality and that anyone would be lucky to be your friend! Reach out to people in your classes and dorms and you will find yourself making friends faster than you thought possible! Friendship is an amazing thing and it will help you get through the tough times that your freshman year presents! Your freshman year will be your BEST year of college so live it up, enjoy, and have confidence! You're amazing and you'll be fine!
