Experience Tribe Perks

PARTIES

Celebrate the joy of life at one of our renowned Garden of Eden parties. You’ll connect with some amazing people, experience transformational talks, enjoy an abundance of high-impact performers, guided experiences, and much more.

BUSINESS NETWORK

The Business Network is filled with local businesses we trust and frequent in the Encinitas and San Diego areas. Show your Experience Tribe Membership Card to any of our trusted businesses and get up to 20% off of their offerings, products, and services.

TRIBE FAMILY DINNERS

Hungry for human connection, great live music, serendipitous collisions, and big belly laughs? Meet up with the Tribe at a local restaurant for our monthly family dinner mingler. Open to all curious Experiencers, card holding Members get half off entrance (includes a full meal and entertainment).

TRIBE DIRECTORY

The Tribe Directory will be your go-to destination for finding professional service providers within the Encinitas/San Diego community which align with your values and offers amazing work.

WORKSHOPS

Attend our thought-provoking and soul-charged transformational workshops lead by remarkable people who are deeply devoted to raising the collective consciousness.

PARTNER EVENTS

We have a powerful tribe that is up to exciting things.  Enjoy free or discounted entry at our partner events.  From Community Speaker Series to sound healing’s and meditations, come check out what the tribe has to offer.  

JOIN THE TRIBE

Where Purpose & Passion Come Together

Experience Tribe was founded in Encinitas, Calif. with a rebellious spirit and an open heart.  It’s built on a foundation of entrepreneurs, change-agents, creators, artists, and local businesses who care.  Together we co-create a vibrant tribe which thrives at the intersection of health, happiness, and prosperity.  We’re creating a new model for community across the globe.

“I’m inspired each time I’m with this powerful community to level up my game… to open my heart more… to surrender deeper… and to LOVE with abandon…”

– Krista Richards

Experience Tribe is so much more than the amazing social events and parties you’ve been hearing about. We’re a thriving community. We are an active, creative, and sustainable tribe of influential leaders and heart-centered humans who are actively making the world a better place to live, work, play, and stay through the decisions and choices we make every day.

If you feel called to serve humanity by offering the gifts that are uniquely yours to give; to use them to positively impact lives, relationships, and the local economy, then we’re inviting YOU to Experience Tribe.
