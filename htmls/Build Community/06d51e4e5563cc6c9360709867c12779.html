  • Pinterest

Are you familiar with the Zig Ziglar quote, “You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with”?

It’s one of my favorite quotes because it points at one of the most critical aspects of success — your tribe.

Your tribe is made up of the people who understand and support you, and can inspire you to keep going is crucial to creating your own freedom lifestyle successfully.

I understand that it can be especially difficult if you don’t feel supported by your friends and family. And even if you have that kind of support, it is important to find people who have done what you want to do and seek guidance from them.

I want you to know that I am 100% in your corner and want you to succeed. In today’s video, I will show you how you can find more mentors and supporters who can inspire you to take action and keep going after your dreams. These tips will be especially helpful if you are feeling alone on your journey right now.

Day 6 Challenge: Find 1 or 2 people who are living the kind of life you want to live, and whose tribe you’d love to be part of. Then write a short post about why you find them inspiring and the one question you would ask them if you met in real life.
