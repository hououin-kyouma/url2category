Meet BounceTribe

Share music you're working on and meet like-minded musicians

What are listening sessions?

You can create or join listening sessions to exchange feedback with other musicians. We will help you find similar musicians based on your musical influences and the genre of your music. 

How can I expose my music to more people?

The higher your feedback rating, the more visible your music will be to users looking to exchange feedback. You can increase your rating by offering quality feedback that other musicians find helpful. Users can also Bounce your songs to share them with their tribe.

Listen To Be Heard

The more you listen, the more your music will be heard.

1. Find Sessions

Join sessions and meet musicians who will understand your music.

2. Listen & Tap

Learn what people like about your music and receive creative ideas.

3. Build Your Tribe

Share your ideas and create quality connections.

1. Find Sessions

Upload a track you’re working on and join a session with someone working on a similar project.

Genre & Influences

The genre for your project and your musical influences will help us match you with the right musicians.

Location

Join sessions with musicians nearby or share your work with artists around the globe.

Experience Level

Enter how many years you've been making music so other users can evaluate your work based on your level of experience.

2. Listen & Tap

Once you have joined a session, tap along to place markers as you listen to their song. Indicate the parts you like and the sections where you have an idea or suggestion.

Idea

Like

Feedback Rating

Listen and give quality feedback to increase your rating. The higher your rating, the more visible your music will become.

Appreciate Feedback

Appreciate helpful feedback you've received to reward your match and increase their rating.

Messaging

Once you have both given your feedback, you may continue messaging each other.

3. Build Your Tribe

Add people to your tribe of trusted musicians who you enjoy receiving feedback from and want to share more of your music with.

Bounce It

Collect songs that you love and share them with your tribe.

SubTribes

Create SubTribes to control who can hear your music.
