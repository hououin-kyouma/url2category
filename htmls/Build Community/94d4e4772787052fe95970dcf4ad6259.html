Published on Jul 3, 2017

Brown Bag Friday is a free concert on the Grundy County Courthouse lawn on select Fridays through the summer months provided by Grundy Bank and WCSJ/WJDK. Entertainers vary for each concert. At most Brown Bag Fridays the Grundy Bank Caring Committee has a concession stand with hot dog lunches to benefit local charities.
Each year Grundy Bank sponsors a Hot Dog Eating Contest as part of our Brown Bag Fridays. The contestants are a variety of local government officials, community leaders, not for profit volunteers and fellow business men and women. Each contestant selects an organization to benefit from the event. Whether or not they win the competition, each organization will walk away with a donation.
Morris Community High School and Saratoga Grade School Bands have performed at our Brown Bag concert series with concession proceeds benefiting both bands.
Our annual Brown Bag Friday Ice Cream Social is always a hit with our ice cream stand during the Liberty Arts Festival. Scoops are sold for 25 cents each with unlimited toppings. All proceeds benefit the Grundy Bank Caring Committee.
