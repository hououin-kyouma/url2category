Charity trustees: finding the right person for the role

We've put together an infographic for Trustees Week 2016 which looks at how to find the right charity trustee for your organisation.  The infographic covers initial questions for trustee boards to ask themselves and includes suggested interview questions for an effective selection process. It also looks at other things to consider, and gives an overview of charity trustees in 2016.

Our infographic will help you find the right person for the role - download the charity trustee infographic here.
