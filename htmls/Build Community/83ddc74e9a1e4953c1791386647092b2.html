Heart of the City launches its Find A Charity Map

  1. Home
  2. News
  3. Heart of the City launches its Find A Charity Map
Jul 28

Ever been left wondering where to start your community or environmental programme? Or perhaps you have not been able to find the right community partner to work with. Heart of the City’s ‘Find a Charity’ tool will present Heart of the City’s (HotC) network with over 700 community groups they can partner and create lasting partnerships with.

Hotc members can now access a map of London packed with information on local community needs and charities they can partner with. We acknowledge that most of our members are fulfilling their Responsible Business role on top of their busy day job. Our map will make it easier for members to quickly identify local needs and charities they can partner with.

Features:

  • Find out the needs of your local community
  • Search for charities looking for support near you
  • Filter charities by causes
  • Access charity contact information

Watch a video of how our Find A Charity map works

Use our Find A Charity map now

Heat map
When starting a community programme, there are any number of issues a company can get involved with. Having a better idea of the local community’s needs is always a great place to start – our ‘heat map’ feature allows members to do just that. Using the Indices of Deprivation 2015 data, the ‘Find-a-Charity’ tool will allow HotC members to view the significance of various social issues as well as the area’s ranking against the rest of the UK. A graphical aid which paints a very concise picture of the social needs of London, the heat map is a great way to tailor your programmes to address the problems closest to your business.

Search for charities
There are around 400,000 charities and community groups in the UK and sometimes finding the right charity and addressing the issues you want to be involved with can be a difficult task. Simply searching Google is not always the best way to connect with the right charity. There are smaller charities out there which, while might not be household names, are highly driven towards achieving social impact. We have added over 700 charities to our map, complete with location, contact details and area of support, making it easier for Hotc members to establish charity partnerships.
