Hello Fitlifers!

Welcome to another Mindset Monday. Always remember, we're in this together!

Do you have a tough time making new friends? Or you have the tendency to stay at home sitting in front of your computer not really connecting with people? On this Mindset Monday, I want to share 3 tools with you that can help transform your social life completely.

Three Amazing Tools for Friendship

Tool 1 - Get Grateful

Tool 2 - Spread Your Good Feelings

Tool 3 - Invite people to experience something fun together!

So let's walk through these three tools in order to gain more friends and have these positive vibes!

Tool 1 - Get Grateful

Making new friends actually start with working on myself. In the mornings, I meditate and think about all the things that are going right in my life. I become emotionally grateful with the environment that I have, friends that I'm surrounded with, the mission that I'm on, the health and vitality that's in my life.

I also keep a journal of all the small things that I am grateful for - even if it's something small. Perhaps someone opened a door for me; I get to pet a cute dog, or play wiffle ball with my co-workers. You'll begin to see that all these little things ADD UP QUICKLY! We have so much to be grateful for. If you agree, please give this video a big thumbs up!

Tool 2 - Spread Your Good Feelings

Now that you're in a state of gratefulness, it's time to share that good feeling with someone. The happiest people are those who are able to express their positivity to others because they are a center for good feelings. Everywhere I go and whenever I buy something, I make a point of being as nice and genuine to the other person as possible. Overtime, I have people recognize me and feel very happy to see me again.

Tool 3 - Invite people to experience something fun together!

Learn people's names :) Ask people what do they love to do for fun! Maybe you have something in common! Here at FitLife.TV, we have something called Friday Fun Day. We like kayaking, paddle boat, or do anything random to mix things up. We like to invite people and share in that experience.

If you like this content as much as we love making it, please give this a big thumbs up! Put one of the tools to use and let me know. Leave a comment below! Better yet, leave a video response :) That way, you're entitling yourself a chance to win a book.

Always remember, we're in this together!

We'll see you next week!
