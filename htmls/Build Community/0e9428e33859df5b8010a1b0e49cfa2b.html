Residents Enjoy Tasty Buffet While Giving Back to the Community

By Brittani Moncrease  |  April 16, 2017 @9:41 PM
SHARE

ENDICOTT, N.Y. -- For many in Broome County, Easter was a day to give back.

Dozens packed the Endicott Elks Lodge for an Easter breakfast buffet Sunday. 

"It's just like a fun time to hang out and get some breakfast. Fill your stomachs up. It's a fun way to spend time with other people and your family," said Alexander Geggert, a Port Dickinson resident.

Proceeds from the breakfast go towards helping charities, veterans and other Lodge service projects.
