    • Our connection to the Cayman Islands community is a key part of our brand. Therefore giving back is at the core of everything we do, and we are dedicated to making a profound and positive impact in the lives of the community we live in. At TEAM Property Cayman at RE/MAX, we continually partner with charities on key initiatives that make a difference in Cayman, while also encouraging the local community to give back and support those less fortunate.

     

    In 2016, with the help of our professional business network and invited video guests, our the5wins video series raised over CI$5,500 for local charities. Our newest tradition entitled, Christmas in July will provide over CI$6,000 in charitable donations, making a significant impact on six local organisations – The Light House School, Big Brothers Big Sisters, Cayman Islands Humane Society, local Government School teachers, The Pines Retirement Home and Meals on Wheels.

     

    The Pines Retirement Home, August 12 2016

     

    “Giving is not just about making a donation; it’s about making a difference!”

     

    As part of RE/MAX Cayman Islands, TEAM Property Cayman also participates, volunteers and contributes to the numerous charitable events and organisations throughout the year. We are dedicated to making a positive impact in our local community to improve and enhance our home country which we love so much.

     

    We encourage you to drop us a line if you would like any more information on these or other local charities, so together we can continue to make a difference.
