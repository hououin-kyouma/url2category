GIVE BACK

At North Point, giving back to our community isn’t just something we do, it’s a vital part of who we are.

We want to see our local communities restored with hope, help, and resources that allow them to dream and achieve more than current statistics would say is possible.

PAST EVENTS:

Summer Serve Sampler at the Dream Center

Summer is upon us! To celebrate we want to give you a sample of all the ways you can serve at the Dream Center this summer. We have highlighted 1 area per week. But you know what, if you see an opportunity you like but the week doesn’t work for your schedule…just email us at jody@springfielddreamcenter.com and we will hook you up!

As a part of Give Back, we take trips, both stateside and internationally, to reach our neighbors who are “far away” but need to experience God’s love and compassion just like us here at home.

So, we’re challenging our church to GIVE and GO!
