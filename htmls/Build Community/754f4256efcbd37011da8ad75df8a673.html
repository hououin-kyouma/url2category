Every year during Christmas season, I visit my local Toys-R-Us to make donations to Toys4Tots. It's always so inspiring and fun to shop for others, imagining a smile on a child fade. This donation would not have been possible without you guys, my viewers. For that, I thank you from the bottom of my heart. Thank you so much for being a part of this donation as a community.
