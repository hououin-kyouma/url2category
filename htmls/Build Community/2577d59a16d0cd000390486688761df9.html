    Giving Back to the Community

    We have partnered with Wheels For Wheels and we are donating our labor on a few Pontiac Solstice custom builds that will be sold when done with all the upgrades with the proceeds going to Children’s Hospital of Phoenix. Currently available is an ’09 GXP Coupe. For specs and pricing click here.

    Currently under construction is a ’07 convertible with an LS engine, 6 speed auto, with parts from Solo Performance, BC Racing, Wilwood brakes and Norms fiberglass so far. More to come as additional sponsors come on board as well as a few different shops donating upholstery, audio, paint and body, etc. Stay tuned for the upcoming link on the availability of this vehicle.
