Published on Jul 31, 2012

Are you worried about not knowing anyone when you go to college?

Meeting new friends is part of the college process. For some people, walking into a room where you don't know anyone can be intimidating. However, there are a few things that you can do to meet people before you arrive on campus in August. Instead of showing up and being by yourself, you can make it feel like you are meeting up with friends with a few proactive steps.

The question that we are answering today is how to meet friends before August so that you don't feel alone.

Here are three tips to avoid being lonely on the first day of college:
1. Contact your roommate
2. Ask if your department has a Facebook page
3. Get involved with Back-to-School activities
