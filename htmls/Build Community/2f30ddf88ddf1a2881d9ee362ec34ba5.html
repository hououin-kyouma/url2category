Published on Jul 30, 2016

Introverts Unite...separately! College is a fun and exciting time. For most of you, you’re finally breaking free from the rules of your parents (most of them anyway) and doing things on your own. It’s also a scary time. You’re moving into a new environment where you know no one. How will I make new friends in college? How did I even make my old ones?

Though those are common worries to everyone on the personality spectrum. This video will focus on the worries of introverts when heading off to college for the first time.
